## Description

As a Legal team member, when I am on a Letter of Intent Contract, I want the ability to Cancel, so that I can indicate that a contract status is Abandoned.

## Acceptance Criteria

AC: Ability to ‘Cancel Contract'

Given I am a Legal Team Member within the Corporate Support Apps

When navigate to a Contract (RT: Letter of Intent) record

Then I have the ability to select the ‘Cancel Contract’ guided experience

And available navigation options:

- Cancel (cancel is via the “X” in upper right)

** No updates are made

** Guided Experience is closed

** User is returned to the Letter of Intent Contract

- Finish/Save

** Guided Experience is closed

** User is returned to the Letter of Intent Contract

** And the system shall:

*** Set the Status = 'Abandoned’

## Solution

Flow Action Contract.RT: Letter of Intent

Update: Contract.Status = 'Abandoned’
