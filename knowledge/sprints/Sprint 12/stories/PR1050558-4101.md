## Description

**Steps to reproduce:**

1. Login to Core HC org as Legal Team Member - Atticus Finch

1. Open a  Client Agreement Request case record in Not Started or In Progress status

1. Click ‘Configure Contract’ button

1. Enter only required fields (keep contract end date as blank as it is optional)

1. Click Next button, select the required service accounts and click Finish button

**Expected Result:**

Client Contract should be created successfully with selected service accounts

**Actual Result:**

Below error is displayed after clicking Finish button

**There is an error while processing the request, please try again or contact your administrator.**

## Root Cause

Out of the box functionality when the contract is created without the end date, the related PNC will not have an end date ( fetched from contract ), but as end date is mandatory for PNC records it was throwing the error, updated the solution to handle this scenario
