## Description

As a recruiter, I the ability to generate a term sheet so that I can expedite the contracting process prior to engaging the Clinician Contracting Specialist team.

## Acceptance Criteria

Split from [+PR10505582811+|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505582811]+.+ Updates in **bold** based on new picklist field.  Also we implemented a List View in story 1809 that needs to be updated based on the new picklist. 

Given I am a Recruiter within the Corporate Support apps

When I am on Contract record page,

And I click “Send Term Sheet for Signature”

When No Term Sheet present:

Show message to user “Please generate term sheet before sending for signature”.

When Term sheet is already generated (based on file attachments)

Then the document is sent via DocuSign to the Clinician’s email address on the related Person Account record 

- Recipient = Clinician’s Email

- CC =  Recruiter (user’s email)

Given I am a Clinician Contracting Specialist within the Corporate Support apps

When the document is signed by the Clinician, 

And the Integration (via DocuSign) has updated the Contract field **“Term Sheet Status” = Signed/Executed**  “Term Sheet Executed” = checked 

Then from the Contract tab List View (existing, needs to be updated) = **Clinician  Approved & Term Sheet Exectd** Clinician Term Sheet Executed-

** Filter = Contract Record Type = Clinician, AND (Status = Approved OR Term **“Term Sheet Status” = Signed/Executed* Sheet Executed = true/checked) 

** Fields to Display: Contract Number, Account Name, Employment Type, Status, **Term Sheet Status* Term Sheet Executed, Contract Start Date, Contract End Date, Contract Approval Date, Previous Contract, Contract Owner Alias

And I navigate to a clinician Contract record with a term sheet

And I navigate to Files section (Files Tab on Contract),

Then I can view the Signed Term sheet document as file attached there.

NOTE: for confirmed during refinement that we want to pull the stars for the term sheet location & compensation (rate + unit of measure)
