## Root Cause

The defect resulting in missing data (e.g., Job Title, HPF, Compensation) on generated term sheets is due to an **incomplete scope in the initial requirements and subsequent implementation** concerning data retrieval rules.

** **Scope Limitation:** The current logic for term sheet generation was implemented only to pull **active HPF data*.

** **Missing Requirement:** The initial design failed to account for or mandate the retrieval of **inactive HPF data*, which is necessary for comprehensive term sheet generation. This gap in the requirement led to the missing fields when the source HPF records were not in an active status.

The longterm fix is dependent on completing the alreadyplanned story **PR1050558-3201**, which will implement the logic to retrieve all HPF records regardless of status. A temporary **workaround** involves manually setting the HPF to an active status to unblock generation, pending approval for the permanent fix.
