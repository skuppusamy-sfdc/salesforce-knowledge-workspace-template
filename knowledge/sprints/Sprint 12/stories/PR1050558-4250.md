## Description

### **Description:**

When initiating onboarding for **SCP** from the Clinician Account, an extra **Facility** section is appearing in the portal.

As per the requirement, **only the SCP section** should be visible under the Onboarding section.

### **Steps to Reproduce:**

1. Log in to Salesforce as an internal user.

1. Navigate to the **Clinician Account** record.

1. From the **SCP** form, initiate the onboarding process.

1. Log in to the **Clinician Portal** using the same user credentials.

1. Navigate to the **Onboarding** section.

### **Actual Result:**

The portal displays an extra **Facility section** along with the SCP section (refer to screenshot below).

### **Expected Result:**

Only the **SCP section** should be displayed in the onboarding screen; no extra Facility section should appear.
