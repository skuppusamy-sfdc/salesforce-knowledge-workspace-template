## Description

As a HR team member, I need accurate Hire Dates in Workday for clinicians, so that I can ensure clinicians are paid and receive benefits according to their employment type

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a HR team member in the Corporate Support apps

When I navigate to a clinician record where they have changed **from Locums to Independent Contractor or Employee**

Then their hire date is First Clinical Shift for IC and First Clinical Shift for EE

Given I am a HR team member in the Corporate Support apps

When I navigate to a clinician record where they have changed **from Employee to Independent Contractor or Leased**

Then their hire date is First Clinical Shift for IC and blank for Leased

Given I am a HR team member in the Corporate Support apps

When I navigate to a clinician record where they have changed **from Independent Contractor to Employee or Leased**

Then their hire date is First Clinical Shift for EE and blank for Leased

Given I am a HR team member in the Corporate Support apps

When I navigate to a clinician record where they have changed **from Leased to Independent Contractor or Employee**

Then their hire date is First Clinical Shift for IC and First Clinical Shift for EE

_note: dates are only updated the first time that First Clinical Shift is received_ 

_note: EE clinicians where Start Administrative Work ahead of Clinical Work is checked, will have a hire date of Company Signed Date (AKA when SCP signed the agreement)_

-When a clinician contract is populated with Start Admin Work Ahead of Clinical-

-Then the Hire Date field on Contract record is populated with the Company Signed Date-

-When First Clinical Shift is populated on HPF-

-Populate Hire Date on Contract object-

When First Clinical Shift is populated on HPF

AND Start Admin Work Ahead of Clinical on Contract is FALSE

Populate Hire Date on Contract object with the same value as First Clinical Shift

## Solution

Flow Object : Contract

Condition : When contract is created

Scenario 1: Contract has been created first time for Practitioner

Check if the Contract does not have Hire date populated .

On the updated contract ( when status = executed), get the HPF via PNC.

Amongst the clinician contract’s HPFs (using PNC from the contract), find the earliest First Clinical Shift date of HPF where Contract.employee type is (Locum or Employee or IC or Leased) and stamp the First clinical Shift date on Contract’s hire date

Scenario 2: Practitioner is changing titles/transitioning. So check if another clinician contract is created for same Practitioner and SA (via PNC record) and there is no gap in date ( new contract’s start date is one day after current contract’s end date) 

Flow on Clinician Contract

On the updated contract ( when status = executed), get the HPF from PNC.

Check if clinician has existing Executed Contract and get the HPF from PNC.

Check if the existing contract has Hire date value populated and if the existing Contract’s employee type is changed to one of the given values in the table in the new Contract’s emp type.

if so, copy the Hire date value from existing Contract to New Contract.

Scenario 3: If  Contract.Employment_Type__c = ‘Employee’ clinicians and Contract.Start_Admin_Work_Ahead_of_Clinical_Work__c is checked ,  then Contract.Hire_Date__c = Company Signed date

|Old Contract Employment_Type__c|New Contract Employment_Type__c|Contract.Hire_Date__c|

|Locum|IC|First Clinical Shift|

|Locum|Employee|First Clinical Shift|

|Employee|IC|First Clinical Shift|

|Employee|Leased|Blank|

|IC|Employee|First Clinical Shift|

|IC|Leased|Blank|

|Leased|IC|First Clinical Shift|

|Leased|Employee|First Clinical Shift|

_If Start Administrative Work ahead of Clinical Work is checked for EE clinicians_,  _then Contract._Hire_Date__c = Company Signed date
