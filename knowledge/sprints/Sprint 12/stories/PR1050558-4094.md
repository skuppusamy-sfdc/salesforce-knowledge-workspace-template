## Description

As a Sales VP, when a Pro Forma Model Approval is rejected, I want a rejection email to be sent to the Submitter & VP Sales Ops Distribution List (DL), so that all VP Sales Operation users will be able to view the email notification. 

Note in Production: there are currently 2 VP Sales Ops and their Director who will be part of the Production VP Sales Ops (DL)

From:  Submitter & one user who is set up as Role = VP Sales Operations
