## Description

**Detailed Description:** ONB-004 Process Manual onboarding Facility only

As per Expected results 

1. For the Hospital Orientation child case, the related Task auto-closes once User fills in the "First Clinical Shift" on the HPF 

but the task is not auto closed.

**Persona:** Medical Staff Liaison

**Tester:** Geetha Bhavani Vanam

**Steps to Reproduce:**

Login as CET

1. From Person Account in ONB-003, click on Cases

1. In the Open Onboarding related list, drill into the "Facility Onboarding - <Facility Name> Case

1. Click on the Related Tab and view the Child Cases, which can include:

-MSS Onboarding

-Hospital Application Preparation

-Hospital Privileging

-Payer Enrollment

-Hospital Orientation

1. Drill into the "Hospital Orientation" child case

1. Drill into the related Task for "Schedule Hospital Orientation"

1. Navigate to the related HPF (Person Account > Facilities > HPF) and Fill in "First Clinical Shift" and Save"

**Expected Result** : For the Hospital Orientation child case, the related Task auto-closes once User fills in the "First Clinical Shift" on the HPF 

**Actual Result** : Schedule Hospital Orientation task is not auto closed once First clinical shift is updated"

## Root Cause

The automation responsible for closing the **"Schedule Hospital Orientation" task** is correctly configured to trigger when the **"First Scheduled Date"** field on the HPF is populated.
