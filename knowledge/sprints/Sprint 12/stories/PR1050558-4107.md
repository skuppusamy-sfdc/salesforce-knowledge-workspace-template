## Description

As a Legal Team member, when I am on a newly created or amended Letter of Intent Contract, I want the ability view the active Opportunity Services, so that I can easily view the Service Accounts that pertain to this Letter of Intent Contract.

## Acceptance Criteria

This story updates the solution design to allow users to view the Opportunity Services on both **new** and **amended** LOI contracts. 

AC1: View related Services on a Newly created & Amended LOI contracts 

Given I am a Legal Team Member within the Corporate Support Apps

When navigate to a newly created or amended Contract (RT: Letter of Intent) record 

- Newly Created Contract - Sales VP ‘Initiate Contracting Request’ for Letter of Intent & a new LOI contract’s 'Previous Contract' field is blank

- Amended Contract - Legal team member selected Action = ‘Amend Contract’ & new LOI contract’s 'Previous Contract' field is populated

Then I can see a ‘Opportunity Services’ related list; user can view the Active Opportunity Services (Services related to the Opportunity in Status = Active)

- Filtered by: Services Status = Active

- Fields to display: Service Account (name) & Type (Service Line)

- NO Actions available

AC2: Clean up tabs based on design 

Given I am a Legal Team Member within the Corporate Support Apps

When navigate to a Contract (RT: Letter of Intent) record

Then I should only see the following tabs:

- Details (default)

- Opportunity Services (NEW per AC1)

- Related

- Provider Network Contracts (REMOVE, no PNC on LOI contracts)

AC3: Delete ‘Letter of Intent’ field from Services

Given I am a System Administrator within the Setup

When I navigate to Object = Services

Then the following field shall be deleted:

- Letter of Intent

## Solution

AC1: Use the Contract.RT=”Letter of Intent” Record Page design to show the “Opportunity Services” using Contract.SourceOpportunity 

AC2: Adjust the Contract.RT=”Letter of Intent” Record Page TAB based on AC

AC3: 

1. Delete the Letter of Intent field from Service Object 

1. Adjust the Omni Script that created the Letter of Intent ([https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505581676|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505581676|smart-link] ) to remove the reference for Letter of Intent
