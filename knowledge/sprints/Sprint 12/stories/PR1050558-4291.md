**Steps to Reproduce:**

3. Select 1 or more (but not all) related Facilities, and click "Next"

5. Populate the "Date Termination Received" and "Last Day"

6. Check the box for "Voluntary Termination"

7.Choose "Reason for Termination"

8. Check the box for "Backfill Recommended"

9. Save and click "Next"
