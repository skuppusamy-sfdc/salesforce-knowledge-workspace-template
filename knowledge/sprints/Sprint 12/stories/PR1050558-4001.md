## Description

As a Recruiter, I need bonuses to be routed for approval appropriately, so that necessary steps are completed to contract clinicians

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a Recruiter in the Corporate Support apps

When I submit a clinician contract for approval with the following bonus configurations:

- No Bonus

** No bonus approval necessary

- Bonus Specified - Over Job Threshold

** Contract is routed for approval by the VPO associated with the HPF where the bonus was applied

- Bonus Specified - At or Below Job Threshold

** Contract is routed for approval by the VPO associated with the HPF where the bonus was applied

** **Bonus Specified - HPF does not have a Job*

*** **Contract is routed for approval by the VPO associated with the HPF where the bonus was applied*

## Solution

In the current approval process, add a condition to check if HPF.Job is null, if so, assign the lVPO as the approver and send for approval
