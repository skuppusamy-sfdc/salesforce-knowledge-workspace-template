## Description

As a Legal team member, I need a view of contract requests that need to be written, so that I can see a clear view of what work I need to complete

## Acceptance Criteria

AC1: View **Letter of Intent** Contract

Given I am a Legal Team Member within the Corporate Support Apps

When I select a Letter of Intent contract record (Contract tab list view = “Open Client Contract Requests” [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505581736|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505581736|smartlink] or from the Opportunity’s Contract related list [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505582166|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505582166|smartlink] )

Then on the Details tab, I shall have the ability to **view** the information the captured via the guided experience and additional fields needed by the Legal Team. 

(Guided Experience stories: [+1676  Client Contracting  “LOI Request”+|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505581676] +&+ [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505583463|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505583463|smartlink] )

And **view** the following fields in the Contract Overview section:

- Contract Owner

- Contract Number 

** Contract Name (**newly added* for action = Amend Request)

** Description (**newly added* for action = Amend Request)

- Status (NOTE: will be visible in the Highlight panel (top of the record)

- Account Name

- Contract Start Date

- Contract End Date

- Source Opportunity

And **view** the following existing fields in the Letter of Intent Request Info (left column) need to be updated:

- Name of Client Representative

** Help Text: Name of Client Representative LOI will be addressed to

- Client Address 

- Name of Client Entity

** Name of each Hospital - **Remove/Delete*

- Location of each Hospital (City & State)

- Other Key Terms & Assumptions

And **view** the following new fields to the Letter of Intent Request Info section (right column):

- Agreement Start Date 

- Perpetual

AC2: Edit **Letter of Intent** Contract

Given I am a Legal Team Member within the Corporate Support Apps

When I select a Letter of Intent contract record 

And Status = Draft

Then on the Details tab, I have the ability to edit fields within the Contract Overview section; EXCEPT for:

- Status (NOTE: visible in the Highlights Panel only)

- Source Opportunity

And in the **Letter of Intent Request Info section**, the user shall have the ability to **edit fields only in Status = Draft**:

Left column:

- Name of Client Representative  [text, 255]

- Client Address [text area, 255]

- Name of Client Entity [text, 255]

- Location of each Hospital (City & State) [text area (long), 131,072]

- Service Line [text, 255]

- Anticipated Start Date for Services [date]

- Proposed Term of Agreement [text, 255]

** Financial Terms [text, 255 **text area (long), 131072*]

** Other Key Terms & Assumptions [text, 255 **text area (long), 131072*]

And in the **Letter of Intent Request Info section**, the user shall have the ability to **edit until Status = Executed** (Edit in status: Draft & Submitted.  Read only for all other statuses)

Right column: 

- Agreement Start Date [date]

- Perpetual [Checkbox]

- Contract Term (Months) [number 3,0]

- Agreement End Date [date]

- Amendment Effective Date [date]

And in **Key Dates section,** the user shall have the ability to **edit fields at ANY Status**:

Left column:

- Requested Date [date]

- Date Sent For Internal Review [date]

- Other Party Sent Date [date]

- Other Party Returned Date [date]

- Outstanding Days [READ ONLY, formula]

Right column:

- Executed Copy Mailed Out Date [date]

** Activated Date [date/time] - **READ ONLY*

** Activated By [lookup to user]  - **READ ONLY*

- Date Distributed [date]

- Total Process Days [READ ONLY, formula]
