## Description

### **Description:**

When viewing a **Clinician Contract** record in the **Summary tab**, the **Delete icon (trash can)** for the HPF record card appears **outside the border** of the HPF tile.

This creates a **UI misalignment** and gives the impression that the button is detached from the component.

This issue is observed consistently for HPFs displayed in the **Summary tab** (e.g., VN Hospital HPF).

### **Expected Result:**

The **Delete icon** should be **aligned within the HPF card border**, maintaining consistent spacing and visual alignment with other card elements.

### **Actual Result:**

The **Delete icon** overlaps the card border and is displayed **outside the visual container**, breaking layout consistency.
