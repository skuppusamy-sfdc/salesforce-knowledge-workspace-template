## Description

As a Corporate Support user with access to Account (RT: Service Account) or Account (RT: Facility/Hospital), I want the see Contract Record Type in the Contract Related Lists, so that I can easily identify the type of contract. Also for users with access to Service Accounts and Provider Network Contracts (PNC), then they should see the PNC related list so that the user can see related client contract information.

## Acceptance Criteria

AC1: Account (RT: **Facility/Hospital**) - Update **Contract** Related List 

Given I am a Corporate Support user with access to Account (RT: Facility/Hospital)

When I navigate to a Account (RT: Facility/Hospital)

And I am on the Related tab

Then on the “Contracts” related list I can see “Contract Record Type” field after the “Contract Number”

AC2: Account (RT: **Service Account**) - Update **Contract** Related List 

Given I am a Corporate Support user with access to Account (RT: Service Account)

When I navigate to a Account (RT: Service Account)

And I am on the Related tab

Then on the “Contracts” related list I can see “Contract Record Type” field after the “Contract Number”

AC3: Account (RT: **Service Account**) - Add **PNC** Related List

Given I am a Corporate Support user with access to Account (RT: Service Account)

When I navigate to a Account (RT: Service Account)

And I am on the Related tab 

Then I have the ability to view related Provider Network Contract records

- Fields to display: Name (link), Contract (link), SCP Corporate Entity, Client Start Up Date (Sort By - most recent/descending order), Client Termination Date

## Solution

Create the Related List as per AC
