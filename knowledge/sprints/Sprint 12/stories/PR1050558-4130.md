## Description

### Description

The automated "Preliminary Sun Notification Report" and "FINAL Sun Notification Report" and “Sun Revision email “ emails are being sent with multiple blank fields. This prevents stakeholders from receiving complete information. The issues appear related, as many of the same fields (e.g., Malpractice Insurance, Corporate Entity, Client Start Up Date) are missing from _all 3_ emails.

### Steps to Reproduce (STR)

1. Log in to **Salesforce** as an **Implementation Specialist** user.

1. Navigate to **App Launcher → Corporate Support App → PMT Cases**.

1. Click **New** to create a **PMT Case** record.

1. Set the **PMT Type** = “SUN - Preliminary”

1. Repeat Steps 2–7 with **PMT Type = “SUN  Preliminary”** and **“SUN  Revision”**.

1. Open the received **Final Sun Notification Email**.

### **4. Expected vs. Actual Results**

#### **Issue 1 – Preliminary Startup Notification Report**

** **Expected Result:*

The email should be fully populated with all required data fields from the PMT Phase, Service Account, and related Contract.

** **Actual Result:*

The following fields are **blank**:

** Medical Malpractice Insurance Limits per Occurrence

** Medical Malpractice Insurance Aggregate

** Corporate Entity

*** **Client Owner*

*** **Annual ED Billable Volume*

*** **Annual HM Patient Encounters*

*** **Tele Services Active* is showing No it should be yes (Condition : Display “Yes” if “TeleMedicine”is Greater than '0')

#### **Issue 2 – Final Startup Notification Report**

** **Expected Result:*

The email should display complete and accurate field values, including those from the **related PNC** and **Client Contract** records.

** **Actual Result:*

The following fields are **blank**:

** Annual ED Billable Volume

** Annual HM Patient Encounters

** Service line

** Medical Malpractice Insurance Limits per Occurrence

** Medical Malpractice Insurance Aggregate

** Corporate Entity

** Client Start Up Date

** Client Start Up Time

** Client Start Up Time Zone

*** **Tele Services Active* is showing No it should be yes (Condition : Display “Yes” if “TeleMedicine”is Greater than '0')

#### **Issue 3 – Startup Revision Notification Email**

** **Expected Result:*

Revision emails should pull the same data fields as the **SUN - Preliminary** Notification.

** **Actual Result:*

Same data gaps observed — several key fields missing from the email body.
