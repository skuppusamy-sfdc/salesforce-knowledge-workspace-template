## Description

As a Legal team member, when I am on a Letter of Intent Contract, I want the ability to Activate, so that I can indicate that a contract status should be Executed and populate Activated by, Activated Date & user who Activated.

## Acceptance Criteria

AC1: Ability to ‘Activate Contract'

Given I am a Legal Team Member within the Corporate Support Apps

When navigate to a Contract (RT: Letter of Intent) record

Then I have the ability to select the ‘Activate Contract’ guided experience

And available navigation options:

- Cancel (cancel is via the “X” in upper right)

** No updates are made

** Guided Experience is closed

** User is returned to the Letter of Intent Contract

- Finish/Save

** Guided Experience is closed

** User is returned to the Letter of Intent

** And the system shall make the following updates:

*** Populate “Activated Date” with current/today’s date

*** Populate “Activated By” with the user who initiated ‘Activate Contract’

*** Updates “Status” = “Executed”

*** And the system shall locate the **previous contract in status = Executed* and make the following update:

*** Update “Status” = “Superseded”

(Note: the way the Previous Contract field gets populated is when a user initiates the “Amend Contract”)

## Solution

Flow Action in Contract RT: Letter of Intent

Name: Activate Contract

Contract.ActivatedDate →  Activated Date (Today)

Contract.ActivatedById → Activated By(Current User)

Update Contract Record 

And the system shall locate the **previous contract in status = Executed**

if Contract.PreviousContractId is not NULL update 

- [Contract.Id|http://Contract.Id] =  Contract.PreviousContractId

- Contract.Status = Superseded

Add Permission set = **Activate Contract** to Permission Set Group = **Legal**
