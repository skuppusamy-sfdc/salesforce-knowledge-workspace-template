## Description

As a Sales VP, I want an email notification to be sent to the Legal Teams DL, so that they are made aware of a new Letter of Intent request.

## Acceptance Criteria

AC1: Send Email 

Given I am a Sales VP in the Corporate Support or Corporate Support Console apps

When I navigate to an **Opportunity** (Growth Opportunity) record 

And I clicked the “**Initiate Contracting Request**” button

And successfully created a **Letter of Intent** contract record (system creates upon completion of the guided experience) [[https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505581676|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505581676|smartlink] & [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505583463|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505583463|smartlink]  ]

Then the system shall create an email:

- An email is sent to:

** DL(PROD): <[+DL.ClientContracting@schumachergroup.onmicrosoft.com+|mailto:DL.ClientContracting@schumachergroup.onmicrosoft.com]>

- Email Subject: New Letter of Intent Request for <Opportunity Name>

- Email Body:

** Hi,

A Letter of Intent request has been created for the following Opportunity: <Display “Opportunity Name”. when the user clicks the opportunity name it will link them back to Opportunity Record>

The following Contract has been created: <Contract Number, link to the letter of intent contract record>

Thank you, 

<Opportunity Owner’s First Name>

## Solution

Update the Omnis Script InitiateContractingRequest to send email upon creation

Contract.RT: Letter of Intent is created 

- Email Subject: as per AC

- Email Body: as per AC

The Email is storied in a Custom Label
