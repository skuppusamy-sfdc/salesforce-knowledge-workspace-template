## Description

As a Legal team member, when I am on a Client Contract, I want the ability to Activate, so that I can indicate that a contract status should be Executed and populate Activated by, Activated Date & user who Activated.

## Acceptance Criteria

AC: Ability to ‘Activate Contract'

Given I am a Legal Team Member within the Corporate Support Apps

When navigate to a Contract (RT: Client Contract) record

And Contract Type = Master

Then I have the ability to select the ‘Activate Contract’ guided experience

And system shall check that field = ‘SCP Corporate Entity’ is populated on ALL related Provider Network Contract (PNC) records

- If yes, then allow user to click 'Finish”

- If no, then display the below message and NOT allow the user to ‘Finish’

** Message: ‘SCP Corporate Entity’ field must populated on all related Provider Network Contract(s) to activate this contract.

And available navigation options:

- Cancel (cancel is via the “X” in upper right)

** No updates are made

** Guided Experience is closed

** User is returned to the Client Contract

- Finish/Save

** Guided Experience is closed

** User is returned to the Client Contract

** And the system shall make the following updates:

*** Populate “Activated Date” with current/today’s date

*** Populate “Activated By” with the user who initiated ‘Activate Contract’

*** Updates “Status” = “Executed”

*** And the system shall locate the **previous contract in status = Executed* (only if it was “Amended” - Previous Contract populated) and make the following update:

*** Update “Status” = “Superseded”

Notes:

|ACTIONS (guided process)|no condition on displaying actions| |

|Activate|Used to update status to Executed. populates Activated by, date & user who “Activated”|System shall also update the original/Previous contract status = “Superseded”|

## Solution

New Button in Contract RT: Client Contract AND Contract_Type__c = “Master” (as part of this story create the Ultiamte_Parent_Id__c field)

On Button Click

Query Contract.Ultiamte_Parent_Id__c = CURRENT.Contract.Ultiamte_Parent_Id__c AND Contract.Status = “Executed” AND [Contract.Id|http://Contract.Id] != [CURRENT.Contract.Id|http://CURRENT.Contract.Id]

- Update all returning records Contract.Status = “Superseded”

Update Current Record 

1. Contract.Status = “Executed”

1. Contract.Activated By = Current User

1. Contract.Activated Date = Current Date
