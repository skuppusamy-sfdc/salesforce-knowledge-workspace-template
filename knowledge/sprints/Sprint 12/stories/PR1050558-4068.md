## Description

Approval Work Item Not Generated After Contract Submission for leased and locum as Clinician

**Persona:** Recruiter

**Tester**: Nikhil Kokate

**Test Case ID:** CONT007,CONT008,CONT009,CONT010

**Steps to Reproduce:**

1. From the Person Account you created in CONT-001, click on the Agreements tab

2. Drill into the Contract Number for a Contract in Status = Draft

3. Add notes into ""Contract Special Terms"" and Save

4. Click on the Summary tab

5. Click ""Submit Contract Request""

_Verify that Contract was Submitted_

6. Click on the Approval tab

7. Validate that an Approval Work Item has been created

**Observation**: The contract status is updated to "Submitted," but no Approval Work Item is created under the "Approval" tab. The item is not assigned to the Tactical Team Queue.

**Expected Results**

1. User can add Contract Special Terms to a Draft Contract

1. User can Submit Contract for Approval

1. Contract gets Submitted and Assigned to the Tactical Team Queue

**Expected Result:**

An Approval Work Item should be created and visible under the "Approval" tab, assigned to the Tactical Team Queue.

**Actual Result:**The contract status is updated to "Submitted," but no Approval Work Item is created under the "Approval" tab. The item is not assigned to the Tactical Team Queue.

## Root Cause

The reported defect concerning the missing approval work item after contract submission was due to a **misunderstanding of the approved business process requirements** for specific contract types.

** The original test scripts and user expectation **assumed** that **leased and locum contracts* required a formal approval work item generated and routed to the Tactical Team Queue.

** **Intended Logic Confirmed:** The system behavior was confirmed to be correct, i.e., leased and locum contracts are designed to **bypass the approval process** and transition directly to the **"Executed" status* because they do not involve special terms requiring review.

** **Resolution:* The issue was closed after review confirmed the system was functioning as intended. The test scripts were updated to align with the correct process, excluding leased/locum contracts from the "Data Setup Required" and approval testing conditions.
