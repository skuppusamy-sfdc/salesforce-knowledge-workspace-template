## Description

As a Legal team member, when I receive a Client Agreement Request from the Sales VP, I want to create a new Client Contract(s) so that I can capture the necessary information and track it through the execution process.

## Acceptance Criteria

AC4: View **Client Contracts - Expanded View**

Given I am a Legal Team Member within the Corporate Support Apps

When I select a Client Contract (Client Agreement Request/Contract Type = Master) record [From: Contract tab list view = “Open Client Contract Requests” [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505581736|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505581736|smartlink]  or from the Opportunity’s Contract related list [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505582166|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505582166|smartlink] ]

Then when I am viewing the related records:

** I have the ability to **filter by a specific Service Account* [via Provider Network Contract record]

** System displays the related records that contain the selected Provider Network Contract Service Account

*** Ability to **view the** **details* of all Related Records for the selected Provider Network Contract Service Account [user should not need to click or expand to view details within each of the related records, each section & record should be expanded by default]

*** Term & Renewals 

*** Compensation

*** Protection

*** Insurance

*** Special Terms

*** Legal Terms

*** Termination

- AND when NO Provider Network Contract Service Account filter is applied, then records displayed within each section will only displaying highlevel details (Name and related Provider Network Contracts Service Account-s)

** User will select a specific record to view all the details

## Solution

In Omni Script:

The Picklist display the list of Account records from ProviderNetworkContract.ContractId = [Contract.Id|http://Contract.Id] (the Contract where Contract_Type__c = Master) display   ProviderNetworkContract.Service_Account__r.Name  (also [ProviderNetworkContract.Id|http://ProviderNetworkContract.Id] needed for next step to filter)

Button Click “Show Service Account Configuration”

Field all Contract record identified by Contract.ParentContractId = [Contract.Id|http://Contract.Id] (the Contract where Contract_Type__c = Master) filter by 

ProviderNetworkContract Service Account Records

Query: Select Id, (Contract Field) FROM Contract WHERE Contract.ParentContractId = [Contract.Id|http://Contract.Id] AND  [Contract.Id|http://Contract.Id] IN (Select Contract__c FROM Contract_Provider_Network_Relation__c Where Provider_Network_Contract__c = [ProviderNetworkContract.Id|http://ProviderNetworkContract.Id] (from selection)

Display all section in an expanded view like below **(View ONLY no other Actions)**

When the selected PNC has no configurations, display the following message:

No Records Found. The selected Service Account does not have any configurations to display. Please choose a different account.
