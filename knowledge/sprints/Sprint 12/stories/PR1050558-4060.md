Persona: Clinician Contracting Specialist

Test Case ID: CONT - 0052

Description:

Navigate to the Clinician - Approved and Term Sheet Executed list view.

The list view that the specialists will be using is over-inclusive. It should show only those requests that have been approved and do not require a Term Sheet as well as those requests that are approved, require a term sheet and the term sheet has been executed.

## Root Cause

The initial requirements for the Clinician Request List View failed to fully specify the necessary **exclusion criteria** related to approval and term sheet status. This resulted in a filter that was too broad.
