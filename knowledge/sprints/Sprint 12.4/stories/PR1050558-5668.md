## Description

**Tester:** Matthew Hebert

**Role:** Clinician Contracting Specialist

**Description:** 

In the tactical AWI queue there were three separate AWIs related to the same Clinician Contract (Elizabeth Galland). We approved one, and the status progressed to the clinician contracting queue. However, the other two AWIs remain in the Tactical queue. I don't want to reject them and inadvertenly cancel the contract record. I spoke to Maci and she thought that there shouldn't have been three AWIs in the first place. What is the appropriate action to take?

**Steps to Reproduce:**

Navigate to Tactical AWI queue, there are mutiple AWIs related to the same Contract record (00470875). In the most recent AWI we selected approve and approved. Contract moved forward in the approval process, but the other two remained.

**Expected vs. Actual:**

There should only be one AWI in the tactical team work items queue related to a contract record at one time. If the recruiter pulls a request back, then the existing AWIs should be canceled. If there will be more than one we need a process related to how to resolve the extra AWIs
