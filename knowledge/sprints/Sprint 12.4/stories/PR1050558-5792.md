## Solution

**As an Admin, I can update an HPF Facility’s Facility Title (CodeSet) lookup value to reflect that a clinician’s role at a particular HPF facility has changed but not ended.** 

Currently the batch close process that deactivates HPFs on their “Effective To” dates assumes the total end of the relationship so the Termination Date is flagged which results in closing all other HPFs. This is a problem when the HPF is only expiring because a NEW HPF was created to represent a change in Facility Title. (Consider a MD that takes a role as a Director of Medicine at a Facility for which they currently work, but only at that one Facility. On all others they remain a MD)
