**Description:** 

Recruiting/Asymbl: During the design and build phases, we discussed reporting needs at great length. Asymbl demo'd "out of box" reports and dashboards that address foundational recruiting reporting needs (time to recruit, candidate pipeline, etc.). We provided access to VITAL and explained how our KPIs are measured and reported. To date, there is zero reporting available for recruiting and no line of sight as to when it will be available or which business users have access to create reports.

Clinician Contracting: One report is available (Daily Contracting Open Cases 2026) - however, this report needs to be modified. It currently adds up all the HPFs associated with requests in the contracting case queue which is misleading to the executive audience that receives this report every day. Additionally, we need a basic report that shows clinical contracting throughput (cases completed). These are basic and foundational reporting needs required to effectively measure performance and to manage the business.
