## Description

**Current State:**

- If a clinician does not receive or loses their intake email, recruiters have no selfservice mechanism to reshare access.

- The previous Corp org allowed recruiters to manually share registration URLs via text/SMS; this capability does not currently exist on the new platform.

**Business Impact:**

- Blocked clinicians require escalation to admins, creating bottlenecks and delayed onboarding. High volume of support tickets stems from this gap.

**Requirement:**

- On the Clinician Account page in Salesforce, surface the Experience Cloud registration URL as a readable field or via a button action.

- Recruiters should be able to copy and share this URL directly with the clinician (via text or other channels).

- Explore use of a button vs. standalone field to minimize page layout clutter (stakeholder concern noted).
