This bug was split from [+PR10505585791+|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505585791], and addresses/ensures the following are configured appropriately:

1. When NP/PA Clinician contract is executed, a single case should be created per Service Account (HPF related to the Contract where "Delete?" = False/Unchecked)) 

1. When MD/DO Clinician contract is executed, a single case should be created per Service Account (HPF related to the Contract where "Delete?" = False/Unchecked)) 

1. Ensure History Tracking is enabled on "Supervision/Collaboration Status", "NP/PA Healthcare Practitioner Facility" & "Physician Healthcare Facility"

1. If an existing CPA record exists between the NP/PA and MD/DO, update the CPA record with new HPF with Supervision/Collaboration Status = “Pending”

1. CPA records are visible under NP/PA & MD/DO clinicians
