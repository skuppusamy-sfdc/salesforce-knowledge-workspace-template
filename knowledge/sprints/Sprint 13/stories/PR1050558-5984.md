## Description

**Submitter:** Matthew Hebert

**Role:** Clinician Contracting Specialist

**Description:** 

The Termination Date field on the HPF is mis-labeled. Termination date implies the effective date that the HPF flips to inactive. The "Information" next to the field says that the date is the date that the clinician stopped working. 

However, upon auditing, it appears to be a date stamp of the completion of the last completed task under the related Offboarding Case (similar to our old TSG Inactive Date)

**Steps to Reproduce:**

Navigate to Roger Shockey Person Account. Under cases, access the Offboarding case related to Holston Valley Medical Center - EM. Under the completed activities, access the task related to Update Malpractice Insurance. Note that the task was completed 2/19. 

Navigate back to the Person Account. Under Facilities, navigate to the HPF for Holston Valley Medical Center - EM. Note that the Termination Date matches the completion date for the Update Malpractice Insurance task. Note also that the HPF Effective To Date is 1/12. 

Hover over the encircled "i" next to the Termination Date field. Neither the label nor the explanation match what is actually filled in.

**Expected vs. Actual:**

Expected vs Actual Result depends upon what was intended in the build.

## Acceptance Criteria

AC1: Update Help Text on HPF field = ‘Termination Date’

Given I am a Clinician Contracting Specialist or user with view access to HPFs

When I am on a Healthcare Practitioner Facility (HPF) record

And I click on the help text icon ('i') for field = ‘Termination Date'

Then I can see help text = AKA ‘TSG Inactive Date’. Date stamp that the HPF was terminated by the user/system.

## Solution

**DevNotes:** 

+ONLY+ - Update the help text property of the field: 

**HealthcarePractitionerFacility**.+_TerminationDate_+ 

**From:** _“Date the practitioner stopped working at this facility.”_

**To:** **_“_**_AKA ‘TSG Inactive Date’. Date stamp that the HPF was terminated by the user/system.”_
