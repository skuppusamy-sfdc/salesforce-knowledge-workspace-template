Business Process Order of Operations. 

A contract is cancelled from the Healthcare Practitioner Facility record rather than unchecking the active boolean on the Service Account. A Termination Date on the HPF will trigger an automated flow to recurse through it’s related records. 

Further, a case is created for a team to manually clean up the remainder of the the items. We can see it on your example below the HPFS
