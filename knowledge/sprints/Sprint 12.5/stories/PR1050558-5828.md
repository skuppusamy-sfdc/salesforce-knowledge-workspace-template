## Description

**Tester:** Kristin Koide

**Role:** IT

**Description:** 

Per story PR1050558-2473, the system should update the HPF to Status = 'Inactive' on the 'Effective To' date.

**Steps to Reproduce:**

1. Persona: MSL, Clinician Experience Specialist, or Data Quality team initiates the 'Initiate Termination Request' from a clinician record.

2. in the 'Initiate Termination Request' guided experience select full SCP termination and ensure that 'Last Day' is in the future. (ex. 3/1/2026)

2. First ensure that the Facility Offboarding tasks are completed and case is closed

3. then ensure that the SCP Offboarding tasks are complete and case is closed with Offboarding Decision: Confirmed Exit

**Expected vs. Actual:**

Expected Result: system should update the HPF record fields when Effective To is in the past)

Status = Inactive

Contracting Status = Terminated

Termination Date = day automation ran

Actual Results: HPF record updates are happening immediately instead of happening on the 'Effective To' Date:

HPF updates (occur when Effective To is in the past)

Status = Inactive

Contracting Status = Terminated

Termination Date = day automation ran
