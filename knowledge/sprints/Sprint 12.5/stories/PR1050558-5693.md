## Acceptance Criteria

As a Payroll Persona, when I clone a Practice Operations Contract the following fields are replicated from the old record to the new clone record. 

- Description

- NPPA Tier

- Avg Monthly NPPA Shifts

- Avg Monthly Physician Shifts

- Leadership Monthly Stipend

- Scribe Hours per Day

- Scribe Provider

- Scribe Rate

## Solution

Update Flow “Clone_Contract” to add the fields in the AC to the “Set New Contract Fields” element
