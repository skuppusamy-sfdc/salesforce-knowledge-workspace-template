## Acceptance Criteria

AC1: As a Recruiter, when I create an Intake Case via the automated process (ATS Applicant record Stage = Awaiting Intake Form) I want the Clinician Profile Update Assessment to be based off of the Employment Type of the Job’s Facility Title, so that the CTI section can be displayed when Employment Type = Independent Contractor. This updates original story [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558231|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558231|smart-link]. 

Given I am a Clinician with an open Intake Form case created when ATS Stage field on an ATS Applicant record to **Awaiting Intake Form**

When I log in to the Core Connect portal

Then I am able to complete the **Clinician Profile Update Assessment** Intake Form

And the CTI (Corporation and Tax Information) section shall be displayed when Employment Type = Independent Contractor (IC), based on the **Job.Facility_Title_Lookup__c** not **HealthcarePractitionerFacility.Facility_Title__c** to determine **Employment Type**

[See story 231 for CTI section information]

NOTE: HPF record does not get auto created until much further in the recruiting process (Stage= Offer)
