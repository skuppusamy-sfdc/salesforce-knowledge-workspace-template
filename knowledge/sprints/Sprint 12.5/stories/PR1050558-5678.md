## Description

**Tester:** Nicole Fontenot

**Role:** Legal – Client Contracting

**Description:** 

The “Send Offboarding Email” step is the final and critical action within the Begin Facility Termination process, serving as the official notification to the company of an upcoming client termination. This step has replaced the former Termination DL process and is now the sole mechanism for communicating termination events.

Currently, when selecting “Send Offboarding Email” from the PNC to release the notification, the email is not being received. This issue was identified during the processing of terminations for Upson Regional Medical CenterEM and Upson Regional Medical CenterHM.

**Steps to Reproduce:**

From the Contract, click on the "Provider Network Contracts" tab

Click into a Provider Network Contract Name

Click on the down arrow next to the buttons in the header and choose "Send Offboarding Email"

Click "Send Email"

**Expected vs. Actual:**

Expected an email that includes the image attached below.
