## Description

Lindsey Calbert, Reianna Lang, and Antonio Suarez need VPO access in order to request Leadership changes. These are the only 3 people who are going to be performing this duty so will need to have ability to make those changes.

## Acceptance Criteria

Currently: Users who will be requesting leadership changes must add themselves as VPO Account Team members in order to progress the record. These are the admins who do the work on-behalf of the VPOs

Future: Users who will be requesting leadership changes on behalf of their VPO’s will be added to a QUEUE named VPO Delegate. The Omniscript “LeadershipChangeOS” is updated to check that the active user is a VPO OR They are a member of the VPO Delegate Queue.
