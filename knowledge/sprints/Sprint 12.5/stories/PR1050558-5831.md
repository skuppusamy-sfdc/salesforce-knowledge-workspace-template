## Description

**Tester:** Maci Broussard

**Role:** Legal – Client Contracting

**Description:** 

Met with business users: Nicole Fontenot, Jonathan Winarski, Abby Stutes, and Lisa Kleyla. Below is the meeting recap of what was discussed and decision made. 

Meeting Purpose: Discuss what involvement Client Contracting is needed for approval of Practice Ops requests, as they’re currently required to approve all submissions.

Meeting Goal: Understand why the decision was made during refinement and what changes are needed.

Decision: Remove Client Contracting from approval process entirely.

Recap: 

- Business intention was not to require Client Contracting’s approval on all Practice Ops requests.

- In the past, Client Contracting has been involved in facility changes via Account Mgmt approvals; however, client contract language has changed significantly since that process was put in place and they no longer need to have oversight into these changes.

- Contract changes should be communicated from operators to Client Contracting via email, like all other changes are requested.

**Business Impact:**

Removes an unnecessary approval requirement from Practice Operations approval workflow by removing Client Contracting entirely. Put Medium Priority as until this is done, Client Contracting is approving the requests even though they shouldn't be required to approve. It's not blocking any process. But we did discuss putting this in R3 if possible.

## Acceptance Criteria

As a Client Contracting Persona I no longer receive approval requests for Practice Operations Contracts so that I’m not bothered by these, now unnecessary and unwanted, requests.

## Solution

Update Flow “Client Contract Review Approval Process”

Remove Step 3 Client Contracting Approval 

Ensure the flow from Existing Step 2 Flows to Existing Step 4 after the removal
