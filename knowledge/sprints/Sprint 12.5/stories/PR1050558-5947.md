## Description

**Submitter:** Ron Gregg

**Role:** IT

**Description:** 

The QGENDA_SCHEDULING_SETUP integration is getting triggered too early. We're seeing this happen on contract execution where its expected to trigger on Credentialing Status = 'Active' OR 'Scheduled Pending'. There would not be a case where we want this to happen on contract execution as they need to go through the credentialing process even if they're rehired.

**Expected vs. Actual:**

The trigger would fire on Credentialing Status change instead of contract execution. The process was setup to trigger on Credentialing Status and we do have successful calls, but these failed ones are happening on Contract Execution.

**Record Link:** 

N/A

**Business Impact:**

Producing errors when the event shouldn't have triggered. Could prevent pay rates from going to QGenda Scheduling which would cause Payroll issues.

## Solution

Data Issue

The Rates (Qgenda Scheduling Setup) event should come to MuleSoft only after the Credential Status is Active or Schedule Pending on HC. As per discussion with Nick & Ron on 26th Feb, the Credential Status for the corresponding contract (800Ps00001GEPzMIAX) was not in Active and MuleSoft received the event.
