## Description

As a Clinician Contract Specialist, I need HPF and Contract Dates in alignment, so that clinicians are contracted and paid appropriately

## Acceptance Criteria

**update to** [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505584997|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505584997|smart-link] 

+AC1: Clinician Contracts can only be requested within HPF Dates+

Given I am a Recruiter in the Corporate Support apps

When I request a contract for a clinician (all employment types and facility titles)

Then I am only able to select HPFs where the HPF Effective From is on or **before** the Start Date of the Contract

_Example 1:_

Hiring EE clinician

HPF 1 Effective From `20260215` (from Asymbl process)

Contract needs to start `20260201`

HPF 1 won’t be available to select, **Recruiter needs to ask Data Quality to** adjust HPF date to `20260201` or earlier then they can adjust/request the contract

_Example 2:_

Updating rates for existing clinician

HPF 1 Effective From `20200601` (from data migration)

Contract needs to start `20260201`

HPF 1 will be available to select, clinician has been working and just needs new rates

## Solution

Modify the criteria in the query to pick HPF as per AC
