## Description

When the “**ContractTerminationBatch” runs,** Offboarding cases is created & Closedwith status = Closed & Complete **but with** blank Subject & Contact name and **Termination details also blank.**

**2. HPF Effective to = 'day automation ran' not updated.** 

Termination date = today is updated.

Expectation : 

- Offboarding case should be created with Subject & Contact name (Clinician)

- HPF Effective date = todays date. / Last day?

## Solution

Per related 3772:

- Date Termination Received = date the automation ran

- Termination Request Date = date the automation ran

- Last Day = date the automation ran

- Reason for Termination = “Contract Termination”
