**Description:** Go to CTI record to view the expiration date of an executed CTI.

Expect a future Expiration Date to be populated for the latest executed CTI records. What we are seeing is a blank expiration date on the latest executed CTI records and therefore the termination workflow to Workday will not fire.

CTI Expiration date is one of the requirements to terminate an IC Provider.  Not all the latest CTI executed records have expiration dates. Krisiti Bouillon is okay to load 12/31/2026 as the future expiration date for those executed records that do not have an expiration date. The termination integration will not fire without seeing a future Expiration date.
