## Description

As a Business Development Associate, from a Service Account I need to be able to view related Corporate Entities and vice versa, so that I can see the relationship between Service Accounts and Corporate Entities.

## Acceptance Criteria

Given that I am a Business Development Associate in the Corporate Support or Corporate Support Console app

When I open an Account record where the Record Type = **Service Account**

Then I am able to see:

- Related Tab

** Account Plans (pcrush object), Opportunities, Contracts, Contacts, Corporate Entities, Account History

*** Corporate Entities related list (Account Account Relationship record between the Service Account and Corporate Entity Accounts)

*** Columns: Account Relationship Id, Account, Tax Designation, Active

NOTE: other existing related lists created in [PR105055886|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR105055886]

Given that I am a Business Development Associate in the Corporate Support or Corporate Support Console app

When I open an Account record where the Record Type = **Corporate Entity**

Then I am able to see:

- Related Tab

** Contacts, Contracts, Service Accounts, Account History, and Files related lists

*** Service Accounts related list (Account Account Relationship record between the Corporate Entity Account and Service Accounts)

*** Columns: Account Relationship Id, Related Account, Service Line, Active

NOTE: other existing related lists created in [PR105055886|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR105055886]

Given that I am a Business Development Associate in the Corporate Support or Corporate Support Console app

When I open an Account Account Relationship record

Then I am able to see:

Header with record name only

One section with tabs: Details

Details tab contains fields as specified in data dictionary

## Solution

configure LRP for Account Account Relationship object

add related lists to Corporate Entity and Service Account pages

create record for Party Role Relationship as specified in the [Health Cloud Records|https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1B6WddJnXHDZD5kx5F1mZDqEvcAwYn1wCPejZ0ZgJpP0/edit?usp=sharing] sheet
