## Description

As a Corporate Support user, I need to see clear related lists on a clinician record, so that I can quickly understand important data for this person to complete appropriate actions

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a Corporate Support user

When I navigate to a clinician record

Then I am able to see the following:

- Facilities tab

** Healthcare Practitioner Facilities related list

*** Columns: Practitioner Facility Name, Account, Status, Facility Title

*** No actions visible

- Agreements tab

** Contracts related list (Contract object, record type = Clinician)

*** Columns: Contract Number, Status, Contract Start Date, Contract End Date

*** No actions visible

*** _Must display records where Clinician = person account record you are viewing_

** Collaborative Practice Agreement related list (Contract object, record type = Collaborative Practice Agreement)

*** Columns: Contract Number, Status, Contract Start Date, Contract End Date

*** No actions visible

*** _Must display records where Clinician = person account record you are viewing_ 

- Qualifications tab

** Business Licenses related list _VISIBLE ONLY TO users with Manage Clinician Identifiers, Credentials, Specialties_

*** Columns: Name, Type, State, Status, Last Modified Date

*** New action available for users with access

** Board Certifications related list _VISIBLE ONLY TO users with Manage Clinician Identifiers, Credentials, Specialties_

*** Columns: Board Certification Name, Board Name, Status, Last Modified Date

*** New action available for users with access

** Healthcare Provider Specialties related list _VISIBLE ONLY TO users with Manage Clinician Identifiers, Credentials, Specialties_

*** Columns: Specialty Name, Specialty, Primary Board Specialty, Sub Specialty, Board Certification Status, Last Modified Date

*** New action available for users with access

** Healthcare Provider Taxonomies related list _VISIBLE ONLY TO users with Manage Clinician Identifiers, Credentials, Specialties_

*** Columns: Taxonomy Name, Taxonomy, Primary Taxonomy, Last Modified Date

*** New action available for users with access

** Person Educations related list _VISIBLE ONLY TO users with Manage Clinician Identifiers, Credentials, Specialties_

*** Columns: Degree, Education Level, Completion Date, Program Name, Last Modified Date

*** New action available for users with access

** Person Employments related list _VISIBLE ONLY TO users with Manage Clinician Identifiers, Credentials, Specialties_

*** Columns: Employer, Employment Type, Employment Status, Last Modified Date

*** New action available for users with access

** Visa related list

*** Columns: Visa Name, Type, Expiration Date, Copy Info, Last Modified Date

*** New action available for users with access

- Activities tab

** Open Activities related list

*** Columns: Subject, Due Date, Status, Assigned To, Create Date, Last Modified Date, Related To 

** Activity History related list

*** Columns: Subject, Due Date, Status, Assigned To, Create Date, Last Modified Date, Related To

- Cases tab

** Open Onboarding related list (Case object, record type = Clinician Onboarding, status DOES NOT EQUAL closed)

*** Columns: Subject, Record Type, Type, Status, Owner, Last Modified Date

*** No actions visible

** Open Risk Review related list (Case object, record type = Risk Review, status DOES NOT EQUAL closed)

*** Columns: Subject, Record Type, Type, Status, Owner, Last Modified Date

*** No actions visible

** Cases related list (Case object, record type does not equal Clinician Onboarding or Risk Review, status DOES NOT EQUAL closed)

*** Columns: Subject, Record Type, Type, Status, Owner, Last Modified Date

*** No actions visible

** Closed Cases related list (Case object, status = closed)

*** Columns: Subject, Record Type, Type, Status, Owner, Last Modified Date

*** No actions visible

- Related tab

** Corporation and Tax Information related list 

*** Columns: Name, Type, Status, Tax Classification

*** New action available for users with access _VISIBLE TO users with Manage Clinician Corporate Tax Information_ 

** Person Account History related list

*** Columns: _standard related list_

** Files related list

*** Columns: _standard related list_

*** Upload Files action available

_Remove Activity tab on right column - only Chatter should display since Activities will move to the larger column_

## Solution

update LRP with related lists and tabs specified in the story

update permission set according to security matrix (Manage Clinician Identifiers, Credentials, Specialties)
