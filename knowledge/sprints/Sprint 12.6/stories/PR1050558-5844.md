## Acceptance Criteria

Changing from a Bug to an Enhancement, we will update the 'Clinician Details' card to hide specific sections ('Active HPFs' & ‘Specialties’) based on user’s access. 

AC1: Update original story 4009, updates in blue

Given I am a user in the Corporate Support apps

When I navigate to a clinician record

Then I am able to see the following attributes on the Clinician Card:

- Account Name (display data only, not the field name)

- Status

- Professional Title

- Mobile

- Email

- NPI

- Global Provider ID

- Active HPF (collapsed by default) – Hide section when user does not have access to the HPF object & related fields

** Healthcare Practitioner Facility object where Status = Active

- Specialties (collapsed by default)  – Hide section when user does not have access to the Healthcare Provider Specialty object & related fields

** Healthcare Provider Specialty object no additional filter

## Solution

1. Users who do not have access to Object: HPF, shall not see just the ‘Active HPF’ section of the component

1. Users who do not have access to Object: HealthcareProviderSpecialty, shall not see just the “Specialties” section of the component
