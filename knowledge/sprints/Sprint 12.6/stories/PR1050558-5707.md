## Description

**Tester:** Maci Broussard

**Role:** Recruiter

**Description:** 

Recruiters are cloning data migrated Clinician Contract record where the entity field has a value from legacy corp, but our data model has changed and entity, in some cases, should not be present on the contract record. Impacts creation of agreement records (Conga / Clinician Contract Specialist impact). 

Short Term/Immediate solution: Clinician Contract Specialist should remove the value when populated incorrectly, prior to creating agreement records.

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a Recruiter within the Corporate Support Apps

When I select “Contract Request” from the Clinician record (person account)

And I select “Start From Previous Active Contract”

Then the system clones and creates a new Clinician Contract in Status = Draft

And the “Contracting Corporate Entity” field shall NOT be cloned from the Previous Contract.
