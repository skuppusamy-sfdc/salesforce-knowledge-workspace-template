## Description

As a Medical Staff Liaison - CPA, I need potential supervisory relationships to be created automatically, so that they are ready for my review as part of the Collaborative Practice Agreement process

## Acceptance Criteria

Story Focus: Creating the CPA Review Object & fields. Functionality should be tested with 146 & 1655.

Given that I am a Medical Staff Liaison - CPA in the Corporate Support apps

When the Collaborative Practice Agreements and Cases are automatically created per [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558146|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558146|smart-link] 

Then the system needs to create one “CPA Review” record per:

- Newly created Collaborative Practice Agreement 

- Updated Collaborative Practice Agreement

And the following fields on the “CPA Review” record shall be populated as specified in the solution:

- Case

- Collaborative Practice Agreement

*** System Administrator

*** System Administrator (R,C,E,D,V,M)
