## Description

As a Recruiter, I need the ability to add additional Healthcare Practitioner Facility records to a Clinician Contract in Draft Status, so that I can appropriately offer appropriate pay and incentives for clinicians to join SCP

## Acceptance Criteria

Given that I am a Recruiter in the Corporate Support apps

When I navigate to a Clinician **Contract** record 

And I am on the Summary tab

And Contract status = **Draft**

Then I have the to click on **Add HPF** button

And the HPF Selection screen is displayed (built in story [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558893|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558893|smartlink] +& additional filter/validation called out in+ [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505581070|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505581070|smartlink])

And the clinician’s existing Healthcare Practitioner Facility records should be filtered based on the contract’s Employment Type.

And available Navigation buttons are:

- Cancel

** Returns to Clinician Contract’s Summary tab and no records were created or updated

- Finish

** If I select an Healthcare Practitioner Facility(s), that:

***  HAS already been added from the active contract (user selected “Start From Previous Active Contract” - facility and rates were cloned) 

***** Then the system shall add Healthcare Practitioner Facility to the Clinician’s Contract in **Green* (note: no rates are added at this point. User must configure rates)

***** And previous Healthcare Practitioner Facility w/cloned rates has been **grey/inactive/removed*.

****** If the Healthcare Practitioner Facility (cloned ID is populated) been **grey/inactive/removed*, then on the “Configure Rates” button shall be hidden.

*** HAS NOT been added from an active contract

**** Then the system shall add Healthcare Practitioner Facility to the Clinician’s Contract (note: no rates are added at this point. User must configure rates)

** Returns to Clinician Contract’s Summary tab
