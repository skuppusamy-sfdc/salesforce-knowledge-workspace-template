## Description

As a Recruiter, I want a comprehensive view of a Clinician’s Contract, so that I can see where they are in then process and what information is needed.

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a Recruiter in the Corporate Support apps

When I navigate to a Clinician record and the Agreements tab

And I click on a Contract record in the Contract related list

Then from the Clinician Contract record, I can view the fields that hold Facility Rate information that the Clinician rate was created from:

- On the Contract Payment Agreement record(s)

** Click on the Related tab

** Click on the Contract Payment Agreement record

** Then “Facility Rate” is displayed on the Details tab

- On the Preventive Care Agreement(s)

** From the Contract Payment Agreement record, click on the Related tab

** Click on the Preventive Care Agreement record

** Then on the Details tab, I can see the following new fields: 

*** Facility Rate

*** Previous Rate

*** Facility Level Rate

Given I am a Recruiter in the Corporate Support apps

When I navigate to a Clinician Contract record and configure a rate which has a standard Facility Rate

Then the “Facility Rate” lookup is populated on: 

- Preventive Care Agreement 

** Populated with the original Preventive Care Agreement record

- Contract Payment Agreement

** Populated with original Contract Payment Agreement record

And on the “Preventive Care Agreement” the following fields are added to support the cloning process: (see DD for details)

- Previous Rate

- Facility Level Rate

Note: These facility fields are populated when pulling rates from a Client Contract. Cloned From (299) fields are populated when rates are cloned from a clinician contract.
