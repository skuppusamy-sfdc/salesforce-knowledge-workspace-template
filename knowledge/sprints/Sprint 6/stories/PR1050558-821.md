## Description

As a Payroll team member, I need to receive a notification when Risk Review assessment is approved, so that I am aware of reviews that included Garnishments/Liens/Child support issues.

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a SVP Litigation, Regional Medical Officer, or Compliance user

When I navigate to an Approval Request record

Then I am able to **Approve** or **Reject** the Risk Review

And the following happens:

- Automated updates

** Email notification with final decision built in [+PR1050558903+|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558903]

*** **Risk Review with “Garnishments/Liens/Child support issues (route to Legal & Payroll)”*

*** If 

**** “Garnishments/Liens/Child support issues (route to Legal & Payroll)” was checked in the Risk Review

**** And the risk review was approved

*** Send a notification to payroll

***** Send an email to [**EEPR@scphealth.com**|mailto:EEPR@scphealth.com] **and** [**PHYSPR@scphealth.com**|mailto:PHYSPR@scphealth.com] **(NOTE: These are Production emails)*

**** Email content

****** **Hello,* 

******A risk review has recently been approved for clinician [full name]. This review included Garnishments/Liens/Child support issues.**

## Solution

Add Final step to Risk Review Approvals Flow

When the Everyone has Approved in the End of the Flow create the Notification to Payroll Step.

Use custom metadata to store the email Ids to send emails to for avoiding hardcoding

Use the Final Decision template created in: [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558903|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558903|smart-link] with different body of the email. In the subject line below, I crossed out rejected because we only send this notification if it’s approved. 

- Email Subject: <insert Case Type> Case <insert case number> is <approved or rejected>
