## Description

As a Recruiter, I want a comprehensive view of a Clinician’s Contract, so that I can see where they are in then process and what information is needed.

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a Recruiter in the Corporate Support apps

When I navigate to a Clinician record and the Agreements tab

And I click on a Contract record in the Contract related list

Then from the Clinician Contract record, I can view the Cloned From field:

- On the Contract Payment Agreement record(s)

** Click on the Related tab

** Click on the Contract Payment Agreement record

** Then “Cloned From” is displayed on the Details tab

- On the Preventive Care Agreement(s)

** From the Contract Payment Agreement record, click on the Related tab

** Click on the Preventive Care Agreement record

** Then “Cloned From is displayed on the Details tab

And on the Clinician Contract record I can see new field = “Previous Contract” on the Details tab

Given I am a Recruiter in the Corporate Support apps

When I navigate to a Clinician Contract record and configure a cloned rate  built in [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505581070|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505581070|smartlink] 

Then the ‘Cloned From’ lookup is populated on:

-  Preventive Care Agreement

** Populated with the original Preventive Care Agreement record

- Contract Payment Agreement

** Populated with the original Contract Payment Agreement record

And the “**Previous Contract**” lookup is populated:

- Contract (RT: Clinician)

*** Populated with the original **Clinician* Contract record. Do NOT populated with a Client Contract.
