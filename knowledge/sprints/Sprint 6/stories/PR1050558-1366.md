## Description

As a SFO (Staffing Fulfillment Optimization) user, I want the system to display an error message if I have not added new Shifts for this Contract and I try to Specify Rates, so that I am reminded to add Shifts first.

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a SFO in the Corporate Support or Corporate Support Console app

When I navigate to a **Contract** record (record type = **Client**)

And I have **NOT created any Shift records** for this Client (part of story [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558697|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558697|smart-link] )

And clicked the **Specify Rates** button (part of [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558703|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558703|smart-link] )

And I am presented with a Rate Type screen to capture:

- Type of Rate (Contract Payment Agreement, field = Type)

** Required to select one from the following list of values to proceed to the next screen:

*** Stipend

**** **Shift*

*** Variable

*** Target

*** Leadership Incentives

*** Bonus

- Facility Selection

** Select one option between (required):

*** Employed NP/PA

*** Other Facility Titles

And when I select **Shift**

**Then I am present with the following error message and I am unable to proceed: Please add Shift record(s) to proceed.  To add a Shift, go to the Related tab and click “New” in the Shift related list.**

## Solution

In OmniScript ServiceAcc/FacilityRateCreation/English add a condition 

When IP FetchShiftAndFacilityTitle returns No Record for Rate Type = Shift then Show

**Please add Shift record(s) to proceed.  To add a Shift, go to the Related tab and click “New” in the Shift related list** 

Else Normal flow as built previously.
