## Description

As a Recruiter, I need to specify bonuses for clinicians, so that they can be included in contracts and clinicians can be paid appropriately

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a Sales Operations team member in the Corporate Support apps

When I am on a Client Contract record

Then I am able to configure the following: 

- Shift Bonus becomes it’s own shift record where the rate includes the bonus

** Need ability to specify a Shift Type = “Shift Bonus” (new Code Set - Shift) 

** Unit of Measure has to be “Per Shift”

** Associate this shift with the appropriate pay tags in Salesforce to enable integration to QGenda

** In QGenda, adding shift to the bonus shift is manual for phase 1

- No other Bonus types are needed for Client Contracts - remove Bonus option

And Update Shift “Shift Iteration” Text from “20” to '255”

NOTE:

- Need a way to relate “Shift Bonus” with a Shift record. Example how we will handled in phase 1:

** Shift type= “Shift Bonus”

*** **Iteration* (Text 20) = “Night 1” (user to will input the Shift name that the Bonus is related to)

** Shift Full Name = Shift Bonus Night 1 (will have it’s own pay codes)

Given I am a Recruiter in the Corporate Support apps

When I am on a Clinician Contract record

And I configure rates where type of rate = Shift

Then I am able to see the following: (filtered by Employment type and facility title - NOT changing the filtering functionality for rates. ) – NO NEW BUILD REQUIRED

- Recruiters select the appropriate shift (with or without the bonus) to associate with a clinician 

- Recruiters have the ability to also unselect/select “Shift Bonus” as needed for the clinician (same filtering functionality as any other Shift rate) 

Given I am a Recruiter in the Corporate Support apps

When I am on a Clinician Contract record

And I configure rates where type of rate = Bonus

Then I am able to see the following: 

- Clinician bonus should have the following Types available: Sign On Bonus, Commencement Bonus, Relocation Bonus, New Grad Bonus, Retention Bonus, Locums Buyout, Other Bonus

- User can only add Additional bonus types to one Healthcare Practitioner Facility (primary) [story [+PR10505582086+|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505582086]]

- Once the “Type” field that holds the Additional Bonuses values is selected, then the following fields are required see story [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505582123|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505582123|smart-link]

## Solution

- Health Cloud Records

** Add new Code Set (Shift)  =  Shift Bonus

Change the length of field “Shift Iteration” from 20 t0 255

When configuring rates, if Shift and Night Shift Bonus is selected, ensure Unit of Measure is per shift - validation check on the screen. “Unit of measure has to be Per Shift for rates of Night Shift Bonus”
