## Description

As a Data Quality team member, I need clinician contract records for leased and locums clinicians to skip approval and contracting processes, so that they can move to onboarding as needed

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a Data Quality team member in the Corporate Support apps

When I create a Contract record where Employment Type = Leased OR Locums

And I select Submit Contract Request

Then the contract status is automatically updated to Executed

## Solution

Existing omniscript for Submit contract request,

If employee type selected is Leased or Locums, and goes through the contract request process, on final submit , create/update the contract in executed status. 

NOTE: This story is to skip the approval process for Locums and Leased. So the solution built for 301 , we have to update the approval process to be skipped for Employee Type Locums and leased.
