## Description

As a SFO, I want the ability to submit a Client Contract (Record type = Practice Operations) for approval, so that the contract can get approved for Clinician contracting.

## Acceptance Criteria

AC1: Prevent “Submit Contract Request” 

Given I am a SFO in the Corporate Support apps

When a Facility Change is made (Coverage or Rate changes)

And there is a Client Contract (Record Type = Practice Operations) in Status = Draft

And on the Service Account the Team Roles needed for the approval process are missing 

Then from the Client Contract record’s Summary tab, the user will NOT have the ability to “Submit Contract Request” 

And the following message is displayed:

“To ‘Submit Contract Request’ ensure all your approval roles are populated, please review the Account Team. Please reach out to the Data Quality team to make updates.”

AC2: Submit for approvals

Given I am a SFO in the Corporate Support apps

When a Facility Change is made (Coverage or Rate changes)

And there is a Client Contract (Record Type = Practice Operations) in Status = Draft

And on the Service Account the Team Roles needed for the approval process are available (if there are more than one member with the same Team Role, the select the most recently added team member) 

Then from the Client Contract record’s Summary tab, the user has the ability to “Submit Contract Request” 

And the following automatically happen:

- Client Contract “Status” set to “Submitted” 

- Client Contract record is +locked+ from being edited

- Approval Work Item records are created for the following approvers: Sequential approval is required

** Step 1: Performance Analyst approval

*** Assigned To: Account Team Role - Performance Analyst

*** Action:

**** If Approved, then proceed to Step 2

***** (NOTE Business process: is to ensure that the Pay Code is set up in Qgenda -work with Karen’s team prior to approving) 

**** If Rejected, then send a notification to the requestor and prior approvers [see AC2 for notification]

***** And update the Contract status = “Draft”

***** Record is unlocked for editing

** Step 2: Ops Finance approval

*** Assigned To: Account Team Role - Ops Finance

*** Client Contract record is +unlocked;+ allow editing in this step. Lock record after this step. 

**** Financial Operations captures financial impact on Contract record - updating the Financial Implications field (text, 1000) 

***** AND Financial Impact (currency, 9 digits, 0 decimals) _(NOTE: current SF field called_ **'Impact of Contract'* and  _is 16 digits, 2 decimals)_

*** Action:

**** If Approved, then proceed to Step 3

**** If Rejected, then send a notification to the requestor and prior approvers [see AC2 for notification]

***** And update the Contract status = “Draft”

***** Record is unlocked for editing

** Step 3: Client Contracting approval

*** Assigned To: Client Contracting Queue (Existing [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558472|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558472|smart-link]+,+ but need to add supported Objects Approval Submission)

*** Action:

**** If Approved, then proceed to Step 4

**** If Rejected, then send a notification to the requestor and prior approvers [see AC2 for notification]

***** And update the Contract status = “Draft”

***** Record is unlocked for editing

** Step 4: VP Operations approval 

*** Assigned To: VPO - Account team role = “VP Operations”

*** Action:

**** If Approved, 

***** And Financial Impact = Yes, then proceed to step 5

***** And Financial Impact = No, then update the Contract Status = “Executed”

**** If Rejected, then send a notification to the requestor and prior approvers [see AC2 for notification]

***** And update the Contract status = “Draft”

***** Record is unlocked for editing

*** Step 5: Division Operating Officer approval - **ONLY WHEN FINANCIAL IMPACT IS UPDATED TO $250,000 or greater IN STEP 2, otherwise skip*

*** Assigned To: DOO - Account team role = “Division Operating Officer” 

*** Action:

**** If Approved, then update the Contract Status = “Executed”

**** If Rejected, then send a notification to the requestor and prior approvers [see AC2 for notification]

***** And update the Contract status = “Draft”

***** Record is unlocked for editing
