## Description

As a Sales VP, I want the system to automatically create task(s) for the Internal Info Due Process, so that the appropriate users get informed to inputed the Pro Forma Information.

## Acceptance Criteria

AC1: Validation to update “Client Questionnaire Status” = Info Due Process Ready

Given I am a Sales VP in the Corporate Support Apps

When I update the Opportunity (RT: Opportunity Growth) field “Client Questionnaire Status” = Info Due Process Ready

Then the system will check that **ALL of the following Opportunity fields** are populated:

- Questions Due Back To Client (RFP/RFI)

- INFO DUE Date for All Inputs

- Targeted DAM Date

- Proposal Due Date

- Program Transition/Start Date (Est)

- Program Transition/Start Time (Est)

And **ALL the Deal Team members** are added to the Opportunity based on the related Services (status= Active):

- Role = Chief Medical Officer - EM

** When the Service Line’s Parent field = EM or UC

- Role = Chief Medical Officer - HM

** When the Service Line’s Parent field = HM

- Role = Hybrid Care Leader

** When the Service Line’s Parent field = VH or CCARE

- Role = Recruiting Manager

** Always, no extra criteria

- Role = BD Finance

** Always, no extra criteria

- Role = BD Enablement

** Always, no extra criteria

And if ALL of the opportunity field and Deal Team members are NOT MET, then:

- Prevent the user from updating/save “Client Questionnaire Status” = “Info Due Process Ready”

- Display the following error message: Please review Deal Team and/or Opportunity Info Due fields to ensure completeness.

And if ALL of the opportunity field and Deal Team members are MET, then:

- Allow the user to change and save “Client Questionnaire Status” = “Info Due Process Ready”

AC2: Auto create Info Due Tasks - Opportunity level

Given I am a Sales VP in the Corporate Support Apps

When I update the Opportunity (RT: Opportunity Growth) field “Client Questionnaire Status” = “Info Due Process Ready”

Then the system shall automatically create the following **Task record(s)**: Note: if there are more that one team member with the same Deal Team role, then create tasks for each team member within that role. 

1. Subject: Update Service Provider Rate Market Analysis

## Assigned To = Deal Team Role: Recruiting Manager

## Comments = On the Opportunity, please attach a copy of the ’Service Provider Rate Market Analysis'  & update following field within the “Info Due” section: “Service Provider Rate Market Analysis - Loaded”

## Related To = <Opportunity>

1. Subject: Start BD Finance Activities

## Assigned To = Deal Team Role: BD Finance

## Comments = On the Opportunity, please confirm finance is beginning proforma activities & update following field within the “Info Due” section: “BD Finance Activities Begun”

## Related To = <Opportunity>

1. Subject: Proposal / RFP Documents Started

## Assigned To = Deal Team Role: BD Enablement

## Comments = On the Opportunity, once you have received details required to kickoff RFP/Proposal/Presentation activities please update following field within the “Info Due” section: “Proposal / RFP Documents Started”

## Related To = <Opportunity>

And all tasks are populated with:

1. Record Type = Default

1. Status = Open

1. Priority = Normal

1. Type = Info Due Process (NEW) 

1. Due Date = <date from field = “INFO DUE Date for All Inputs”>

AC3: Auto create Info Due Tasks - Services level

Given I am a Sales VP in the Corporate Support Apps

When I update the Opportunity (RT: Opportunity Growth) field “Client Questionnaire Status” = “Info Due Process Ready”

Then the system shall automatically create **one Task record per Services (status = Active)**: Note: if there are more that one team member with the same Deal Team role, then create tasks for each team member within that role. 

(example: if there are 5 active services w/one person per Deal Team Role, then I expect 10 tasks)

1. Subject: Update Service Line Coverage Model & Approve Rate(s)

## Assigned To = Deal Team Role: Chief Medical Officer  EM, Chief Medical Officer  HM, or Hybrid Care Leader

### Assign based on the Services record:

#### Role = Chief Medical Officer - EM

####* When the Service Line’s Parent field = EM or UC

#### Role = Chief Medical Officer - HM

####* When the Service Line’s Parent field = HM

#### Role = Hybrid Care Leader

####* When the Service Line’s Parent field = VH or CCARE

## Comments = On the Services record, please review the recommended rates provided by recruiting & adjust as necessary.  Then update the following fields within the “Info Due” section: “Service Line Coverage Model”, “Coverage Model Completed By”, & “Field Operations Leadership - Rate Approval”

## Related To = <Services>

1. Subject: Update Clinical Rates - Recommended Rate(s)

## Assigned To = Deal Team Role: Recruiting Manager

## Comments = On the Services record, please update the following fields with your recommended clinical rates to be reviewed & approved by CMO: “Info Due” section: “MD/DO W2 Rates  Recommended”, “NPPA W2 Rates  Recommended”, “CPM Rate  Recommended”, “HPS Rate  Recommended”, “Sign-on bonus/Retention Bonuses”, & “Medical Director Stipend”

## Related To = <Services>

And all tasks are populated with:

1. Record Type = Default

1. Status = Open

1. Priority = Normal

1. Type = Info Due Process (NEW) 

1. Due Date = <date from field = “INFO DUE Date for All Inputs”>

NOTE: User(s) to manually close task once complete (Status = Completed)
