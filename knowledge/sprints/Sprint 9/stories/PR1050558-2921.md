## Description

1Login to Corp HC org As MSLCPA - Gloria Gilbert.

2-Navigate to Cases tab> Open Case record (RT= Collaborative Practice Agreement Review) & Click on Review CPA tab.

3-Confirm CPA records are being display on under the tab.

Max record on page : 20

4- Select checkbox against required record and update the value for Relationship Status” & “Supervision/Collaboration Status” field at the bottom and click 'Save' button

Issue - The Filter is not present on Facility and alongwith it when doing the mass update the “Relationship Status” & “Supervision/Collaboration Status”  not getting updated.

Expected Result - The The Filter should be present on Facility and along with it when doing the mass update the “Relationship Status” & “Supervision/Collaboration Status”  should be updated.

Actual Result - The Filter is not present on Facility and alongwith it when doing the mass update the “Relationship Status” & “Supervision/Collaboration Status”  not getting updated.

## Root Cause

**Filter Missing on Facility**

The Facility filter was missed in the original user story **US 302** during requirement analysis and implementation. It was not carried over into the configuration.

**Mass Update Failure**

The issue was not with the update logic itself. Records failed to update due to **manual data setup in QA**. Some records did not meet **validation rule conditions**, which caused the update to be blocked for those records.
