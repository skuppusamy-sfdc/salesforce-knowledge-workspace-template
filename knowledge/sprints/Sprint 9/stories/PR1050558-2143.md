## Description

As a Sales VP, I want to include fields need for our Info Due Process within our Opportunity and Services objects, so we have place to capture the information needed.

NOTE: Information from the Info Due Process is used to support the creation of the Pro Forma (Excel), which is then loaded into Salesforce as Pro Forma Model and Pro Forma Facility Detail records on the Opportunity record.

## Acceptance Criteria

AC: **Opportunity** fields for Info Due

Given I am Sales VP in the Corporate Support App

When I am on an Opportunity (RT: Growth Opportunity) record

Then on the Details tab I can see new section = “Info Due”, located under the “War Room Details” section with the following fields: (see data dictionary for additional info)

|Questions Due Back To Client (RFP/RFI)|Date|

|INFO DUE Date for All Inputs|Date|

|Targeted DAM Date|Display Opportunity's “DAM Date”|

|Proposal Due Date|Display Opportunity's “Original Proposal Due Date”|

|Program Transition/Start Date (Est)|Display Opportunity's “Target Contract Start Date”|

|Program Transition/Start Time (Est)|Time|

|Service Provider Rate Market Analysis

Help Text: Service Provider Rate Market Analysis has been loaded|Checkbox|

|BD Finance Activities Begun

Help Text: Volume, Reimbursement, etc|Checkbox|

|Proposal / RFP Documents Started|Checkbox|

AC: **Services** fields for Info Due

Given I am Sales VP in the Corporate Support App

When I am on an Services record

Then on the Details tab I can see new section = “Info Due”, located under the “Pro Forma Details” section with the following fields: (see data dictionary for additional info)

|Service Line Coverage Model|Text Area (Rich) (131,072)|

|Coverage Model Completed By|Lookup to User|

|MD/DO W2 Rates - Recommended|Currency (16,2)|

|NPPA W2 Rates - Recommended|Currency (16,2)|

|CPM Rate - Recommended|Currency (16,2)|

|HPS Rate - Recommended|Currency (16,2)|
