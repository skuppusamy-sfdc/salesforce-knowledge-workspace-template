*** Answer each question below per facility (when HPF status is not Prospect or Inactive AND Employment Type is Locums, **Leased**, or **Managed*):

*** Date Termination Received (date, required)

**** Help Text: date termination request was received

*** Termination Request Date (date, automatically captured)

**** Help Text: date termination request was submitted or automated

*** Last Day (date, required)

**** Help Text: Agreement termination date

*** Voluntary Termination (checkbox, optional)

*** Reason for Termination (required, same list regardless if Voluntary Termination is checked or not)

***** picklist values:  **Agreement Not Returned, Coverage Discontinued, Deceased, Hospital App Not Returned, Hospital Contract Termination, Insurance Expired, Past Issues with Facility, PEP Not Returned, Per Hospital, Per Provider. Per Risk Manager, Privilege Expiration*

*** Ability to attach files

## Solution

In the existing Clinician offboarding OS,

As part of the condition/filter check on Employee type for Facility table,

Remove Prospect (IF HPF status != Inactive) and include employee type Leased or Managed.

Create a separate picklist field with the options mentioned above for the emp type = Leased or Managed ( conditional visibility)
