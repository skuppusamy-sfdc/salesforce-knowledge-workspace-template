## Description

As a SFO (Staffing Fulfillment Optimization) user, I want the ability to add/update rates based on Time Type, so that I can specify Shift rates by Type (Shift), Facility Title, Unit of Measure, and Time Type combination.

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a SFO in the Corporate Support or Corporate Support Console app

When I navigate to a **Service Account** record (Account record type = Service Account) 

And I clicked Specify Rates button

And I selected Type of Rate = Shift

And I selected Shift Type = NP/PA

And I selected Employment Type = Employed

Then:

** The system will create a **new Rate* if the a matching record is NOT found

** The system will **update a Rate* if a matching record IS found

And Match defined as same:

- Contract Payment Agreement: Type of Rate

** Preventive Care Agreement: Shift, Facility Title, Unit of Measure, & **Time Type*
