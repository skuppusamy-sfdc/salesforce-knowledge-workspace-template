## Description

As a Medical Staff Liaison, I need to terminate a clinician’s employment at a specific facility in Salesforce, so that I can accurately update the status of employees at facilities that SCP staffs for

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a Medical Staff Liaison in the Corporate Support apps

When I view a **Clinician** record

Then I am able to select Initiate Termination Request and see the following: 

- Facility Selection  [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558701|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558701|smartlink] 

- Termination Details

** Answer each question below per facility (when HPF status is not Prospect or Inactive AND Employment Type is Locums):

*** Last Day (date, required)

**** Help Text: agreed Agreement termination date

*** Date Termination Received (date, required)

**** Help Text: date termination request was received

*** Termination Request Date (date, automatically captured)

**** Help Text: date termination request was submitted or automated

*** Voluntary Termination (checkbox, optional)

*** Reason for Termination (picklist dependent on Voluntary Termination, required when condition met)

**** picklist values to be provided - must align with Workday and Asymbl (why an open req was created due to clinician exit)

*** Ability to attach files

## Solution

On the omniscript (used in story [701|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558-701]), after the HPF are selected,

Check if the selected HPF match the criteria

status is not Prospect or Inactive AND Employment Type is Locums

Then display the fields mentioned above in a table format for each of the HPF ( row)

Map the details to Case (of record type Clinician offboarding) (This already exists as part of [306|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558-306])

On Termination Details step, display the following fields: (upon clicking edit, then user will be present all editable fields listed in the story.  The initial display is just displaying common fields between this story and 306)

- Name

- Status

- Last Day 

- Date Termination Received
