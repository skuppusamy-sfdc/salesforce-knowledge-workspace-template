## Description

As a Recruiter, I need to specify Anticipated Clinical Hours per HPF, which should rollup to the Clinician’s Contract, so that the information can be used for the Contract creation within Conga.

## Acceptance Criteria

AC1: Add “**Anticipated Clinical Hours**”

Given I am a Recruiter in the Corporate Support apps

When I navigate to a **clinician contract** record where Status = **Draft**

And I am on the Summary Tab

Then I have the ability to capture “**Anticipated Clinical Hours**” for each HPF

- Anticipated Clinical Hours (optional, number (3,0))

And if Anticipated Clinical Hours are captured, then user should have visibility to see the amount of hours captured

- Format: “Anticipated Clinical Hours: <#>”

AC2: Prevent adding “**Anticipated Clinical Hours**”

Given I am a Recruiter in the Corporate Support apps

When I navigate to a **clinician contract** record where Status = **Draft**

And I am on the Summary Tab

And an HPF is grayed out (Removed by using “Add HPF”, see story [+PR10505581389+|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505581389]) 

Then the user should **NOT** be able to capture “**Anticipated Clinical Hours**” for that HPF

-AC3: RollUp Summary of “**Anticipated Clinical Hours**” on the Clinician Contract. [Split to Story [+PR10505582947+|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505582947]]

-Given I am a Recruiter in the Corporate Support apps-

-When I navigate to a **clinician contract** record-

-And I have captured “**Anticipated Clinical Hours**” for HPF(s)-

-Then on the Clinician contract record, I can see:- 

** **Anticipated Clinical Hours* (read only, rollup summary: sum of the “Anticipated Clinical Hours” captured per HPF/Provide Network Contract)-

## Solution

capture when HPF is selected (create contract & Add HPF) - map to HPF.Anticipated_Hours__c

Create the field if its not already added to HPF. Add to layout.

To edit the added rates, As part of Configure Rates, on the first screen, before Rate type, Display the Anticipated Hours  prepopulated, with filled rate with option to edit.
