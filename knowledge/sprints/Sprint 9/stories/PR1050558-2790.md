## Description

As a SFO (Staffing Fulfillment Optimization) user, I want the ability to capture "Stipend Tier" & "Admin Hours" when creating  Stipend facility rates, so that we have clearly defined rates for each Service Account.

## Acceptance Criteria

AC1: Add "Stipend Tier" & "Admin Hours"

Given I am a SFO in the Corporate Support or Corporate Support Console app

When I navigate to a **Contract** record (record type = **Practice Operations**)

And Contract Status = Draft

And I am on the Summary tab

And I selected the **Specify Rates** button

And selected Type of Rate = **Stipend**

Then on the Rates Details step, I shall have the ability to capture the following:

- Type (Existing field, no change)

- Facility Title (Existing field, no change)

- Rate (Existing field, no change)

- Unit of Measure (Existing field, no change)

** **Stipend Tier (NEW field, required only in guided experience)*

*** **Required, Picklist (single select): Tier 1, Tier 2 , Tier 3, Tier 4, Legacy, Flat, NPPA*

** **Admin Hours (NEW field, required only in guided experience)*

*** **Required, number (3, 0)*

** Error message: Please enter a value with no more than 3 digits and 0 decimal places

- Override Prohibited (Existing field, no change)

- Notes (Existing field, no change)

And available navigation options 

- Cancel (Existing functionality, no change) 

- Previous (Existing functionality, no change) 

- Finish (Update functionality, changes listed below)

** Update Preventive Care Agreement

**** **Stipend Tier = values selected by user*

**** **Admin Hours = value inputted by user*

## Solution

Add new Preventive Care Agreement fields:

** **Stipend Tier (NEW field)*

*** **Required, Picklist (single select): Tier 1, Tier 2 , Tier 3, Tier 4, Legacy, Flat,  NPPA*

** **Admin Hours (NEW field)*

*** **Required, number (3, 0)*
