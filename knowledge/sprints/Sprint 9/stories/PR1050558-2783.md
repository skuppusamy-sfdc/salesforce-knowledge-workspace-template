## Description

**Description:**

Corporate Support team members can access the **Inactive HPF report**, but required fields and filters are **not visible** due to missing field-level security (FLS) permissions. This impacts visibility of key report data such as Termination Reason, Last Date Worked, Hospital Privileged Date, etc.

**Preconditions:**

- User is logged in as a Corporate Support team member.

- Inactive HPF report is available in Salesforce.

**Steps to Reproduce:**

1. Log in as a Corporate Support team member.

1. Navigate to Reports → Open **Inactive HPF Report**.

1. Review the displayed columns and filters.

**Actual Result:**

- Report opens successfully, but several fields/filters are not displayed (e.g., Termination Reason, Last Date Worked, Hospital Privileged Date, Company Signed Date).

**Expected Result:**

- All required fields and filters defined in the report should be visible to Corporate Support users.
