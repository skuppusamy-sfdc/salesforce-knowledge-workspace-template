## Description

**Description:** The "For additional information about the Company’s data privacy practices, please review our Privacy Policy" URL in the CCPA notice on the Core Connect portal appears as a disabled or unlinked element with a grey color. This is inconsistent with the attached Intake Form document, where the same URL is a standard clickable link with a blue color. This UI inconsistency may lead users to believe the link is not functional or active.

**Steps to Reproduce:**

1. Log in to the Core Connect portal as a Clinician.

1. Navigate to the Intake Form case.

1. Locate the CCPA Notice section.

1. Observe the color of the privacy policy URL.

**Expected Result:** The URL should be blue, consistent with the attached Intake Form document, and should be an active, clickable link.
