## Description

As a Sales VP, when I capture the Client Discovery Questionnaire, I want the ability to capture and track, so can easily see where we are in the client questionnaire review process and have access to the Client Discovery Questionnaire in one location.

## Acceptance Criteria

AC1: Ability to **Add Client Discovery Questionnaire file & update status**

Given I am a Sales VP within the Corporate Support Apps

When I receive a new Client Discovery Questionnaire

And navigated to the Opportunity (RT: Opportunity Growth) record

Then from the Related tab (right region), related list = **Files** (related list added in story [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558310|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558310|smart-link] )

I have the ability to add a new Client Discovery Questionnaire file 

And I have the ability to manually update field **“Client Questionnaire Status” from “Not Received**”(default) to  **“Assessment Submitted”**

AC2: Ability to update status to **Ready for Review, Info Due Process Ready**, or **Submission Incomplete**

Given I am a Sales VP within the Corporate Support Apps

When I navigate to a Opportunity (RT: Opportunity Growth) record with field “Client Questionnaire Status ” = Assessment Submitted

And I reviewed the Client Discovery Questionnaire file

Than I manually determine if the data is complete:

** If yes, then I am able to update the “Client Questionnaire Status” to “**Ready for Review**” or “**Info Due Process Ready*”

** If no, then I am able to update the “Client Questionnaire Status” to “**Submission Incomplete*”

AC3: Ability to **view** **all Ready for Review** 

Given I am a user within the Corporate Support Apps that have access to Opportunity

When I navigate to the Opportunity tab

Then in list view = Ready for Review

- Filter: Record Type = Opportunity Growth, Client Questionnaire Status = Ready for Review

- Columns: Account Name, Opportunity Name, Target Contract Start Date, Closed Date, Count of All Services
