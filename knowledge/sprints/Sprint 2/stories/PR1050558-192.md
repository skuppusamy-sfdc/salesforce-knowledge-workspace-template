## Description

As a Recruiter, I need to enter NPIs for clinicians in one spot, so that identifiers are accurately maintained and stored in Salesforce

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a Recruiter in the Corporate Support or Corporate Support Console app

When I navigate to a clinician record

Then I can select Update NPI and see the following: 

- Clinician NPI

** Field to enter or update NPI for the clinician

** Input must be 10 digits

** IF **matching NPI is found*

** Display the clinician record with the matching NPI value and display the following message: “There is an existing clinician record with this NPI: [Full Name]. Please resolve the duplicate before moving forward.”

** Do not save the NPI update

** IF validation through NPPES returns **invalid NPI*

** Display the following message: “[NPI] was not found in the NPPES database”

** Do not save the NPI update

** IF **validation through NPPES is successful*

** Display the existing Clinician data and the NPPES output side by side

*** FirstName, MiddleName, Last Name, NPI, Healthcare Provider Taxonomy, Board Certification

*** All these data will be displayed read only

** Checkbox to “Update clinician record?”

*** Help text: “If left unchecked, this data will not sync to the clinician record”

*** User cannot pick and choose what they what. Its either full synch or no synch.

*** Irrespective of they they choose NPI should be updated in the Record

** If the checkbox is checked, update the clinician’s NPI and create/update new records for any other attributes from the NPPES output

** If the checkbox is not checked, do not save the NPI or other updates

Given I am a Recruiter in the Corporate Support or Corporate Support Console app

When I navigate to clinician person account 

Then I am **not able to update the NPI field in the page layout**

And I do not see **Healthcare Provider NPI records** in the Related tab

Given I am a Recruiter in the Corporate Support or Corporate Support Console app

When I navigate to clinician person account and the Details tab

Then I am able to see the **NPI field displayed without commas**

Given I am a Recruiter in the Corporate Support or Corporate Support Console app

When I navigate to a Healthcare Provider NPI record

Then I am able to see the Details and Related tabs (Details is the default tab)

And the **Healthcare Provider NPI History related list in the Related tab**

Given I am a Recruiter logged in to the Corporate Support app

When I use the “Update NPI” wizard

Then I do not have the option to Save for Later

Given I am a Recruiter logged in to the Corporate Support app

When I navigate to a clinician record

Then I am able to delete Business License, Healthcare Provider Specialty, Healthcare Provider Taxonomy, Board Certification, Person Education, and Person Employment records as needed

## Solution

OmniScript

- When NPI field on Contact is updated

- Check if Healthcare Provider NPI record exists for this contact

** If a record exists: update NPI field on Healthcare Provider NPI record with the value from the NPI contact field

** If a record does not exist: create a Healthcare Provider NPI record with the following

*** NPI field on Healthcare Provider NPI record = NPI field on Contact

*** Practitioner field on Healthcare Provider NPI record = Contact record

*** NPI Type field on Healthcare Provider NPI record = Individual

*** Name field on Healthcare Provider NPI record = [Contact Last Name] NPI

*** Owner field on Healthcare Provider NPI record = Record Creator

*** NPPES Output = what returns from NPPES database

-Update object permissions on Manage Clinician Identifiers, Credentials permission set: read only for Healthcare Provider NPI- 

Remove the Healthcare Provider NPI from the Related List

Enable history tracking on Healthcare Provider NPI object for the fields on the data dictionary

Update NPI field on Contact object from number to text & create validation rule that only 10 digit numbers are able to be entered

Fields to Populate from NPPES Registry:

`results.basic.first_name = FIRS TNAME`

`results.basic.last_name = LAST NAME`

`results.basic.middle_name = MIDDLE NAME`

`results.taxonomies.desc = Taxonomy (Can have multiple taxonomies record and use them pick all of them - only record should exist no duplicate)`

Use the description for the Taxonomies Name.  

`results.basic.certification_date = Board Certified (Create a Board certification Record with Status = Certified One - only record should exist no duplicate)`
