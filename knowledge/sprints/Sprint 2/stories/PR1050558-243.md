## Description

**As a** Salesforce Administrator, **I want to** configure a dedicated Salesforce External Client Apps for MuleSoft integration, **so that** MuleSoft can securely authenticate and interact with Salesforce data via API calls, adhering to the principle of least privilege and maintaining a clear audit trail.

## Acceptance Criteria

** **Connected App Creation:*

** The Salesforce Administrator can successfully create a new Connected App in Salesforce Setup.

*** The Connected App is named **Mulesoft Integration*

** **OAuth 2.0 JWT Bearer Flow Configuration:*

** OAuth settings are enabled for the Connected App.

** A digital signature (X.509 certificate) generated and uploaded to the Connected App matches the private key securely held by the MuleSoft application.

** The `Permitted Users` setting is configured as `Admin approved users are pre-authorized`.

** **OAuth Scopes (Principle of Least Privilege):*

** The Connected App has _only_ the minimum necessary OAuth scopes selected  `api`, `refresh_token, offline_access`

** **User & Permission Management:*

*** A dedicated Salesforce integration user **Integration Mulesoft* created for MuleSoft to assert its identity.
