Steps to Reproduce:

1. Login to Corporate Support app.

1. Navigate to Contacts tab and click on Create Clinician button

1. Fill the fields match with existing record

1. Click on Next button

1. Select both existing checkbox and New Clinician Creation checkbox.

1. Click on Next button

1. Verify the screen

**Expected Result:** Next button should be disabled or rename when selecting existing record and New Clinician Creation checkbox.

**Actual Result:** Showing Next button is enabled.
