## Description

As a Recruiter, I need a guided experience to create clinician records in Salesforce, so that I can quickly create a new prospect

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a Recruiter logged in to the Corporate Support app

When I navigate to the Contacts tab and click “Create Clinician”

Then I am able to populate the following fields:

- Clinician Information

*** Name (Salutation, **FirstName**, MiddleName, **LastName**, Suffix) _**First and Last are Required_

*** Professional Title _**Required_

** Email 

** Mobile

*** _Either Email OR Mobile are required_

- Additional Information

** Practicing Specialty

*** Help Text = 

The specialty in which a clinician is formally trained and, board certified (if applicable).

Example: The clinician is residency-trained and board certified in Family Medicine.

** Primary Specialty

*** The specialty in which a clinician is currently providing clinical care, regardless of board certification or formal training background.

Example: The clinician is residency-trained and board certified in Family Medicine, but they work in an Emergency Medicine role.

** Taxonomy

** Board Certified?

** Medical License

** Lead Source

** Geographic Preference

** Comments

If the **Email or Mobile fields match an existing clinician record**, display the following message: “There is an existing clinician record with this +Email+/+Mobile+/+Email and Mobile+: [Full Name]. Display the matching duplicate record (max 10) sorted by last  updated and provide navigation to the individual duplicate records for user”

And when I select Next, I am brought to the newly created clinician person account

Given I am a Recruiter logged in to the Corporate Support app

When I use the “Create Clinician” wizard

Then I do not have the option to Save for Later

## Solution

**field mappings**:

- Name (Salutation, FirstName, MiddleName, LastName, Suffix) _PERSON ACCOUNT_

- Professional Title _PERSON ACCOUNT_

- NPI _PERSON ACCOUNT_

** triggers creation of Healthcare Provider NPI record and task (if necessary) as described in [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558192|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558192|smart-link] 

- Email _PERSON ACCOUNT_

- Mobile _PERSON ACCOUNT_

- Practicing Specialty _HEALTHCARE PROVIDER SPECIALTY - sub specialty field = checked_

- Primary Specialty _HEALTHCARE PROVIDER SPECIALTY - primary board specialty field = checked_

- Taxonomy _HEALTHCARE PROVIDER TAXONOMY - primary taxonomy = checked_

- Board Certified? _BOARD CERTIFICATION - status = Certified, if the Board Certification field? is checked_

- Medical License _BUSINESS LICENSE - type = Business License, Jurisdiction Type = State, Jurisdiction State = picklist in flow_

- Lead Source _PERSON ACCOUNT_

- Geographic Preference _PERSON ACCOUNT_

- Comments _PERSON ACCOUNT_

**check for duplicates based on email and mobile**
