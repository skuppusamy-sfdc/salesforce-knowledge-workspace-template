## Description

As a Recruiter, I need to use a guided wizard for my screening calls with prospects, so that I can quickly update prospect data and have prompts for items to cover on the call

## Acceptance Criteria

Given that I am a recruiter on the Corporate Support app

When I open a clinician record and click the “Create HPF & Job Association” button

Then I am able to see the following: 

1. IF this clinician record does not have the NPI field populated

#* Clinician NPI

#** Same ability to enter and validate NPI as specified in [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558192|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558192|smart-link] 

1. IF this clinician record have the NPI field populated

#* Clinician Information

#** Full Name (view only)

#** Professional Title

#** Practicing Specialty

#*** Help Text = 

The specialty in which a clinician is formally trained and, board certified (if applicable).

Example: The clinician is residency-trained and board certified in Family Medicine.

#** Primary Specialty

#*** Help Text = 

The specialty in which a clinician is currently providing clinical care, regardless of board certification or formal training background.

Example: The clinician is residency-trained and board certified in Family Medicine, but they work in an Emergency Medicine role.

#** Practicing Preference

#*** Help Text =

The specialty or clinical setting the clinician prefers to work in, regardless of current or roles.

Example: The clinician is residency-trained and board certified in Family Medicine and they work in an Emergency Medicine role currently. However, they have express interested in working in an Urgent Care setting.

#** Medical License

#** Taxonomy

#** Board Certified?

#** Professional Status

#*** Program (display & required when Professional Status = Resident OR Student)

#*** End Date (display & required when Professional Status = Resident OR Student)

#** Sponsorship Need? 

#*** Visa Type (display when Sponsorship Need? = Yes) 

#* Notes

#** Comments

NPI & Facility selection built in [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558209|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558209|smart-link] 

Then when I select save, the populated fields are updated (regardless of whether all the field requirements were met).

## Solution

Use a Salesforce Flow

**field mappings**:

- Professional Title _PERSON ACCOUNT_

- Practicing Specialty _HEALTHCARE PROVIDER SPECIALTY - sub specialty field = checked_

- Primary Specialty _HEALTHCARE PROVIDER SPECIALTY - primary board specialty field = checked_

- Practicing Preference _PERSON ACCOUNT_

- Taxonomy _HEALTHCARE PROVIDER TAXONOMY - primary taxonomy = checked_

- Board Certification? _BOARD CERTIFICATION - status = Certified, if the Board Certification field? is checked_

- Professional Status _PERSON ACCOUNT_

- Program _PERSON EDUCATION_

- End Date _PERSON EDUCATION_

- Sponsorship Need? _PERSON ACCOUNT_

- Visa Type _VISA_

- Comments _PERSON ACCOUNT_
