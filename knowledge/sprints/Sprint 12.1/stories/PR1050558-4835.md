## Description

### **Description:**

During testing of the SCP Offboarding automation, the **Last Day** field—expected to auto-populate with the latest “Last Day” value from the associated Facility Offboarding cases—is **not visible in the UI**.

As a result, users cannot confirm that the value has been correctly calculated or updated, even though it may exist in the backend.

### **Steps to Reproduce:**

1. Log in as **MSL user**.

1. Navigate to **Corporate Support Apps → Clinician record**.

1. Create a **Termination Request** with **Full SCP Offboarding = Yes**.

1. Ensure linked **Facility Offboarding cases** exist with valid _Last Day_ values.

1. Complete all **SCP Offboarding tasks** to close the SCP Offboarding case.

1. Open the closed SCP Offboarding case record.

1. Attempt to view the **Last Day** field on the page layout.

### **Actual Result:**

** The **Last Day** field is **not visible* on the SCP Offboarding case UI 

### **Expected Result:**

** The **Last Day** field should be **visible in the SCP Offboarding case UI**, either on the **details layout** or the **highlights panel*, to allow users to verify the populated value without backend access.
