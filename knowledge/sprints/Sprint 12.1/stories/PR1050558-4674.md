## Description

As a HR team member, I need accurate Hire Dates in Workday for clinicians, so that I can ensure clinicians are paid and receive benefits according to their employment type

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a HR team member in the Corporate Support apps

When I navigate to a clinician record where:

- They have a Contract in Executed status with a previously Executed Contract of a different employment type (ANY employment type change qualifies)

Then their hire date is Contract Start Date 

And integration sends information to Workday on:

- If First Clinical Shift was blank: when First Clinical Shift is populated (normal process) 

- If First Clinical Shift was populated: when the new Contract is Executed (new integration) 

_Contract Start Date is also known as Effective Date_

## Solution

Update a record triggered flow for Contract when Contract is Record is created (Fast Field Update)

Check if the Contract has any Executed Contract with Different Type

Query: Select Count(Id) from Contract where Contract.AccountId = NewContract.AccountId AND Contract.RecordType = Clinician

AND Contract.EmploymentType != (NewContract.EmploymentType) AND Contract.Status = Executed **AND StartDate < NewContract.StartDate**

When Count > 0 then Update the newContract.Hire_Date__c = newContract.StartDate
