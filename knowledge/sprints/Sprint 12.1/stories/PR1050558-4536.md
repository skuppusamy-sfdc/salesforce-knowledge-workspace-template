## Description

Need to update flow = Welcome Clinician Onboarding Email to handle if the Clinician Email is Null.

Expected Results:

1. If Clinician’s Email is BLANK or NULL, then no SCP Welcome Onboarding email is sent

1. Also, if the the Clinician’s Email is present (NOT BLANK or NULL) and the CES is not present on the Account Team, then we should be sending the email to the Clinician using the generic CES information provided in story 1723.

## Solution

To Address for the Email should be 

Contract.Email

1. Update flow = Welcome Clinician Onboarding Email Flow to handle if Clinician’s email is blank or NULL

1. Remove the condition to check if the Clinician Email is NOT present under “Check if CES is Present”

## This should not be here, we already checked for the Clinician’s email higher in the flow and also the condition was written incorrectly in general.

## Root Cause

When a clinician record with a **null or blank email** field entered the flow, the system attempted to execute the "Send Email" action, resulting in flow failure notifications.
