## Description

As a Legal team member, when I am on a Client Contract, I want the ability to Terminate, so that I can indicate that a contract status is Terminated.

## Acceptance Criteria

AC: Ability to ‘Terminate Contract'

Given I am a Legal Team Member within the Corporate Support Apps

When navigate to a Contract (RT: Client Contract) record

And Contract Type = Master

Then I have the ability to select the ‘Terminate Contract’ guided experience

And available navigation options:

- Cancel (cancel is via the “X” in upper right)

** No updates are made

** Guided Experience is closed

** User is returned to the Client Contract

- Finish/Save

** Guided Experience is closed

** User is returned to the Client Contract

** And the system shall make the following updates:

*** Updates “Status” = “Terminated”

## Solution

New Button in Contract RT: Client Contract AND Contract_Type__c = “Master” (as part of this story create the Ultiamte_Parent_Id__c field)

On Button Click (Terminate Contract)

Update Current Record

1. Contract.Status = “Terminated”
