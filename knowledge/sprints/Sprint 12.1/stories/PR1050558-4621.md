## Description

**Tester:** Nicole Fontenot

**Role:** Legal - Client Contracting

**Test Case ID:** CTERM-001

**Description:**

"When executing the process "Begin Facility Termination" it was noted that the

Active and Contract Active checkboxes were not checked, and the user was still able to initiate the process. Need to ensure that there is validation in place that both checkboxes are checked in order for a user to "Begin Facility Termination""

**Record Link:**

None provided

**Steps to Reproduce:**

1. From the Corporate Support Console, choose Accounts and navigate to the Service Account that needs a Facility Term

2. Click on Contracts

3. Click on the down arrow next to "Recently Viewed" and choose the "All Client Contracts" list view

4. Find the Contract you'd like to Term (Contract must be a Client Contract in Status = Executed) and click on the Contract Number

5. From the buttons in the header, click on "Begin Facility Termination"

6. Choose at least 1 Service Account and click "Next"

7. For each Service Account chosen, click the down arrow and choose Edit

8. Fill in the required fields - with Termination Date = tomorrow's date and click "Save"

9. Review changes and click "Save""

**Expected vs. Actual Results:**

Under the Expected Results in the test case, a related Services Account is displayed in the guided workflow. In the test itself, If the checkboxes were not checked, why did the system display the related service account? 

Want to ensure that there are checks/validation in place and the system is displaying the related Service Account based on a specific set of rules. The user (or tester) should have received an error because those checkboxes were not checked.

## Acceptance Criteria

This Enhancement makes updates original story [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558317|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558317|smart-link] by adding a check that the ‘Contract Active’ checkbox is checked for a Service Account to be selected within the "Begin Facility Termination" guided experience.

AC1: updates to existing process in blue, all other existing processes remain the same. 

Given I am a legal team member in the Corporate Support apps

When I navigate to a Contract (RT: Client Contract) record

And Contract Status = Executed

Then I am able to select “Begin Facility Termination” from the header

And the user is only allowed to proceed with Service Account(s) where ‘Contract Active’ = True/Checked.

And if there are no Service Accounts with where ‘Contract Active’ = True/Checked, then display the following message: No active Service Accounts available.

And user can not proceed with the "Begin Facility Termination" guided experience, user must ‘Cancel’ to exit the process. 

## Solution

Adjust the Client Omni Script to put in the additional filter based on AC
