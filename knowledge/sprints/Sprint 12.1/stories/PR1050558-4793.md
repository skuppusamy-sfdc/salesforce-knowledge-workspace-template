## Description

As a Recruiter, on the Summary Tab of a Clinician contract I should only see unique clinician rates displayed, so that I do not see both Facility/Client rates and the Clinician Rates as I do when I am on the “Rate Details” step of the “Configure Rates” guided experience.

## Acceptance Criteria

AC1:

Given I am a Recruiter within the Corporate Support apps

When I am on a Clinician Contract in Status = Draft

And I clicked “Configure Rates” guided experience

Then I shall not see duplicate rates on the following:

- On the ‘Rate Details’ step, I shall not see duplicates rates, I shall only see unique rates.  I shall see either:

** Source = “Existing Clinician Rate” if the rate has been added to the clinician contract or

** Source = “Facility Rate” if rate has not been added to the clinician contract, only exists on the Practice Operations/Facility contract. 

- On the Summary tab, I shall only see the unique Clinician Rates, I shall not see duplicate rates, I shall only see unique rates that have been selected for the Clinician.
