## Description

As a MSL, I need to see the Assigned To column in Task related lists, so that I can easily understand who is responsible for a specific task record

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a user in the Corporate Support apps

When I navigate to a **clinician person account record**

And I open the Activities tab

Then I am able to see the Open Activities & Activity History related lists with the following columns: Subject, Assigned To, Status, Due Date

Given I am a user in the Corporate Support apps

When I navigate to a **onboarding case record**

And I open the Activities tab

Then I am able to see the Open Activities & **Activity History** related lists with the following columns: Subject, Assigned To, Status, Due Date

Given I am a user in the Corporate Support apps

When I navigate to a **offboarding case record**

And I open the Activities tab

Then I am able to see the Open Activities & **Activity History** related lists with the following columns: Subject, Assigned To, Status, Due Date
