## Description

**Steps to reproduce:**

1. Login as Contracting Specialist - Scott Fitzgerald

1. Open a clinician contract in ‘Draft’ status with a HPF (linked to a service account with a PO contract in Executed status)

1. Verify if the user can see the rates of the corresponding facility title from the executed PO contract in the 'Facility Rates as Reference' component

**Expected Result:**

User should only be able to view the PO contract rates in the 'Facility Rates as Reference' component

**Actual Result:**

User is able to delete the PO contract rates using the delete icon in the 'Facility Rates as Reference' component

## Acceptance Criteria

Observation bug, converted to an enhancement story to ensure that Corporate Support User who have access to Clinician Contracts can NOT delete rates within the 'Facility Rates as Reference' component on clinician contract.

AC1:

Given I am a Recruiter or Clinician Contracting Specialist within the Corporate Support Apps

When I am on a Clinician Contract

And I am on the Summary tab

And I click on facility/HPF 

And the system displays the Practice Operations rates ([+PR10505584863+|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505584863]) for specific Facility Title

Then the system shall NOT be allowed the user to delete any rates; this component should be read only as reference to the user.
