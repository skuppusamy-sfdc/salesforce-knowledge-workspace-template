**Description:**

For both Letter of Intent and Client Contract details, any Legal - Client Contracting persona needs edit functionality for all fields no matter the status.

## Acceptance Criteria

Need to be able to edit the fields on the details and configuration tabs. 

AC1: Letter of Intent Contract

Given I am a Legal Team Member within the Corporate Support Apps

When I select a **Letter of Intent** contract record

Then regardless of the contract ‘Status’ I can edit fields on the **Details** tab (updates original story 3464)

- Exceptions, the following are read only:

** Contract Number - this is an auto number

** Status - updated via the actions or updating specific Key Date fields (“Date Sent For Internal Review” or “Other Party Sent Date“, initial update of theses field update the status to ‘Submitted' and sets the 'Category’ to Internal or External.

** Source Opportunity - set by the system upon creation or amend for traceability

** Outstanding Days - formula field

** Activated Date - populated by Action = “Activate Contract”

** Activated By - populated by Action = “Activate Contract”

** Total Process Days - formula field

** System Information fields are not editable.
