**Description:** 

HPF Effective from date: When provider can start working/contract is considered active; must align with contract effective date

Contract Effective date: must be equal or greater than the HPF Eff date

SFDC Hire Date: derived from admin date or first clinical shift; for leadership update with FCS once clinician begins working - Working dates stay the same

WD Position Availability/Earliest Hire: Should always equal the SF Hire Date

Additional note: Start admin work before clinical work flag is checked and recruiter enters Administrative date, Adminstrative date and contract effective date cannot be before the HPF from date; need something in place to block this from happening. Also, if clinician starts admin work before clinical work, the SF hire date will eventually be updated with their FCS date, but nothing in Workday changes.

As a Clinician Contract Specialist, I need HPF and Contract Dates in alignment, so that clinicians are contracted and paid appropriately

## Acceptance Criteria

+AC1: Clinician Contracts can only be requested within HPF Dates+

Given I am a Recruiter in the Corporate Support apps

When I request a contract for a clinician

Then I am only able to select HPFs where the HPF Effective From is on or after the Start Date of the Contract

_Example:_ 

Hiring EE clinician

HPF 1 Effective From `20260115` (from Asymbl process)

Contract needs to start `20260201`

HPF 1 won’t be available to select, **Recruiter needs to ask Data Quality to** adjust HPF date to `20260201` and adjust/request the contract

+AC2: Prevent Users Except Admins and Data Quality from Editing HPF Dates+

Given I am a System Administrator (IT Permission Set Group) or Data Quality team member (Data Quality Permission Set Group) 

When I navigate to a Healthcare Practitioner Facility record

Then I am able to edit the Effective From and Effective To fields

Given I am NOT a System Administrator or Data Quality team member

When I navigate to a Healthcare Practitioner Facility record

Then I am unable to edit the Effective From and Effective To fields

+AC3: Clinician Contracting Specialist Can Adjust Contract and HPF Dates+

Given I am a Clinician Contracting Specialist (Clinician Contracting Permission Set Group)

When I view a Clinician Contract

Then I am able to adjust the Contract Start Date AND the Effective From field on all of the HPFs related to the Contract

_Example:_

NPPA Hospital B start up

HPF 2 Effective From `20260503`

Recruiter requests and submits contract for `20260503`

Rate finalization must happen before date adjustment (so that rates are appropriately linked to client contract)

Clinician Contracting Specialist updates **Contract** to `20260501`AND **HPF** Effective From to `20260501`
