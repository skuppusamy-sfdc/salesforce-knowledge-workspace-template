**Description:**

After all approvals are received, an email is supposed to be sent to the submitter and all approvers.

**Steps to reproduce:**

1. From Email, locate email with Subject = Approval Request for <Pro Forma Model “Name” field>

2. Review the Pro Forma Model details included in the email

3. Click on the link within the email to navigate to the approval request

4. Click "Approve"

Repeat Steps 1-4 for each Approver

**Expected vs Actual Result:**

Expected: Email sent to submitter and all approvers

Actual: No email received

## Root Cause

The underlying automation logic that triggered the final notification emails appeared to be functional (working in QA and in one UAT test). However, a misconfiguration of the UAT2 environment's email settings—such as system email addresses, email deliverability restrictions, or security settings— prevented the reliable delivery of these specific final notification emails.
