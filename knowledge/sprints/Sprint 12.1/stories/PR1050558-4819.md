## Description

**Steps to reproduce:**

1. Login to Core HC org as Recruiter

1. Open a clinician account with an Executed clinician contract having 2 HPF records

1. Add new HPF for the same service account as the HPF part of the Executed clinician contract with different Facility title

1. Using ‘Start from Previous Active Contract’ option, create a new clinician contract

1. From Summary tab of the new clinician contract, add the 2 new HPFs with different Facility title

**Expected Result:**

1. When new HPF is added with a facility change, all rates should be cloned from the previous HPF with all required fields

**Actual Result:**

1. When new HPF is added with a facility change, all rates are not cloned from the previous HPF

1. For the only 1 cloned rate, CPA.StartDate is not populated
