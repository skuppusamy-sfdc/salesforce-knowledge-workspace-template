## Solution

When Clinician contract record Status = Executed

Check if the most most recently added HPF to the executed Contract using 

1. Contract.ProviderNetworkContract.Healthcare_Practitioner_Facility__r.Practitioner.PersonContactId Order By Contract.ProviderNetworkContract.Healthcare_Practitioner_Facility__r.Created Order BY DESC LMIT 1

1. Query User.ContactId = Contract.ProviderNetworkContract.Healthcare_Practitioner_Facility__r.Practitioner.PersonContactId and check if FederationIdentifier = null

## Send Email with data and subject as per AC

### Hospital Name = Contract.ProviderNetworkContract.Healthcare_Practitioner_Facility__r.Account.Name (from Step 1)

### When Professional Title = NP or PA or RN or LPN Contract.ProviderNetworkContract.Healthcare_Practitioner_Facility__r.Account.Professional_Title__pc (from Step 1)

#### Contract.ProviderNetworkContract.Healthcare_Practitioner_Facility__r.Account.FirstName (from Step 1)

### When Professional Title = MD or DO Contract.ProviderNetworkContract.Healthcare_Practitioner_Facility__r.Account.Professional_Title__pc (from Step 1)

#### Contract.ProviderNetworkContract.Healthcare_Practitioner_Facility__r.Account.LastName (from Step 1)

### CES Name = Contract.ProviderNetworkContract.Healthcare_Practitioner_Facility__r.AccountId in AccountTeamRole WHERE RoleName = “Clinician Experience Specialist” order by LastModified DESC LIMIT 1

#### Pull User.Email and User.Phone from the User Record (from Query 2)
