## Description

As a Legal team member, I want to Edit a Client Contract in status = Draft, so that I can capture the necessary information and track it through the execution process.

## Acceptance Criteria

AC5: Edit **Client Contracts [APPROVED]**

Given I am a Legal Team Member within the Corporate Support Apps

When I select a Client Contract (Client Agreement Request) record 

And **Status = “Draft”**

And **Contract Type = “Master”**

Then I have the ability to **Edit the Details** of the contract [see [+PR10505583022+|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505583022] for fields]

And I shall have the ability to add **new** ('Add Configuration' button) **or edit existing related records**: [editing of existing records split to story [+PR10505583519+|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505583519]]

- Term & Renewals 

- Compensation

- Protection

- Insurance

- Special Terms

- Legal Terms

- Termination

And from a related record, I have the **ability to link Service Account(s)** [linked via Provider Network Contract (PNC) record(s)]

- Service Account(s) that can be linked, must be linked to this Contract 

** Service Account(s) can NOT be linked to more than one related record of the same type. (**see example below)

- Service Account(s) can be removed, but a related record MUST have at least one linked Service Account 

And upon completion of ‘Add Configuration’, the system shall display the newly added record within it’s corresponding section.

And from the Provider Network Contract record, the user shall have the ability to edit: (NOTE: user is not limited to these fields, but listing for traceability that they were moved to the PNC record)

- Services Sold

- SCP Corporate Entity 

** When the user populates the ‘SCP Corporate Entity'field, then the system shall update the ‘Healthcare Payer Network’ field based on the associated Account (RT: Corporate Entity). (_note: since 'Healthcare Payer Network’ is a required field, prior to this the system had defaulted it to use the Corporate Entity TBD’s Healthcare Payer Network record._)

*Example: of positive and negative scenarios

You have 5 Service Accounts (SAEM, SAHM, SACC, SAWW, SA-PC)

And you have 2 Term & Renewal records & 3 Special Terms, then:

Positive scenario: 

Term & Renewal #1: SAEM, SAHM, SA-CC

Term & Renewal #2: SAWW, SAPC

Special Terms #1: SA-EM

Special Terms #2: SA-HM

Special Terms #3: SACC, SAWW, SA-PC

Negative scenario: 

Term & Renewal #1: SAEM, SAHM, **SA-CC**

Term & Renewal #2: SAWW, SAPC, **SA-CC**

Special Terms #1: SA-EM

Special Terms #2: SAHM, **SAPC**

Special Terms #3: SACC, SAWW, **SA-PC**
