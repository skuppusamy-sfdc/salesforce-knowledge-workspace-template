## Description

As a recruiter, I the ability to generate a term sheet so that I can expedite the contracting process prior to engaging the Clinician Contracting Specialist team.

## Acceptance Criteria

Updates story [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505583201|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505583201|smart-link] updates in bold

Given I am a Recruiter within the Corporate Support apps

When I navigate to a clinician Contract record 

And the following criteria is met:

- Contract Status = “Approved“

And 

- [This is a new Contract (Previous Contract field is blank) 

OR 

- Clinician is an Independent Contractor (IC) moving to Employee (EE) employment type]

Then I shall have the ability to click “Generate Term Sheet”, (should NOT be displayed if the criteria above is not met)

Then a document is generated with the following template (attachment = **Conditional Offer Letter (8)**), 

And the following fields are pulled into the merge fields with the initiating Clinician/Contract details

**Conditional Offer Letter (8)** - merge fields listed below:

- Date = [today’s date]

- Clinician/Clinician Name = Contract.AccountID 

- Clinician contact info = [Clinician’s > Mailing Address]

- Insert Title = [ATS Applicant’s > Job > Job Title]  (Note: multiple values should NOT be an issue, should only have one job title. _If there are multiple, add multiple values separated by commas_)

- Insert Name = [ATS Applicant’s > Hiring Manager’s Name] (Note: multiple values should NOT be an issue. [REMOVE From Template in story [+PR10505583201+|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505583201]]

- Position = [ATS Applicant’s > Job > Job Title] 

- Position Type = Contract.Time_Type__c

- Start Date = Contract.StartDate

- Locations = [Display the contract’s active HPFs Service Account name (Include related HPFs to the Clinician Contract that are in Status = “Prospect” and “Candidate”)]

- Compensation = [Display the Rates, see below for logic to display] 

** If Employment Type = Employee (EE) AND Time Type = Full Time AND Shift Type = NP/PA, then display:

*** Contract’s “EE NP/PA Full Time Monthly Blended” 

*** Format = [EE NP/PA Full Time Monthly Blended] per Month

**** Example: $200.00 per Month

** If Employment Type = Employee (EE) AND Time Type = Part Time AND Shift Type = NP/PA, then display:

*** Contract’s “EE NP/PA Hourly Blended”

*** Format = [EE NP/PA Hourly Blended] per Hour

**** Example: $55.00 per Hour

** Else, display all Type of Rates (Shift, Bonus, Leadership Incentives, Variable, Stipend, Target, Locum)

*** Shift:

**** Display: [Shift Full Name] [Rate] [Unit of Measure]

*** Bonus:

**** Display: [Type] [Rate] [Unit of Measure]

*** Leadership Incentive:

**** Display: [Type] [Rate] [Unit of Measure]

*** Variable:

**** Display: [Type] [Rate] [Unit of Measure]

*** Stipend:

**** Display: [Type] [Rate] [Unit of Measure]

*** Target:

**** Display:  [Rate] [Unit of Measure]

*** Locum: 

**** Display: [Type = Shifts Requested] [Shift Requested (#)] [Unit of Measure = _per Month_]

**** Display: [Type] [Rate] [Unit of Measure]

- Anticipated Monthly Clinical Hours = [Contract> Anticipated Clinical Hours]  [REMOVE From Template in story [+PR10505583201+|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558-3201]]

** **Anticipated Monthly Clinical Hours = [Contract> Anticipated Clinical Hours] - [Added back in this story 4409]*

- Special Terms = [Contract > Contract Special Terms] 

** ONLY display in the template when populated. 

** If Special Terms is blank, then this field within the template shall NOT be displayed.

- SCP Employee Name = User.FirstName + User.LastName

- SCP Employee Title = User.Title

And a preview of the generated document is displayed to the initiating user

And Additional actions:

- Cancel

- Finish
