## Description

**Steps to reproduce:**

1. Login to Core HC Org as Medical Staff Liaison - Antony Patch

1. Open a clinician record without offboarding case

1. Click ‘Initiate Termination Request’ button

1. Select the required HPFs, enable SCP termination, click ‘Next’ button

1. Enter the termination details for all selected HPFs [same Reason for Termination for all HPFs] and click Next button

**Expected Result:**

Reason for Termination field should be populated on SCP Offboarding case with same value as Facility Offboarding case

**Actual Result:**

Reason for Termination field is not populated on SCP Offboarding case and is blank

## Root Cause

In the omniscript, **SCP Offboarding Case** is getting created first, then **Facility Offboarding Cases.** So Flow is unable to update **Reason for Termination** on **SCP Offboarding Case.**

Design: Inconsistent Design
