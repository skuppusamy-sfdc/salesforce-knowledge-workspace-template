**Description:** 

An issue was identified while executing a one-time cloned contract, where the rate information did not pass through. We later realized this occurred because the “refresh client rates"  button was not clicked during the cloning process. As a result, the contract was cancelled; however, we observed that all contract data was deleted. Upon further analysis, Sakti confirmed this behavior is a defect, as cancelling the contract should not delete all contract elements and data. This issue should be logged as a defect in Jira.
