**Description:**

Nicole completed the Test scripts and the expected results was to have a Parent case create. Navigated to all Open cases and the Parent case wasn't created.

**Record Link:**

https://corehc--uat2.sandbox.lightning.force.com/lightning/cmp/force__dynamicRelatedListViewAll?force__flexipageId=Service_Record_Page&force__cmpId=lst_dynamicRelatedList8&force__recordId=001bf00000S3zr3AAB&ws=%2Flightning%2Fr%2FAccount%2F001bf00000S3zr3AAB%2Fview&uid=1765403976504

**Steps to Reproduce:**

Tester: Nicole Fontenot

1. From the Corporate Support Console, choose Accounts and navigate to the Service Account that needs a Facility Term

1. Click on Contracts

1. Click on the down arrow next to "Recently Viewed" and choose the "All Client Contracts" list view

1. Find the Contract you'd like to Term (Contract must be a Client Contract in Status = Executed) and click on the Contract Number

1. From the buttons in the header, click on "Begin Facility Termination"

1. Choose at least 1 Service Account and click "Next"

1. For each Service Account chosen, click the down arrow and choose Edit

1. Fill in the required fields - with Termination Date = tomorrow's date and click "Save"

1. Review changes and click "Save"

**Expected vs. Actual Results:**

Expected: Parent Case Created (per Service Account) for Client Offboarding (viewable on the Related Tab, in the Open Cases related list) 

Five Child Cases are created  per Parent Case  with Tasks auto-generated based on the Service Account's Parent Service Line and assigned to a queue or Account Team Role Members  

Actual: Parent case wasn't created

## Root Cause

This issue was coming because we changed the format for Termination Time in OS for Begin Facility Termination flow but case Termination Request Time field type was not changed to accomodate the format change.
