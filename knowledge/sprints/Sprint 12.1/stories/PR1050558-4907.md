## Description

Observation bug _System allows duplicate email during Customer User creation — user unable to log in to portal after logout

Description:

While creating a new **Customer User** from a Clinician record, the system allows using an **email address that is already associated with another Salesforce user**.

Although the user can initially log in to the portal after receiving the credentials, upon logging out and attempting to log in again, the system throws an error and prevents login.

This indicates a **duplicate email handling issue** during Customer User creation.

### **Steps to Reproduce:**

1. Log in as any **Corporate Support User** in the **Core HC org**.

1. Navigate to a **Clinician record**.

1. Under the **Highlights panel**, click on **Enable Customer User**.

1. Enter an **email address** that is already used by another user in Salesforce.

1. Complete the user creation and password recovery process.

1. Open the received email from SCP (contains username and temporary password).

1. Log in to the portal successfully using:

👉 [https://corehc-qa.sandbox.my.site.com/s|https://corehc-qa.sandbox.my.site.com/s]

1. Log out from the portal.

1. Attempt to log in again using the same username and password.

### **Actual Result:**

** User is **created successfully* even though the email address already exists in the system.

** User is able to **log in initially* to the portal.

** Upon logout, user is **unable to log in again* — system throws a login error.

### **Expected Result:**

** The system should **prevent duplicate email addresses* during Customer User creation.

** If a user already exists with the same email, the system should **display an error message* like:

“A user already exists with this email address. Please use a unique email.”

- Portal login should be consistent and functional for newly created users.

## Acceptance Criteria

New Enhancement story to ensure that when setting up a Clinician Portal User, the email must be unique to log into the Care Connect/HC Portal. 

AC1: Create Task when auto provision of HC Portal access fails (updates story [+PR10505581789+|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505581789])

Given I am a Recruiter in the Corporate Support or Corporate Support Console app

When I update the ATS Stage field on an ATS Applicant record to ‘Awaiting Intake Form’

And the clinician has not yet been provisioned HC Portal access

And the system was **unable** to **automatically provisioned HC Portal access** (creation failed due to: clinician ‘Email’ is blank OR clinician ‘Email’ is duplicate to an existing, active HC Portal user) 

Then the **system shall automatically create** a task for the user who updated the ATS Stage and instruct them to manually create the user ('Enable Customer User'). 

- Task Record Type = “Default”

- Assigned To = <insert user who moved the ATS Applicant stage to “Awaiting Intake Form”>

- Subject = “Email Validation Failed – Manually Enable Customer User to Continue”

- Comments = “The system was unable to automatically create a portal user due to the ‘Email’ field on the clinician record being blank or the Email address is a duplicate to an existing, active portal user.  The clinician cannot proceed through the recruiting process until this task is complete. Please navigate to the Clinician record and select “Enable Customer User” in the upper right to manually enable the Clinician’s to access the Care Connect portal to view Helpful Links and complete any ‘To Do’ or open tasks. “

- Status = Open

- Due Date = <7 days>

- Priority = High

- Name = <insert Clinician record>

- Related To = <insert ATS Applicant record>

AC2: Add ‘Email’ validation to Manual Creation ‘Enable Customer User' (original story [+PR1050558811+|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558811]+)+

Given I am a **Recruiter**, **Clinician Experience Team Member**, **Data Quality team member** in the Corporate Support or Corporate Support Console app

When I open a clinician record where they have not been provisioned portal access

And I select the “Enable Customer User” button to manually initiate their portal access (see [security matrix|https://schumachergroup.sharepoint.com/:x:/r/teams/UnifiedPlatform-ReGenesis/Shared%20Documents/Program%20Documentation/Program%20Planning/Discovery%20Deliverables%20%26%20Documentation/Salesforce%20team%20Deliverables/Architecture/Security%20Matrix.xlsx?d=w796b8306c5314622a033076ac7c727e6&csf=1&web=1&e=I8Q1fo] for appropriate user license and profile selections)

And in the **'Email’ field, the email address entered is the same/duplicate of an existing, active portal user**

Then the system shall display the following **error message**:

- “This email address is a duplicate to an existing, active portal user. Please provide a unique email address for this clinician. The clinician cannot proceed through the recruiting process until this step is complete.”

And the user must correct in order to successfully create a new portal user. (Once the Clinician user is enabled, on the Clinician record the button = “Login to Experience as User” )
