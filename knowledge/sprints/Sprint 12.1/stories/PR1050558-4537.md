## Description

**Tester:** Ashlee Savoy

**Role:** Field Ops/SFO

**Description:** When performing the test script, can not add pay codes. Nothing populates in drop down

**Steps to Reproduce:**

1. Initiate Facility Change from Service Account record by clicking the down arrow and choosing "Request Contract Change" in the header 

1. Check the radio button for Yes for the coverage change. 

1. Fill in the "Contract Start Date" and "Description" and click "Next". 

1. Review the Contract info presented and click "Create" . 

1. Add a New Shift(s) by clicking on the Related Tab and then click "New". 

1. Add a new shift with the same Shift Type and fill in required fields, and "Save". 

1. Navigate back to the Contract, click on the "Summary" tab. 

1. Click on "Specify Rates". 

1. Choose the Type of Rate = Shift, Shift Type = <select same value as newly created shift> and click "Next". 

1. Input the required fields with the same rate and click "Finish". 

1. Click on the "Rate Pay Code" tab and navigate to your newly created Shift. 

1. Update (allowed to add/remove) one or more Rate Pay Codes (as needed) - by clicking on "Add Pay Codes" or delete icon. 

1. When adding you can select one or more Pay Codes and click "Finish". 

1. From the Contract, click on the "Summary" tab and click "Submit Contract Request"  NOTE: Make Note of the Contract Number you submitted

**Actual vs. Expected Results:**

Expected: When on step 12, ability to add pay codes is present  

Actual: Ability to add pay codes not present
