## Description

As a System Administrator, as part of the all Client and Clinician Contract Cloning process, I do NOT want the Legacy Record information to be cloned, so that our data migration process doesn’t get affected by newly created records.

## Acceptance Criteria

AC1: Contract Cloning - Exclude Legacy Info

Given I am a System Administrator within the Corporate Support apps

When users clone/amend **Contracts** (RT:Clinician, Client Contract, Letter of Intent, or Practice Operations)

Then the following Legacy fields shall NOT be cloned to the new Draft contract. 

1. Legacy_Corp_Id__c  (Legacy Corp Id)

1. Legacy_Record_Id__c (Legacy Record Id)

1. Legacy_Record_Name__c (Legacy Record Name)

1. Legal_Entity__c (Legal Entity)

AC2: Preventive Care Agreement Cloning - Exclude Legacy Info

Given I am a System Administrator within the Corporate Support apps

When clone/amend Contracts (RT:Clinician or Practice Operations) with Preventive Care Agreement records

Then the following Legacy fields shall NOT be cloned to the new **Preventive Care Agreement** records:

1. Legacy_Pay_Rate_Id__c (Legacy Pay Rate Id)

1. Legacy_Facility_Rate_Title_Id__c (Legacy Facility Rate Title Id)

1. Legacy_Shift_Name__c (Legacy Shift Name)
