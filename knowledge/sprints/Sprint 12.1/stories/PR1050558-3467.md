## Description

As a Legal team member, when I am on a Letter of Intent Contract, I want the ability to Expire, so that I can indicate that a contract is expiring and a NEW agreement/contract is created. (not amending, the contract has ended. A brand new contract is being created or would need to be created)

## Acceptance Criteria

AC1: Ability to ‘Expire Contract'

Given I am a Legal Team Member within the Corporate Support Apps

When navigate to a Contract (RT: Letter of Intent) record

Then I have the ability to select the ‘Expire Contract’ guided experience

And available navigation options:

- Cancel (cancel is via the “X” in upper right)

** No tasks are initiated

** Guided Experience is closed

** User is returned to the Letter of Intent Contract

- Finish/Save

** Guided Experience is closed

** User is returned to the Letter of Intent Contract

*** System shall update **current** & **All* Previous Contract Status = “Expired”

## Solution

Flow Action Contract.RT: Letter of Intent

Update all related Contract using the formula field 

- Contract..Ultiamte_Parent_Id__c = CURRENT.Contract.Ultiamte_Parent_Id__c (field build in [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505583521|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505583521|smart-link] )

- Contract.Status = “Expired”
