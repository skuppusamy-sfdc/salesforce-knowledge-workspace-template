## Description

As a Legal team member, when I am on a Client Contract, I want the ability to Cancel a Contract, so that I can indicate that there was a decision to not move forward with this contract.

## Acceptance Criteria

AC: Ability to ‘Cancel Contract'

Given I am a Legal Team Member within the Corporate Support Apps

When navigate to a Contract (RT: Client Contract) record

And Contract Type = Master

Then I have the ability to select the ‘Cancel Contract’ guided experience 

And available navigation options:

- Cancel (cancel is via the “X” in upper right)

** No updates are made

** Guided Experience is closed

** User is returned to the Client Contract

- Finish/Save

** Guided Experience is closed

** User is returned to the Client Contract

** And the system shall:

*** Set the Status = 'Abandoned’

Notes:

|ACTIONS (guided process)|no condition on displaying actions| |

|Generate|Generate agreement documents from the system - phase 2| |

|Regenerate|Generate agreement documents from the system - phase 2| |

|Cancel Request|If there is a decision to not move forward|Status = Abandoned|

## Solution

Create a Action in Contract RT: Client Contract

On Button Click Update the Contract.Status = 'Abandoned’
