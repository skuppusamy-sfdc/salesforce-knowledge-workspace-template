## Description

Per story 3021, upon completing the “Configure Contract” guided experience, the system shall close the guided experience.

Issue: the tab remains open when the legal team member “**Cancel**” or **Finish** the guided experience.  (see screenshot of tab that remains open & screenshot of AC that is not working)

!image20251120203116.png|width=315,alt="image20251120203116.png"!

!image20251120203353.png|width=315,alt="image20251120203353.png"!

Note: 

For Finish - the system is correctly directing the user to the new Client Contract and Updating the case status.

## Root Cause

The issue occurred only in the Salesforce Console view. By default, OmniScript does not close sub-tabs on Cancel. We resolved this by updating the OmniScript configuration.
