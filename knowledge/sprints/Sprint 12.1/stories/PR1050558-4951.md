## Description

When Facility offboarding case is closed then Contract should be cancelled given that there is no more active facility at that clinician.

**Steps to Reproduce.**

1. Run “Initiate Termination request” Flow to create Facility offboarding case.

1. Complete/Close the Tasks related to cases.

1. Verify case is closed with status closed & Reason = complete.

1. Verify Contracts are being updated to Cancelled related to HPF.

**Actual Result:** Contracts are in Executed status.

**Expected Result:** Contract should be Updated to Cancelled status & Reason = Terminated.
