## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a System Administrator within the Corporate Support apps

When the following criteria is met:

- HPF > Anticipated First Shift [First_Scheduled_Date__c] is populated

- HPF Service Account > Billing Company = “EMBCC”

- HPF > Credentialing Status ≠ Inactive

Then the system shall automatically generate an email with the following information:

- Recipient:(PROD) [+servicerequest@scphealth.com+|mailto:servicerequest@scphealth.com]

- Subject: Add Clinician_[Clinician’s Global ID]_[HPF’s Service Account’s Global Facility ID]_[HPF’s Account (service account name)]_[HPF’s Anticipated First Shift (First_Scheduled_Date__c)]

- Body: This new clinician needs to be added to AthenaIDX. Please review the information below and complete the necessary steps to add this clinician.

+Clinician Details:+

Clinician Name: [Clinician’s Name]

First Scheduled Date: [HPF’s Anticipated First Shift [First_Scheduled_Date__c]

Credentialing Status: [HPF’s Credentialing Status]

Professional Title: [Clinician’s Professional **Title** Status]

+Hospital Details:+
