## Description

**Description:**

User can not edit the "financial implications" and "impact of contract" fields

**Tester:** Brent Malaski

**Role:** Finance

**Test Case ID:** MAIN-005

**Steps to Reproduce:**

1. From the Service Account used in MAIN-001, click on the "Related" tab. 

1. Click on the "Contract Number" of the Contract that needs approval.

1. Click on the "Approval" tab. 

1. Click on the Approval Work Item Name you'd like to work on. 

1. Click on Related Record to navigate to the Practice Operations Contract. 

1. On the Details tab, edit the 'Financial Implications' and 'Impact of Contract' fields as needed. 

1. Click the Approval tab to navigate back to your approval work item. 

1. Click on your Approval Work Item Name you'd like to work on. 

1. Click on the "Review" button in the header. 

1. Click the radio button for "Approve the work item". 

1. Add any Comments, if desired. 

1. Click "Confirm"

**Expected vs. Actual Results:**

Expected: Ops Finance rep can edit financial implication and impact of contract 

Actual: Edit options for these fields not present

## Solution

Update “Finance” Permission Set Group.

- Remove 'View Client Contracts' permission set

- Add ‘Manage Client Contracts’ permission set

## Root Cause

The Permission Set Group assigned to the Finance personas lacked the necessary edit access (e.g., missing Field-Level Security or Profile/Permission Set permissions) for those specific contract fields when the contract status was "Submitted." This configuration flaw blocked the Finance users from performing a required step in their approval process.

## Root Cause Type

Human Error: Developer Mistakes
