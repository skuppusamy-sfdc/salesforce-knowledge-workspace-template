## Solution

Update the Integration Procedure ContractRate_FacilityToClinician

1. Add a condition to filter criteria PreventiveCareAgreement.Is_Deleted__c = false

1. Add a Filter condition to DREGetPCAandClientContractDiffFacility to include ContractId [Practice Operation Contract for the Client]

## Add a step before DREGetPCAandClientContractDiffFacility to identify the ContractId

## Query: Select Id from Contract where RecordType.DeveloperName= “Practice Operation” AND AccountId = lstAccount (Service AccountId) and StartDate <= Clinician.Contract.StartDt AND (EndDate >= Clinician.Contract.StartDate OR EndDate = null) Order By StartDate Desc LIMIT1

e.g. 

PO Contract Date 

C1: `20260101`

C2: `20260102`

C3: `20260110`

Clinician Contract Date: `20260115`

Pick: C3 ContractId

Clinician Contract Date: `20260110`

Pick: C2 ContractId
