**Description:** In the offboarding process, if the termination request date is prior to the clinician agreement effective date, an error from mulesoft occurs. It does not notify the person initiating the termination, thus all cases are closed without the requestor or support teams aware that the action wasn't actually closed. Additionally, when going through and attempting to resubmit the termination, it does not trigger any offboarding cases to begin the process with the appropriate dates.

**Steps to Reproduce:** Victoria Hornsby clinician HPF, initiate termination all HPFs selected with a full SCP termination. All fields submitted and additional check boxes for bonus repayment, exit interview selected. Term request date 1/14, last date 3/15.

**Expected vs. Actual:** A blocker to submitting the IA based on the request date submitted is needed, to inform the initiator that this is not a feasible date based on the clinician hire date.
