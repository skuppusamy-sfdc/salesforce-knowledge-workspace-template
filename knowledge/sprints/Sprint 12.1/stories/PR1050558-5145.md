## Description

### **Description:**

In the _Initiate Early Onboarding_ flow, when a user selects a **Facility Type** and chooses a **Facility** that already has an existing **Active** or **Prospect HPF record**, the **HPF details** section displays **blank** values.

However, when a **new HPF** (without any existing onboarding record) is selected, the HPF details populate correctly.

### **Steps to Reproduce:**

1. Log in to **CoreHC QA Sandbox**.

1. Navigate to **Clinician Manual Onboarding** flow.

1. Select **“Initiate Early Onboarding”**.

1. Choose **Onboarding Type = Facility / SCP/ BOTH**.

1. In the **Facility Selection** list, check a facility where an **HPF record already exists** with status **Active** or **Prospect**.

1. Observe the **HPF details section**

### **Actual Result:**

** The HPF details section appears **blank** for facilities with an **existing HPF* (Active or Prospect).

- Onboarding record creation does not prepopulate data.

### **Expected Result:**

** The HPF details should populate for **existing HPF records (Active or Prospect)* as well,

similar to how it displays for newly created HPFs.

- Users should be able to proceed with early onboarding without data gaps.
