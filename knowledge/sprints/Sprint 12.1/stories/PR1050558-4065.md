## Description

As a System Administrator, I want only specific section(s) and fields to be displayed on a Client Contract record based on “Contract Type”, so users are only present with fields for the specified contract type.

## Acceptance Criteria

Story to cover the layout of the Configuration tab

AC1: (requirements split from story [+PR10505583022+|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505583022])

Given I am a Legal Team Member within the Corporate Support Apps

When I select a Client Contract

Then on **tab = Configurations**, I shall have the ability to view the following sections in the below order:(expanded by default)

- Term & Renewal

- Compensation

- Protection

- Insurance

- Special Terms

- Legal Terms

- Termination

And within each section I can see high-level details about each record (Contract Number and related Provider Network Contracts Service Account name)
