## Description

**Tester:** Kristi Bouillion

**Role:** Data Quality

**Test Case ID:** Gold Set 18 - rehire at one location

**Description:** 

Incorrect Effective From Date Populating on Rehire HPF-

Provider was inactive at Fulton Urgent Care, with an HPF status= Inactive and HPF Effective to date= 8/23/2017.

We created a new HPF for Fulton Urgent Care to rehire him, we listed the new HPF effective from date as 1/1/2026.

When the HPF was created, it listed the HPF effective from date as 8/24/2017 (one day past the old HPF's effective to date). This is not what was expected, we expected to see the new HPF Effective from date as 1/1/2026, as requested.

**Steps to Reproduce:**

Rehire a clinician at a location where they are currently listed with an Inactive HPF status and their HPF effective to date is populated.

**Expected vs. Actual:**

When the HPF was created (rehire scenario), it listed the HPF effective from date as 8/24/2017 (one day past the old HPF's effective to date). This is not what was expected, we expected to see the new HPF Effective from date as 1/1/2026, as requested.
