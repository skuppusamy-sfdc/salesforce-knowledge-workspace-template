## Description

As a Clinician Contracting Specialist, I need to see field history on HPF and Contract records, so that I can determine when fields were updated and who made updates

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a Clinician Contracting Specialist in the Corporate Support apps

When I navigate to a Healthcare Practitioner Facility record

Then I am able to see a **Healthcare Practitioner Facility History related list** in the Related tab (placement: Healthcare Practitioner Facility History, Client Change Managements)
