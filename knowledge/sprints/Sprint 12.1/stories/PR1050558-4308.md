## Description

As a Clinician Contract Specialist, I need to see the Term Sheet Status field, so that I can understand where a contract is at in the lifecycle

## Acceptance Criteria

-Given I am a Corporate Support user with access to the Clinician Contract object (check this should be covered under [+PR10505583521+|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505583521])-

-Then when I navigate to a Clinician Contract record-

-I am able to see the Term Sheet Status field- already resolved as shown in comments

Given I am a Clinician Contract Specialist in the Corporate Support apps

Then when I navigate to a Contract record

I am able to **edit the Status field as described in the scenarios below:**

- Approved to Draft

- Approved to Contracting - In Progress

- Contracting - In Progress to Draft

- Contracting - In Progress to Approved

- Contracting - In Progress to Executed

- Contracting - In Progress to Canceled

- Executed to Canceled

## Solution

Modify the Contract Status Category to accommodate for Status Change

Draft (Draft)

Submitted (Draft)

Approved (Draft)

Contracting - In Progress (Draft)

Executed (Activated)

Canceled (Canceled)

Adjust the Record Type for Clinician and Client Contract
