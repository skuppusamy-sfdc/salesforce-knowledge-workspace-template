## Description

Steps to reproduce:

1. Login as MSL user

1. Open a clinician record with 1 HPF and open SCP & Facility onboarding tasks

1. Update PEP Sent Date on the HPF record

1. Update I-9 Status field on clinician record

**Expected Result:**

1. `Send PEP` task should be closed when PEP Sent Date field is updated

1. `I9 Update` task should be closed when I-9 Status field is updated

**Actual Result:**

1. `Send PEP` task is not getting closed when PEP Sent Date field is updated

1. `I9 Update` task  is not getting closed when I-9 Status field is updated
