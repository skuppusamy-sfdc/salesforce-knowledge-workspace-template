## Description

As a Legal team member, when I am on a Letter of Intent Contract, I want the ability to Terminate, so that I can indicate that a contract status is Terminated.

## Acceptance Criteria

AC: Ability to ‘Terminate Contract'

Given I am a Legal Team Member within the Corporate Support Apps

When navigate to a Contract (RT: Letter of Intent) record

Then I have the ability to select the ‘Terminate Contract’ guided experience

And available navigation options:

- Cancel (cancel is via the “X” in upper right)

** No updates are made

** Guided Experience is closed

** User is returned to the Letter of Intent Contract

- Finish/Save

** Guided Experience is closed

** User is returned to the Letter of Intent Contract

** And the system shall make the following updates:

*** Updates “Status” = “Terminated”

## Solution

Flow Action in Contract.RT: Terminate Contract

Update: Contract.Status = “Terminated”

Inactivate Validation Rule for Contract: 

- Termination_Reason_not_chosen

- Termination_Date_not_in_future

- Termination_Date_Null

- Termination_Justification_Required
