## Description

As an HR team member, I need to know the Last Day for a clinician leaving SCP, so that Workday can be updated accordingly for pay and benefits

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a MSL in the Corporate Support apps

When I complete a termination request for a clinician where Full SCP Offboarding is indicated AND all of the SCP Offboarding tasks are completed

Then I can see that the **Last Day** field is populated with the **farthest in the future Last Day from the associated Facility Offboarding cases**

Example: 

I request clinician offboarding, Full SCP, on 12/1/2025 and populate the following:

HPF 1 - Last Day = 1/1/2026

HPF2 - Last Day = 1/15/2026

HPF3 - Last Day = 1/31/2026 

On 12/15/2025, I update the Last Day field on the Facility Offboarding case for HPF2 to 2/15/2026 

On 12/29/2025, all child tasks on the SCP Offboarding are completed and their exit is confirmed so the SCP Offboarding case is automatically closed AND Last Day is now automatically populated to 2/15/2026 as this is the date furthest in the future for that clinician based on the Facility Offboarding cases
