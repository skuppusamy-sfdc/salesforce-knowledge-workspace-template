**Test Case ID:** CTERM-009

**Description:** 

Facility Offboarding is occurring at the wrong time

**Steps to Reproduce:**

To Offboard:

1. From the Corporate Support Console, choose Accounts and navigate to the Service Account that needs a Facility Term

1. Click on Contracts

1. Click on the down arrow next to "Recently Viewed" and choose the "All Client Contracts" list view

1. Find the Contract you'd like to Term (Contract must be a Client Contract in Status = Executed) and click on the Contract Number

1. From the buttons in the header, click on "Begin Facility Termination"

1. Choose at least 1 Service Account and click "Next"

1. For each Service Account chosen, click the down arrow and choose Edit

1. Fill in the required fields - with Termination Date = 2 day out  and click "Save"

1. Review changes and click "Save"

To review Case:

1. From the Corporate Support Console, click on Cases

1. Choose the "All Open Cases" list view

1. Search the list view for "Offboarding", then sort results by created date with most recent at the top

1. Click into a Case with Subject = "Facility Offboarding" (created yesterday)

**Expected vs. Actual:**

Should be Offboarding at night. Currently happening at 12:00 AM

NOTE: Per 1/8 testing meeting, we discussed that night = 23:59PM --- **UPDATED to 11:45PM/23:45PM to allow time to complete**

Regardless of the Client Termination Time, we should run our scheduled flow (PNC Scheduled Flow) at 23:59PM on the “Client Termination Date”

In the testing scenario the “Client Termination Date” = 1/7/2026, so Expected Result should be that Clinician Offboarding cases are created on 1/7/2026 11:59PM (23:59PM) to ensure that offboarding activities do not occur until the Client Termination occurs.
