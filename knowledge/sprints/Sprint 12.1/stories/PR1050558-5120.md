## Description

As a Payroll team member, I need to update Rate Pay Codes on practice operations contracts, so that I can confirm data is correct prior to contract execution

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a Payroll team member, SFO, or System Administrator

When I navigate to a Practice Operations contract

And the contract has reached approval step 1: Performance Analyst approval (see [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505581740|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505581740|smart-link]  for approval process details)

Then I am able to edit Rate Pay Codes

Given I am a non-System Administrator user

When I navigate to a Practice Operations contract

And the contract in in Executed status

Then I am NOT able to edit fields on Contract, Contract Payment Agreement, Preventive Care Agreement, Rate Pay Code, and Shift

Given I am a Recruiter or Clinician Contracting Specialist

When I navigate to a Practice Operations contract

Then I am NOT able to edit Rate Pay Codes

_Note: notification to Payroll to complete this item is manual, no task to be built as part of phase 1_
