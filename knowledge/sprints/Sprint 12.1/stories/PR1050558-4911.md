**Description:** 

Account Name on Facility Offboarding case is invalid. Should be linking to Service Account, but is linked to Clinician name.

**Steps to Reproduce:**

1. From the Corporate Support Console, click on Cases

2. Choose the "All Open Cases" list view

3. Search the list view for "Offboarding", then sort results by created date with most recent at the top

4. Click into a Case with Subject = "Facility Offboarding" (created yesterday)

5. Click on "Account Name" to navigate to the Service Account
