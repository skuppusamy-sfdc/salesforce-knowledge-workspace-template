## Description

As a Recruiter, when I select “Refresh Client Rate” on a draft Clinician Contract, I want to see the time in the "Last Synched Date" field, so that if I refresh multiple times within the same day, I can tell if the system refreshed the rates.

## Acceptance Criteria

AC1: Add Time to "Last Synched Date" field

Given I am an Contracting Specialist or Recruiter within the Corporate Support Apps

When I am on a Clinician contract in Status = Draft

And on the Summary tab, I click “Refresh Client Rates”

Then the system shall update the "Last Synched Date" field to display the Date & **Time** that the client(s) rates were refreshed.
