## Root Cause

The affected users were not properly set up or provisioned with the correct Sales VP role/profile in the UAT2 environment. This missing or incorrect role assignment meant the users lacked the necessary permissions and Sharing Settings (like Organization-Wide Defaults or Role Hierarchy access) to view Opportunity records and update the client questionnaire status.
