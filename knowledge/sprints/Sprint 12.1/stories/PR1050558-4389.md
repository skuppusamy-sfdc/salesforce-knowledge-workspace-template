## Description

Tester: Maci Broussard

Persona: Clinician Contracting Specialist

Detailed Description: Would expect Clinician Name to pull when no active CTI is on Person Account. When active CTI is on person account we have entity language for clinicians that should be used.

1. Navigate to a person account without a CTI record

2. Generate any agreement document

3. Validate no Clinician Name pulled

Discussed last week and Perficient (Jide) is already working on needed change. Submitting this for documentation and tracking purposes. 

Templates were built to always require a CTI record on a person account to pull Clinician Name and entity when applicable. However, Clinicians will only have CTI records in SF in future state when they have an entity. 

Perficient to make updates to templates with below requirements. 

Future State Corporation and Tax Information (CTI) in SF:

•	Clinicians will only have CTI records when they have (or have had) an entity

•	Clinician Contracts will assume individual (use Clinician Name) when there is no active CTI record on the Person Account

•	Definition of active CTI:

o	When there is only an Effective Date on the CTI record = Contract Start Date is on or after the Effective Date and the status is Verified or Executed

o	When there is an Effective Date and Expiration Date on the CTI record = Contract Start Date falls within the Effective Date and Expiration Date and the status is Verified or Executed

	Above criteria should also be used when/if Clinician has multiple CTI records on their Person Account

CTI data details:

•	Draft status: Draft record, needs review

•	Verified status: data on CTI record has been validated by DQ team

•	Executed status: There is an executed Clinician Contract tied to that CTI record

•	Effective Date: date when Corporation and Tax Information will be in effect (start)

•	Expiration Date: date when Corporation and Tax Information will expire (end)
