## Description

Tester: Anna Lewis Elledge

Persona: Legal - Client Contracting

Test Case ID: CC-008

**Description:** 

Service Account name is showing correctly when Configuring a Contract. It is currently shows the Service Account number.

**Steps to reproduce:**

1. From the Console, click on the "Cases" tab

1. From the down arrow for the List Views, choose "Client Contracting Queue"

1. Choose the case created in Test Case CC-007 by drilling into the Case Number

1. Click on the "Configure Contract" button in the Header

1. Fill in the required fields 5a. Select Agreement Category = Facility Agreement

1. Ensure at least 1 Service Account is present from associated Service Accounts

**Expected vs Actual Result:** 

Should be seeing Service Account name, not number. (not Services name)

**Record link:** https://corehc--uat2.sandbox.lightning.force.com/lightning/page/omnistudio/omniscript?omniscript__type=Client&omniscript__subType=ConfigureContract&omniscript__language=English&omniscript__theme=lightning&omniscript__tabIcon=custom%3Acustom18&omniscript__tabLabel=CARConfigureContract&c__ContextId=500bf00000M0Qid&ws=%2Flightning%2Fr%2FCase%2F500bf00000M0QidAAF%2Fview

## Root Cause

The application's code, which populates the display fields during contract configuration, was incorrectly mapping or referencing the "Service Name" field where the "Service Account Name" field was intended to be displayed.
