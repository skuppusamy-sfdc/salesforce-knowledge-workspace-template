## Description

As a Clinician Contract Specialist, I need to view NP/PA tiers and where clinicians are expected to practice, so that I can generate contracts accurately

## Acceptance Criteria

**Non EE NP/PA Clinicians**

Given I am a Recruiter in the corporate support apps

When I request a contract for a clinician where **Professional Title does not equal NP or PA** and **Employment Type does not equal Employee**

Then I cannot specify NP/PA Tier and NP/PA Tier is not automatically populated

**Part Time EE NP/PA Clinicians**

Given I am a Recruiter in the corporate support apps

When I request a contract for a clinician where **Professional Title equals NP or PA** and **Time Type (Contract) is populated with Part Time** and **Employment Type equals Employee**

Then I +cannot specify NP/PA Tier+ and NP/PA Tier is not automatically populated

And EE NP/PA Hourly Blended is automatically calculated based on logic in [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505584667|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505584667|smart-link] 

**Full Time EE NP/PA Clinicians**

Given I am a Recruiter in the corporate support apps

When I request a contract for a clinician where **Professional Title equals NP or PA** and **Time Type (Contract) is populated with Full Time** and **Employment Type equals Employee**

Then I +can specify NP/PA Tier+ or it is automatically populated based on logic described in  [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505582235|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505582235|smart-link] and approval process

And NP/PA Rate fields are populated based on the logic described in [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505583779|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505583779|smart-link]

## Solution

Update the calculation base on AC (existing code)
