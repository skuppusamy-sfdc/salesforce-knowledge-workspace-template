+Manual Create HPF Date Validation+

Given I am a Recruiter or Data Quality team member in the Corporate Support apps

When I click Create HPF from a clinician record

Then the system shall display the following input fields:

- Facility Title (required)

- Time Type (required)

- Practice Type (required)

** Effective From (**required*, defaulted to current date and user can update)

If I have selected a Service Account where the clinician already has an HPF where status is ≠ Prospect

Once I click Save the system shall **validate the following before creating a new HPF record:**

- ‘Effective From’ date of the new HPF does not overlap with an existing HPF in Status ≠ Inactive for the same Service Account

** Example 1 - ERROR

*** Existing HPF: Service Account = Hospital A, Status = Active, Effective From = `20260101`, and Effective To = `20270101`

*** New HPF:  Service Account = Hospital A and Effective From = `20260601`

*** Error Message: HPF effective dates can not overlap. Please review this clinician’s existing HPFs.

** Example 2 - PASS

*** Existing HPF: Service Account = Hospital A, Status = Active, Effective From = `20260101`, and +Effective To = blank+

*** New HPF:  Service Account = Hospital A and Effective From = `20260601`

And if validation passes:

- The system creates new HPF record (existing functionality [+PR1050558291+|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558291])

- The system clones over specific fields from the existing HPF record in Status ≠ Inactive with the same Service Account (original story [+PR10505582119+|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505582119])

*** **ADD ‘Okay to Send Rates’ and ‘Rate Active Date’ fields as one of the fields to clone over as part of AC1 of story 2119.*

**And the system shall update the ‘Effective To’ date of the existing HPF (Status ≠ Inactive AND the same service account) if the field is currently populated or blank.** 

** **Effective To = <the new HPF’s ‘Effective From’ date minus 1 day>*

** Example: New HPF Effective From = `20260601`, original HPF Effective To = `20260531`
