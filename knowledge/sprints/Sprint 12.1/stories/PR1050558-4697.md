## Description

**Scenario:  Given** that Clinician is working as **Full time** in facility, 

                  **And** when clinician contract is created for **Full Time.** 

                  **And** when rates are configured using “**Configure rate**” button. 

                  **Then Part Time** rates are also being displayed. & No indication on the screen where its Part time or full time rate.

**Impact:** NPPA calculation is considering full Time & Part Time Rates for Salary calculation. (if Selected.)

**Suggestion:** Rates should be filtered based on HPF’s Time type. (i.e. Full Time/Part time)
