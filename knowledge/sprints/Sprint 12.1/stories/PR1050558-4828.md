## Description

EE NPPA Monthly/Weekly/ Overage fields should not be calculated for EE NPPA Part Time Contract when HPF is deleted.

**Steps to Reproduce:** 

1. Create a EE NP/PA Part Time contract with 2 HPF. 

1. COnfigure the rate for both HPF.

1. Delete one HPF from Contract.

1. **Actual Result :**   EE NPPA Monthly/Weekly/ Overage fields are  being calculated.

1. **Expected Result:** EE NPPA Monthly/Weekly/ Overage fields should not be calculated.

## Root Cause

In enhancement **PR1050558-4667**, the **Time Type condition** was missed in one of the flows.

The initial development was done by a different developer, and the calculations are handled across **three separate flows**:

1. **PCA After Insert and Update Flow** – triggered on PCA insert and update

1. **Capture PCA Event Subscribe Flow** – triggered on PCA delete

1. **CPA Delete Flow** – triggered on CPA delete

The missing Time Type condition in **CPA Delete Flow**  caused inconsistent calculation behavior.
