## Description

As a Recruiter, when I “Add HPF” for the same Facility (Service Account) but a different Facility Title on a draft Clinician Contract,  I want the system to clone the clinician rates from existing Facility (gray) to the new Facility (green), so that I can maintain any over rates from the old Facility Title to the new Facility Title. 

Note: After a user “Add HPF” they should click “Refresh Client Rate” to ensure that the most recent rates are synced.

## Acceptance Criteria

AC1:

Given I am a Recruiter within the Corporate Support apps

When I am on a Clinician contract in Status = Draft

And I “Add HPF” for the same Service Account/Facility as an existing (Eg. Clinician has a change in Facility Title at the same Service Account) 

Then the system shall:

1. Gray the original Service Account (Is Cloned = True and marked as deleted) – Existing functionality per [+PR10505583931+|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505583931]

1. Clone rates from original Service Account (Gray  Is Cloned = True and marked as deleted) and add to new Facility Title (Green  not marked as deleted and not cloned)

1. On the Summary tab display the cloned rates for new facility title. 

And when user clicks “Refresh Client Rate”, the system shall sync/update the rates based on the matching client contract – Existing functionality newest story [+PR10505584791+|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505584791]

Note: to add additional rates, the user shall “Configure Rates” as normal.
