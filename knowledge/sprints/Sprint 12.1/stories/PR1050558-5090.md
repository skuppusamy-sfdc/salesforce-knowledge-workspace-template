+Automated Create HPF from Job Date Validation+

Given I am a Recruiter in the Corporate Support apps

When I update the ATS Stage field on an ATS Applicant record to Offer 

And a Healthcare Practitioner Facility record is automatically created (original story [+PR1050558823+|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558823])

** **HPF Effective From = <the Requested Start Date field on ATS Applicant>* this is a new update to be added in this story

** Asymbl process update to require this field is populated: [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505585051|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505585051|smart-link] 

If the HPF is for a Service Account where the clinician already has an HPF where status is ≠ Prospect

Once I click Save the system**:**

- Create New HPF Record

** Clone over specific fields from the existing HPF record in Status ≠ Inactive with the same Service Account (original story [+PR10505582119+|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505582119])

**** **ADD ‘Okay to Send Rates’ and ‘Rate Active Date’ fields as one of the fields to clone over as part of AC1 of story 2119.*

** IF clinician already has an HPF where status is ≠ Prospect for this Service Account 

AND Original HPF Effective To is prior to New HPF Effective From (+new role introduces a gap, pull forward their new role to match+)

*** Then the New HPF Effective From is updated to the Original HPF Effective To + 1

*** _Example:_ 

**** Original HPF 1 Effective To `20260301`

**** New HPF 2 Effective From `20260401`_AKA Requested Start Date from ATS Applicant_

**** Update New HPF 2 Effective From to `20260302`

- Update Existing HPF Record

** IF clinician already has an HPF where status is ≠ Prospect for this Service Account 

And Original HPF Effective To has a gap between New HPF Effective From (+new role has overlap, end their original role to match new+)

*** Then the Original HPF Effective To is updated to the New HPF Effective From - 1

*** _Example:_ 

**** Original HPF 1 Effective To `20260801`

**** New HPF 2 Effective From `20260701`_AKA Requested Start Date from ATS Applicant_

**** Update HPF 1 Effective To to `20260630`

- Create Task for Data Quality (only for gap and overlap scenarios)

** Task Record Type = ‘Default’

** Subject = ‘Review overlapping HPF effective dates'

** Assigned To = Data Quality Queue

** Comments = ‘A new HPF record was requested for <new HPF Effective From date> which created an overlap or gap with an existing HPF. Please review the clinician’s HPF records to confirm dates are set correctly.'

** Status = 'Open'

** Due Date = <1 day>

** Priority = 'High’

** Name = <set to Clinician>

** Related To = <set to newly created HPF>
