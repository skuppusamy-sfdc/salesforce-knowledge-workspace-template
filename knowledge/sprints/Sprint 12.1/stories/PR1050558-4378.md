## Root Cause

Conga related bug.

The user (**Scott Fitzgerald**) was **missing the required "Clinician Contracting Specialist" permission set** on their profile. This omission in the security matrix blocked the user from having the necessary access rights to view the addendum's content (specifically, the Sign-On Incentive Paragraph).
