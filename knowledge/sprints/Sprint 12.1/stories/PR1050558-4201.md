## Description

As a MSL and Clinician Experience Specialist, I need SCP Offboarding cases to be created with necessary information, so that teams can perform needed activities with accurate data available for review

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a MSL or Clinician Experience Specialist in the Corporate Support apps

When I submit a clinician offboarding request where Full SCP Termination is indicated

Then I can see that **Termination Request Date is automatically populated to today** (as built in[https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558306|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558306|smart-link] )

Given I am a MSL or Clinician Experience Specialist in the Corporate Support apps

When I submit a clinician offboarding request where Full SCP Termination is indicated

Then I can see that **the following fields are not available on the SCP Offboarding Case record**:

- Backfill Recommended

- Account Name

- Healthcare Practitioner Facility

- Date Termination Received

- Last Day

- Reason for Termination

- Voluntary Termination

- Exit Interview Requirement

- Bonus Repayment

- Pay Through Date

And these **fields are not required to close the case record**

_all of the above fields are populated on the Facility Offboarding case_
