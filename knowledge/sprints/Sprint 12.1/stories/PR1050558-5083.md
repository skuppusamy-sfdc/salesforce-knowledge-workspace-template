## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a Recruiter or Clinician (with portal access)

When I view the At A Glance component

Then I am able to see:

- Reference Check (HC)/ Reference Checks (portal)

*** In Progress = bpats__ATS_Applicant__c.References_Complete2__c "**Initiated*"

*** Completed = bpats__ATS_Applicant__c.References_Complete2__c "**Completed*"

- Virtual Interview (logic already in place)

** In Progress = bpats__Interview__c.bpats__Location_Type__c = "Online" AND bpats__Interview__c.bpats__Status__c = "Scheduled"

** Completed = bpats__Interview__c.bpats__Location_Type__c = "Online" AND bpats__Interview__c.bpats__Status__c = "Feedback Provided"

- Onsite Interview  (logic already in place)

** In Progress = bpats__Interview__c.bpats__Location_Type__c = "InPerson" AND bpats__Interview__c.bpats__Status__c = "Scheduled"-

** Completed = bpats__Interview__c.bpats__Location_Type__c = "InPerson" AND bpats__Interview__c.bpats__Status__c = "Feedback Provided"-
