## Description

As a System Administrator, when a new Clinician is added, I want the system to automatically send an Athena Notification so that SCP Medical Billing is aware of the new Clinician and can take appropriate actions.

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a System Administrator within the Corporate Support apps

When the following criteria is met:

** HPF > **Anticipated First Shift* [First_Scheduled_Date__c] is populated

** HPF Service Account > **Billing Company* = “EMBCC”
