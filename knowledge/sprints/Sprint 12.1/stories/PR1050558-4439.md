## Description

**Steps to reproduce:**

1. Login to Salesforce as System Administrator

1. Open a clinician record of professional title NP/PA with HPF having Service Line 'Pediatric - Hospital Medicine'

1. Complete Intake Form flow

1. Login to HC portal as customer user

1. Verify if CoC (Peds HM) form is created for the user

1. Open a clinician record of professional title RN/LPN with HPF having Service Line 'Pediatric - Hospital Medicine'

1. Complete Intake Form flow

1. Login to HC portal as customer user

1. Verify if CoC (Peds HM) form is created for the user

**Expected Result:**

For clinicians with professional title NP/PA and RN/LPN,  when HPF Account’s Service Line is 'Pediatric - Hospital Medicine' then 

CoC (Peds HM) form should be created for the user

**Actual Result:**

For clinicians with professional title NP/PA ,  when HPF Account’s Service Line is 'Pediatric  Hospital Medicine' then EM NPPA is created for the user

For clinicians with professional title RN/LPN,  when HPF Account’s Service Line is 'Pediatric - Hospital Medicine' no CoC form is created for the user
