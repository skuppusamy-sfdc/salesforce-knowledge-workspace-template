## Description

Tester: Geetha Bhavani vanam

Persona: Business Development Associate

Test Case ID: CDM_002 and CDM 003

**Description:**

Unable to add Deal team when logged in as persona "BD". The error is shown up saying "Team members' access level for this opportunity may be greater than your organization's default opportunity access setting"

**Steps to reproduce:**

Login into SF HC UAT 2 and Login as Persona BD( Zachary Kieffer) 

1. From the Corporate Support Console, click on the Opportunity tab and drill into the Opportunity 

2. Navigate to the "Deal Team" related list, click the down arrow and choose "Add Deal Team Members"

**Expected Result:**

User should be able to add Deal team  

**Actual Result:**

Facing error "Team members' access level for this opportunity may be greater than your organization's default opportunity access setting"

## Root Cause

The page layout required to perform the "Add Deal Team Member" action was not assigned to the "Corporate Support" profile (which is likely the underlying profile for the "BD" persona).
