## Description

Steps to Reproduce:

1-Login as Legal Team member and navigate to the LOI Contract record.

2-Ensure status of LOI Contract is Draft.

3-Navigate to Key Dates section fields and update the Date Sent For Internal Review or Other Party Sent Date field.

4-Click on Save it and an error message is displayed.

5Repeat steps 14 on Client Contract.

Issue  While working on LOI and Client Contract with status Draft  When user updates the Key Dates section fields as - Date Sent For Internal Review or Other Party Sent Date then upon saving an error is being displayed.

Expected Result - Error shouldn't be displayed.

Actual Result - As of now error is being displayed.

## Solution

Fix the Status → Category field dependency by moving _Internal_ and _External_ from **Submitted__old** to **Submitted**, and add this as a post-deployment step in Copado.

## Root Cause

Status and Category field dependency was incorrect in QA because the picklist values were manually changed earlier.
