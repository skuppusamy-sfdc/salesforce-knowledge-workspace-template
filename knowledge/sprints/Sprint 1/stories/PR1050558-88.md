## Description

As a Business Development Associate, I need to be able to create Service records in Salesforce, so that I can document potential business for my accounts

## Acceptance Criteria

Given that I am a Business Development Associate logged in to the Core HC org

When I navigate to an Opportunity record

Then I can click on the Related tab, then I am able to create a Service record with the fields specified in the data dictionary (neither Pro Forma section is available on create)

Given that I am a Business Development Associate logged in to the Core HC org

When I navigate to a Services record where Type = Behavioral Health, Core Placeholder, Emergency Medicine (EM), Free Standing ED (FSED), Hospital Medicine (HM), Hospital Medicine Post Discharge Clinic, Integrated Care Physician (ICP), Intensive Care Unit (ICU), Needs Review, Urgent Care (UC)

Then I can see the +Pro Forma (Core)+ section on the page and NOT the Pro Forma (Non-Core) Section

And I can edit the fields in this section (excluding formula fields)

Given that I am a Business Development Associate logged in to the Core HC org

When I navigate to a Services record where Type = Connected Care  Complete, Connected Care  During, Connected Care  Next, Connected Care  Proactive, Innovation / Noncommercial, Other Service Lines (NONCORE), Value-Based Solutions, Virtual & Hybrid Care Solutions

Then I see the +Pro Forma (Non-Core)+ section on the page and NOT the Pro Forma (Core) Section

And I can edit the fields in this section (excluding formula fields)

## Solution

Confirm Service record types and page layouts

Add Service related list on Opportunity record

Create fields

Configure LRP with dynamic forms

Create Default record type
