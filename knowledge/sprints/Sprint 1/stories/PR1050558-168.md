## Description

As a Business Development Associate, I need to **edit** Account records for Facilities, Services, and Competitors, so that I can maintain accurate information for the sales process

As a Legal team member, I need to **edit** Account records for Corporate Entities, so that I can maintain accurate information for contracting and other key activities

As a Recruiter, I need to **edit** Person Account records for Clinicians, so that I can maintain accurate information for prospective employees

## Acceptance Criteria

Given that I am a Business Development Associate, Sales VP, Sales Operations, Implementation Specialist in the Corporate Support app

When I open an Account record where Record Type = Facility/Hospital, Business Account, Competitor, or Service Account

Then I am able to edit the fields on that record

Given that I am a Legal team member in the Corporate Support app

When I open an Account record where Record Type = Facility/Hospital, Business Account, Competitor, Service Account, or Corporate Entity

Then I am able to edit the fields on that record

Given that I am a Recruiter, Data Quality, MSL, MSL - CPA, Enrollment Specialist, or Legal in the Corporate Support app

When I open a Person Account (combo of Account and Contact) record where Record Type = Clinician

Then I am able to edit the fields on that record
