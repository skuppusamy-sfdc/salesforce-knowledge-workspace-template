## Description

As a Business Development Associate, Sales VP, Sales Operations, Implementation Specialist, I need to be able to create Facility/Hospital, Business Account, Competitor, and Service Account account records in Salesforce, so that I can capture holistic views of existing and potential business

As a Legal team member, I need to be able to create Corporate Entity account records in Salesforce, so that I can maintain accurate entity data for facilities

## Acceptance Criteria

Given that I am a Business Development Associate, Sales VP, Sales Operations, or Implementation Specialist logged in to the Core HC org

When I navigate to the Accounts tab

Then I can click on the New button and select between: Facility/Hospital, Business Account, Competitor, and Service Account

Then I am able to create a record with the fields specified in the data dictionary

Given that I am a Legal team member logged in to the Core HC org

When I navigate to the Accounts tab

Then I can click on the New button and select between: Facility/Hospital, Business Account, Competitor, Service Account, and Corporate Entity

Then I am able to create a record with the fields specified in the data dictionary

Given that I am a Business Development Associate, Sales VP, Sales Operations, Implementation Specialist, or Legal team member logged in to the Core HC org

When I navigate to the Accounts tab and create a new record

Then I am able to see the following descriptions:

+for Facility/Hospital+: Used to create Prospective and Current Clients at a facility and regional level

+for Business Account+: Used to create Non-Medical accounts (all other)

+for Competitor+: Used to record Incumbent or Competing accounts

+for Service Account+: Used to create all Service Lines at a facility, both for Current Opportunities and Future Business

+for Corporate Entity+: Used to record Legal attributes related to a Client’s organization, tax, and employment information

## Solution

Create 5 Account record types and 5 page layouts

Create approx. 50 fields

Grant access to record types and page layouts
