**Description:** 

On the provider contract request, we need the shift "time type" to be visible on the Summary page and the Configure Rates screen.

## Acceptance Criteria

AC1: Summary tab - add Time Type

Given I am a Recruiter or Clinician Contracting Specialist within the Corporate Support apps

When I am viewing a **Clinician Contract** record

Then on Summary tab, I can see the ‘Time Type’ after the ‘Facility Title’

And if a rate does not have a ‘Time Type’, ensure that we do not display '|’ after the Facility Title.

+Example with time type:+

‘Facility Title’ | ‘Time Type’

‘Rate’ ‘Unit of Measure’

+Example without time type:+

‘Facility Title’

‘Rate’ ‘Unit of Measure’

AC2: Configure Rates > Rate Details step - add Time Type

Given I am a Recruiter or Clinician Contracting Specialist within the Corporate Support apps

When I am working with a **Clinician Contract** in **Status = Draft**

And I selected button = Configure Rates

-And I selected Type of Rate = Shift-(for 2/13 release ‘Time Type’ will be displayed for all Type of Rates)

Then on the step = Rate Details, I can see the ‘Time Type’ displayed after ‘Type’

## Solution

AC1: Summary tab Omni add the PCA.TimeType

AC2: Configure Rates Omni add PCA.Time Type
