**Description:** 

When a data quality team member creates a "Contract" for a leased clinician, the system will skip contract confirmation and a message indicating that a signed contract is not needed is displayed. There are arrangements with specific clients where a "Contract" known as a leased provider acknowledgement is required. 

While a contract record is not needed, we need the system to notify Legal - Client Contracting for Healthcare Practitioner Facility record(s) having a Facility Title of Leased Employee or Leased Medical Director for Service Accounts with a Leased Provider Acknowledgment requirement. 

The applicable service accounts are listed in the attachment.
