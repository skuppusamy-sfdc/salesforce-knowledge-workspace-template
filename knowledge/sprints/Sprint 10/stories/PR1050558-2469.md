## Description

As a Clinician Experience Specialist or Scheduler, I need to request shiftspecific bonus approval in Salesforce, so that I can offer appropriate incentives for clinicians to work a hardto-staff shift

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a Clinician Experience Specialist or Scheduler in the Corporate Support apps

When I navigate to a Healthcare Practitioner Facility record

Then I am able to go through the guided [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558309|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558309|smart-link] process

- APAY Used for the Month (currency, 8 digits 0 decimals, required)

- APAY Budgeted (currency, 8 digits 0 decimals, required)

** Shift Compensation (formula, length of shift input in first screen ** hourly rate entered in second screen, only when unit of measure is per hour)

- Updated Help Text on % of Clinicians Contracted to Work to “Recruiting % without Candidates”

And when I select Save

- Case record is created

** Status = Submitted

- Approval Work Item is created related to the Rate Approval case record

** When APAY Used for the Month + Amount (Shift Compensation formula or amount specified per Shift) in this APAY Request is greater than the APAY Budgeted, assigned To: Account Team - DOO

** All others (else), assigned to Account Team - VPO

** Rate Approval case is locked from being edited

- Approve/Reject Actions

** When users approve/reject they can add comments

** Send Final notification (Rejected or approved) to request submitter and all approvers with comments

** Approved:

*** Case Status updated to Approved

** Not Approved: 

*** Case Status updated to Not Approved

Assumption is that the Service Account has the following team roles, if not the approval will be rejected. 

- Account Team - DOO

- Account Team - VPO

## Solution

In the existing oS for Open Shift APAY , add the three fields. The fields are mapped to Case (of record type Rate Approval)

- APAY Used for the Month (currency, 8 digits 0 decimals, required)

- APAY Budgeted (currency, 8 digits 0 decimals, required)

** Shift Compensation (formula, Shift Duration (first screen) ** Clinician Rate (hourly rate entered in second screen, only when unit of measure is per hour). 

Updated Help Text on % of Clinicians Contracted to Work to “Recruiting % without Candidates”

- On save, 

Rate Approval case record is already being created in submitted status

Create approval process/ modify existing approval process to accommodate the approval mentioned in AC

When APAY Used for the Month + Amount (Shift Compensation formula or amount specified per Shift)  If on the second screen, unit of measure is per hour, is selected  then Use the Shift compensation formula specified as part of this story, if  unit of measure is per hour is not selected, then use the Amount .
