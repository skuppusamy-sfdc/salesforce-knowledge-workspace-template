## Description

**Preconditions:**

- At least one related HPF record exists for the Clinician Contract.

- Field `No_of_Hours_Anticipated_to_Work__c` is populated on related HPF(s).

### **Steps to Reproduce:**

1. Log in as a Recruiter in the Corporate Support apps.

1. Navigate to a Clinician Contract record.

1. Ensure that associated HPF/Provider Network Contract records exist and have values populated for `No_of_Hours_Anticipated_to_Work__c`.

1. Look for the field `Anticipated Clinical Hours` on the Clinician Contract record detail view.

### ❌ **Expected Result:**

** A **read-only** field labeled `Anticipated Clinical Hours` should be **visible* on the Clinician Contract UI.

** It should display the **roll-up sum* of the field `No_of_Hours_Anticipated_to_Work__c` from all related HPF records.

### ❗ **Actual Result:**

** The field `Anticipated Clinical Hours` is **not visible* on the Clinician Contract record page.(its there in backend/)
