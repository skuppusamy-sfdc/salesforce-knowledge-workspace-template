## Description

As a system administrator, when adding members to an Account or Deal/Opportunity team, I want users to use the most updated Team Roles so that we have a concise view of team members on accounts and opportunities.

## Acceptance Criteria

AC1: Add Team Role

As a system administrator within the Corporate Support apps

When user wants to add a Account or Deal Team (opportunity team) member

Then they should be able to add Team Role = “Virtual Health Program Manager”

AC2: Remove Team Role

As a system administrator within the Corporate Support apps

When user wants to add a Account or Deal Team (opportunity team) member

Then they should **NOT** be able to add Team Role = “SVP eHealth Solutions Core”

NOTE: removing Team Role = “SVP eHealth Solutions Core” per 9/5 Client Offboarding tasks discussion, replacing with new role = “Virtual Health Program Manager”.
