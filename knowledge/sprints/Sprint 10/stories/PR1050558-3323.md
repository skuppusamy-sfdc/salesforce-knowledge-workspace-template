## Description

**Preconditions:**

- Clinician record exists with Professional Title = NP/PA, Employment Type = Employee.

- HPFs with valid PCA per-hour rates (e.g., 80 and 90) exist for averaging.

- User logged in as PayrollUser with access to Clinician Contact request

**Steps to Reproduce:**

1. Login as PayrollUser.

1. Navigate: App Launcher → Corporate Support Apps → Clinician Contacts.

1. Open a valid NP/PA Employee clinician record.

1. Start New Contact Request → Select HPFs.

1. Navigate to Contract Details / Compensation Details section.

1. Review calculated fields after saving the reques

**Expected Result:**

- The following fields should display in the UI per requirement:

*** **EE NP/PA Hourly Blended* = Hourly Rate averaged across all HPFs.

*** **EE NP/PA Full Time Overage Rate* = Hourly Rate averaged across all HPFs + $2.

*** **EE NP/PA Full Time Weekly Blended** = Hourly Rate averaged across all HPFs ** 40.

*** **EE NP/PA Full Time Monthly Blended** = EE NP/PA Hourly Blended ** NP/PA Tier.

**Actual Result:**

** **EE NP/PA Full Time Weekly Blended** field is **not present in UI*.
