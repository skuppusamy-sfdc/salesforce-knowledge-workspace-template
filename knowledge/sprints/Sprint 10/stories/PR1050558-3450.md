## Description

Steps to Reproduce:

1-Login as SFO and navigate to the Service account record.

2-Ensure on Service account cloned contract exists which has Shift coverage true.

3-Now navigate to PCA shift record and verify on old/cloned PCA record, Shift record value is coming from parent contract.

Issue - Duplicates records are getting created because of Cloned Shift records PCA. 

Expected Result

Duplicates records shouldn't be getting created because of Cloned Shift records PCA.

Actual Result

1. Duplicates records are getting created because of Cloned Shift records PCA. Shift record on the cloned PCA is populated with the Shift record of parent contract instead of cloned shift record

1. Cloned From field on the cloned shift record is not populated

## Root Cause

As, It was not specifically mentioned in the solution, what all fields needed to be cloned, and what has to be replaced with new field after asking the same multiple times. It was told to clone all the fields except few and this(Shift field on PCA) one wasn’t under exceptions. So, it was referring to the previous shift field instead of newly cloned one. 

Due to time constraint for development, as solution was properly written on Friday, I also didn’t get time to think about it and do it from my side only. I just did what was asked as mentioned above. If this had been mentioned clearly on solution what all fields need to be cloned and what not for every object. I wouldn’t have cloned it.
