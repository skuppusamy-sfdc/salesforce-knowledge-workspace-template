## Description

As a Recruiter, when I clone Clinician Contract rates, and I “Add HPF” for the same service account (eg. Facility Title change), then  I want the system to copy rates from the current/cloned contract to the new HPF, so that I ensure I am offering the same rate as the current contract. 

Also, I want to update the naming convention for Contract Payment Agreement records, so that we avoid duplicates when adding an HPF with the same Service Account as a cloned HPF.

AC3: Copy clinician rates from old contract to new HPF (same service account)

Given that I am a Recruiter & Clinician Experience Specialist in the Corporate Support apps

When I navigate to a **Clinician** **Contract** record in Status = Draft that was “Start From Previous Active Contract” (clone option - see story 1070 for rules)

And I select the Summary tab

And click “**Add HPF**” and add an HPF for the same Facility (service account) as the Cloned HPF, but **Facility Title is different**

And I clicked “Configure Rates” for the newly added HPF (green highlighted)

Then the system should **display the** +**higher**+ **rates from the cloned clinician contract or Client/Facility rate** based on **Type** (Type of Rate ≠ Shift) OR **Shift Full Name** (Type of Rate = Shift).

And **user can edit the Rate** as long of the Override Prohibited is NOT checked. 

NOTE: Type of Rate = Bonus is NOT copied over per story <2593>

Example:

1. **Client** Contract 1 for Service Account A

## EE Lead Midlevel Provider - Day 1 rate = $100

## EE Nurse Practitioner - Day 1 rate = $100

1. **Clinician** **Contract 1** (created off of client contract 1)

## Service Account A, EE Lead Midlevel Provider - Day 1 rate = $150

1. **Clinician** **Contract 2** Facility Title change from “EE Lead Midlevel Provider” to “EE Nurse Practitioner” (clone of Clinician Contract 1)

## Service Account A, EE Lead Midlevel Provider - **Day 1** rate = **$150** **(Cloned Rate from HPF)**

## User Clicks **“Add HPF”** to add new prospect HPF for same Service Account A, but change in Facility Title = EE Nurse Practitioner. 

### On the Summary tab: Cloned HPF is Gray and newly “Add HPF” is Green

## User clicks on the green HPF and clicks Configure Rates

### On rate details step,

#### **Current/old** **process** - we would display the rate from the Client Contract: 

##### Service Account A, EE Nurse Practitioner - Day 1 rate = $100 (clone from client) 

#### **New** **process** - we display the rate from the cloned clinician contract based on matching the Type (Type of Rate ≠Shift) or Shift Full Name (Type of Rate = Shift).

##### Service Account A, EE Nurse Practitioner - **Day 1** rate = **$150**

## Solution

This functionality may already exist. Please test before developing.

As part of the existing Clone functionality of a clinician contract, 

If executed Clinician contract exists and we submit a new contract request, 

check if the SA of the HPF is same as the existing  contract’s ( cloned) HPF

Check if the Facility title is different

If both conditions are true, 

Copy the rate from the existing contract’s HPF> CPA> PCA rate based on matching facility title

As part of Configure rates, match the HPF based on same SA. Match the CPA based on Type__c and use the appropriate field as key match.,

If Type__c = Shift, PCA.Shift__r.Shift_Full_Name__c

If Type__c != Shift, PCA > Type

Notes

 we display the rate from the cloned clinician contract based on matching the Type (Type of Rate ≠Shift) or Shift Full Name (Type of Rate = Shift).

Add to configure rates button to copy rate$ to HPF with same service account(HPF)
