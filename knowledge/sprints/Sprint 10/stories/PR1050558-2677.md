## Description

As a Medical Staff Liaison, I need to terminate a clinician’s employment at a specific facility in Salesforce, so that I can accurately update the status of employees at facilities that SCP staffs for

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a **Medical Staff Liaison, CET user, or Data Quality team member** in the Corporate Support apps

When I view a **Clinician** record

Then I am able to select Initiate Termination Request and see the following:

- Facility Selection  [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558701|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558701|smartlink]

- Termination Details - 

*** Answer each question below per facility (when HPF status is not Prospect or Inactive AND Employment Type is **Partner*):

*** Date Termination Received (date, required)

**** Help Text: Date termination request was received

*** Termination Request Date (date, automatically populated to today)

**** Help Text: Date termination request was submitted or automated

*** Last Day (date, required)

**** Help Text: Agreement termination date

*** Voluntary Termination (checkbox, optional)

*** Reason for Termination (picklist dependent on Employment Type AND Voluntary Termination, required)

**** Employment Type = Partner and Voluntary = TRUE: Lack Of Advancement, Compensation, Management, Career Change, Retirement, School, Working Conditions, Wrong Job, Mutual Agreement, Status Change  Provider Use Only, Payment Relationship  K-1 Use Only, Family Reasons, Provider Unresponsive, Location, Hours of Coverage, Accepted Another Job, Deceased, Not Disclosed, Work Environment, Lack of Flexibility, Job Abandonment

**** Employment Type = Partner and Voluntary = FALSE: Acquisition/Merger, Attendance, Contract Termination, Misconduct, Performance, Reduction in Force, Reorganization, Gross Misconduct, Violation of Company Policy, Provider Inactivity >12 Months, Privileges Expired, Coverage Discontinued, ADA Accommodation Denied

*** Exit Interview Requirement (checkbox, optional)

*** Bonus Repayment (checkbox, optional)

*** Backfill Recommended (checkbox, optional)

*** Ability to attach files

- Clinician Offboarding case is created with Rehirable checkbox defaulted to TRUE

And Update Facility Offboarding task below to send to HR Queue for Employment Type = Employee**, or Partner**

|Review Contract for Termination|Parent Case = Facility Offboarding|When Employment Type = Employee**, or Partner** HR Queue

All others, Transition Specialist Queue|

## Solution

As part of the existing Clinician offboarding OS,

For Emp type = Partner, ( add to existing condition)

Display the fields mentioned above 

Facility Offboarding Task

Update the critieria for task creation to include Partner Employee type
