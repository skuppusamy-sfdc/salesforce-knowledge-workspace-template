## Description

As a HR team member, I want an email notification to be sent when a Clinician changes Employment Types, so that key teams can manually coordinate downstream updates.

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a HR team member within the Corporate Support App

When a Clinician already has an executed contract and a **NEW contract gets “Executed”** for the clinician with a different employment type

Then in the following scenarios an email notification will be automatically sent: 

** Old Clinician Contract Employment Type = **Employee** → New Contract Employment Type = **Independent Contractor*

** Old Clinician Contract Employment Type = **Independent Contractor**  → New Contract Employment Type = **Employee*

** Old Clinician Contract Employment Type = **Independent Contractor** or **Employee** → New Contract Employment Type = **Leased*

And the email shall include:

- Recipient: [HR@SCPhealth.com|mailto:HR@SCPhealth.com]

- Email Subject: Employment Type Change for <Clinician> 

- Email Body: 

[Clinician Name] ([Global Provider ID]) is changing Employment Type. Kindly coordinate with the relevant stakeholders and initiate the necessary preparations for processing.

Thank you for your attention to this matter.

Example: 

Jane Doe (1234567890) is changing Employment Type. Kindly coordinate with the relevant stakeholders and initiate the necessary preparations for processing.

Thank you for your attention to this matter.

** Old Clinician Contract Employment Type = **Employee** → New Contract Employment Type = **Independent Contractor*

** Old Clinician Contract Employment Type = **Independent Contractor**  → New Contract Employment Type = **Employee*

** Old Clinician Contract Employment Type = **Independent Contractor** or **Employee** → New Contract Employment Type = **Leased*

then, Send email action

- Recipient: [HR@SCPhealth.com|mailto:HR@SCPhealth.com]

- Email Subject: Employment Type Change for <Clinician>

- Email Body: 

[Clinician Name] ([Global Provider ID]) is changing Employment Type. Kindly coordinate with the relevant stakeholders and initiate the necessary preparations for processing.

Thank you for your attention to this matter.

Use Clinician.Global_Provider_ID__c

Set Recipient Email Address: Setup > email alerts > Send Employment Change Notification -> edit and under **Additional Emails give the HR email or any email that can be used for testing**
