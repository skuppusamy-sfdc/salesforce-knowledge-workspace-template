## Description

As a Sales VP, when I identify that an Opportunity is “Green Light for Client Outreach”, then information needs to go to Asymbl, so that we can start early onboarding activities.

## Acceptance Criteria

AC1: Job Requisition Handoff to Asymbl

Given I am a **Sales VP** in the Corporate Support Apps

When I navigate to an **Opportunity** record (RT: Growth Opportunity)

And I have updated “Green Light for Client Outreach” to Checked/True

Then the system shall create one task per Service Account (Opportunity Services in Active) and assigned to the **VP Operations** based on the Account Team role. 

- Subject: Create Job Req for <Service Account Name>

** Assigned To: **VP Operations*

- Status: Open

- Due Date: <3 days from create date>

- Priority: Normal

- Comments: <1. Navigate to the Service Account in the system. 2. Click on the 'Related' tab to view connected pieces such as contracts, accounts, and opportunities. 3. Click 'New' on the job layout to initiate job creation.>

- Related To: <Service Account>

And if the VP Operations team member has not been setup, then default the Assigned To = **Sales VP** (user who gave the “green light” the one there make this change)

Note: **VP Operations** Account Team role should be already added to the Service Account at this point.

## Solution

Use Service__c.Account__c to get the Service Account

Use the records triggered flow for Update Opportunity

Create Task records based on AC
