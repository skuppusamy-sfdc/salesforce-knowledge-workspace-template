## Description

### **Description**

When generating PCA rate plans, the **Bonus Rate** is not being calculated. Currently:

** `EE_NPPA_Hourly_Blended__c` = **Average Hourly Rate* (working correctly).

** `EE_NPPA_Full_Time_Overage_Rate__c` = **Average Hourly Rate + 2* (working correctly).

** **Bonus Rate** = **Not calculated* (missing or blank).

All rates for PCA have **Unit of Measure = Per Hour**. The expected calculation for Average = **Sum / No. of PCA**

### **Preconditions**

** Rate Plan exists with 3 Rate Types: **Bonus, Variable, Target*.

- PCA records available with Hourly Rates.

- User has access to view Rate Plans and related fields.

### **Steps to Reproduce**

1. Login as **payroll User**.

1. Navigate to **Contracts → PCA Rate Plans**.

1. Open a rate plan with Rate Types = Bonus, Variable, Target.

1. Verify values of `EE_NPPA_Hourly_Blended__c` and `EE_NPPA_Full_Time_Overage_Rate__c`.

1. Check for Bonus Rate calculation.

### **Expected Result**

** **Hourly Blended Rate* = Avg Hourly Rate.

** **Full-Time Overage Rate* = Avg Hourly Rate + 2.

** **Bonus Rate* = Should calculate as per formula defined (e.g., Avg Hourly Rate + Bonus logic).

- All 3 rate plans (Bonus, Variable, Target) should calculate consistently for PCA.

actual result  

**Bonus Rate is not calculated**
