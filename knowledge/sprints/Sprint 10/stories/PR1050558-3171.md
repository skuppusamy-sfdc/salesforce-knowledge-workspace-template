## Description

**Steps to reproduce:**

1. Login as Recruiter

1. Open a Clinician Contract record in ‘Approved’ status and having multiple HPF records

1. Click “Generate Term Sheet” button

**Expected Result:**

Locations value on the Term Sheet document should be populated only for contract’s active HPFs **Service Account** name

**Actual Result:**

Locations value on the Term Sheet document is populated even with contract’s HPFs **Service Account** name when HPF status != Active
