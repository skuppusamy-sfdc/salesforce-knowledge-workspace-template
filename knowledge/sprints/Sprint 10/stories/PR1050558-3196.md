## Description

**Steps to reproduce:**

1. Login as Recruiter

1. Open a Clinician Contract record in ‘Approved’ status and having multiple HPF records

1. Click “Generate Term Sheet” button

**Expected Result:**

1. Term Sheet document should be generated with all required details - Compensation for EE Full Time/Part Time contract should be in the format [EE NP/PA Full Time Monthly Blended] per Month or [EE NP/PA Hourly Blended] per Hour

1. Job details should be populated on the Term Sheet document for multiple HPFs

1. File name should be in the following format when there are multiple HPFs on the contract <ClinicianFullName><DateSent>Termsheet.pdf

**Actual Result:**

1. Compensation for EE Full Time/Part Time contract does not have the measure populated

1. Job details are not populated at all when there are multiple HPFs on the contract - AC change

1. File name of the generated term sheet of contract with multiple HPFs is coming as <ClinicianFullName>null<DateSent>Termsheet.pdf  Not an issue as this was based on the Suggestion mentioned in AC but was never finalized
