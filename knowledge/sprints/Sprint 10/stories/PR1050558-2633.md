## Description

As a user, I need the ability to view a component that highlights the status(es) of a clinician, so that I am able to understand where they are during their journey with SCP Health.

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am an a SCP Corporate Support user in the Corporate Support apps,

When I navigate to the Person Account Clinician record At a Glance tab,

Then I see: (NOTE: these are UPDATES to existing At a Glance)

- A key/legend at the top displaying:

** Grey Circle Icon “Not Started”

** Blue + White Circle Icon “In Progress”

** Green Circle with Check Icon “Completed”

- In the Applicant section:

** For the following Status/Labels, once it is marked “Completed” it should NOT revert back to “Not Started” or “In Progress” as the ATS Applicant record stage progresses.

*** Application Submitted

**** Show Completed when ATS Applicant Stage = “Submission”

***** Still show Completed when ATS Applicant Stage progress to Stage = “Recruiter Screen”, “Awaiting Intake Form”, “Presenting”, “Interview”, “Offer”, “Term Sheet”, “Contracting”, “Placement”

*** Recruiter Screen

**** Show Completed when ATS Applicant Stage = “Recruiter Screen”

***** Still show Completed when ATS Applicant Stage progress to Stage = “Awaiting Intake Form”, “Presenting”, “Interview”, “Offer”, “Term Sheet”, “Contracting”, “Placement”

*** Candidate Presentation

**** Show Completed when ATS Applicant Stage = “Interview”

***** Still show Completed when ATS Applicant Stage progress to Stage = “Offer”, “Term Sheet”, “Contracting”, “Placement”

*** Verbal Offer

**** Show Completed when ATS Applicant Stage = “Term Sheet”

***** Still show Completed when ATS Applicant Stage progress to Stage = “Contracting”, “Placement”

*** Term Sheet

**** Show Completed when ATS Applicant Stage = “Contracting”

***** Still show Completed when ATS Applicant Stage progress to Stage = “Placement”

Given a Clinician in the HC Portal

When I navigate to the Home tab, and view At a Glance information

Then I see:

- A key/legend at the top displaying:

** Grey Circle Icon “Not Started”

** Blue + White Circle Icon “In Progress”

- In the Applicant section:

** For the following Status/Labels, once it is marked “Completed” it should NOT revert back to “Not Started” or “In Progress” as the ATS Applicant record stage progresses.

*** Job Application In Process

**** Show Completed when ATS Applicant Stage = “Submission”

***** Still show Completed when ATS Applicant Stage progress to Stage = “Recruiter Screen”, “Awaiting Intake Form”, “Presenting”, “Interview”, “Offer”, “Term Sheet”, “Contracting”, “Placement”

*** Recruiter Screen

**** Show Completed when ATS Applicant Stage = “Recruiter Screen”

***** Still show Completed when ATS Applicant Stage progress to Stage = “Awaiting Intake Form”, “Presenting”, “Interview”, “Offer”, “Term Sheet”, “Contracting”, “Placement”

*** Offer In Process

***** Still show Completed when ATS Applicant Stage progress to Stage = “Contracting”, “Placement”
