## Description

As a Medical Staff Liaison, I need to terminate a clinician’s employment at a specific facility in Salesforce, so that I can accurately update the status of employees at facilities that SCP staffs for

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a **Medical Staff Liaison, CET user, or Data Quality team member** in the Corporate Support apps

When I view a **Clinician** record

Then I am able to select Initiate Termination Request and see the following: 

- Facility Selection  [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558701|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558701|smartlink] 

- Termination Details  originally built in [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558306|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558306|smartlink] 

** Answer each question below per facility (when HPF status is not Prospect or Inactive AND Employment Type is Employee or Independent Contractor):

*** Date Termination Received (date, required)

**** Help Text: Date termination request was received

*** Termination Request Date (date, automatically populated to today)

**** Help Text: Date termination request was submitted or automated

**** Last Day (date, required) **update placement to after Termination Request Date*

**** Help Text: Agreement termination date

*** Voluntary Termination (checkbox, optional)

*** Reason for Termination (picklist dependent on Employment Type AND Voluntary Termination, required)

***** **Employment Type = Employee and Voluntary = TRUE*: Lack Of Advancement, Compensation, Management, Career Change, Retirement, School, Working Conditions, Wrong Job, Mutual Agreement, Status Change  Provider Use Only, Payment Relationship  K-1 Use Only, Family Reasons, Provider Unresponsive, Location, Hours of Coverage, Accepted Another Job, Deceased, Not Disclosed, Work Environment, Lack of Flexibility, Job Abandonment

***** **Employment Type = Employee and Voluntary = FALSE*: Acquisition/Merger, Attendance, Contract Termination, Misconduct, Performance, Reduction in Force, Reorganization, Gross Misconduct, Violation of Company Policy, Provider Inactivity >12 Months, Privileges Expired, Coverage Discontinued, ADA Accommodation Denied

***** **Employment Type = Independent Contractor*: Agreement Not Returned, Coverage Discontinued, Deceased, Hospital App Not Returned, Hospital Contract Termination, Insurance Expired, Past Issues with Facility, PEP Not Returned, Per Hospital, Per Provider. Per Risk Manager, Privilege Expiration

*** Exit Interview Requirement (checkbox, optional)

*** Bonus Repayment (checkbox, optional)

**** **Backfill Recommended (checkbox, optional)*

*** Ability to attach files

** **Clinician Offboarding case is created with Rehirable checkbox defaulted to TRUE*

## Solution

on Case, Reason for Termination - the options mentioned should be added.

On the omniscript created as part of 306, 

update placement of last day field to after Termination Request Date

Add Backfill Recommended field (checkbox, optional)

Ensure Clinician Offboarding case is created with Rehirable checkbox defaulted to TRUE

Add additional filtering option for Reason for Termination picklist option.
