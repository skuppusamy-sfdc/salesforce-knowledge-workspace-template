## Description

As a Recruiter, I want to see the facility rate (where applicable) compared to the clinician rate, so that I can understand the amount overrate a clinician is

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a Recruiter in the Corporate Support apps

When I create a Clinician Contract

And I select rates that have a facility rate

Then the facility rate is populated from Client Contract onto the Clinician Contract 

**UNLESS the clinician contract has been cloned (then show previous clinician contract rate built in user story** [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505582822|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505582822|smart-link]

Notes:

Looking to see a comparison and the change. 

Must have in the **approval**, ability see current and New rate. (note: user will be able to view on the Summary tab)

Not as important to see the diff on the Clinician contract UI

## Solution

Context: When a clinician contract is created, and the rates (that are derived from practice operations contract) are modified, an approval process is triggered. The approvers want the ability to view the old rate (from PO contract that was changed) and the new rate, before approving.

 Similar to Client Contract, In Summary scree, where we display the rate that was changed, FlexCard : **SpecifyRates_RoleRate**

Pass the client contract’s CPA, (instead of the cloned contract’s cpa). If the value is different from current contract’s rate, strikeout the client contract’s rate.
