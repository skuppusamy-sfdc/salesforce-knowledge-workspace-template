## Description

As a Medical Staff Liaison, Data Quality & Clinician Experience Specialist, once the legal team has completed the “Begin Facility Termination”, then on the **Client Contract’s Termination Date**, I want the Clinician Facility Offboarding Cases/tasks to be automatically created, so that HPFs can be inactivated and Clinician Contracts can be terminated as needed.

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a Medical Staff Liaison, Data Quality & Clinician Experience Specialist in the Corporate Support apps

When a Legal team member has completed the “Begin Facility Termination” guided experience

Then if the related Service Account(s) on the Client Contract (RT: Client Contract) has a “Termination Date”, then on the **night of** the “Termination Date” the the following actions shall occur: 

** For **each HPF (not in Status = Inactive) related to the terminated Service Account** the system shall automatically create the **Clinician Offboarding Cases* with the following information based on Employment Type: [leverage functionality build in [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505581727|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505581727|smart-link]]

** Employment Type = Employee, Independent Contractor, Partner

*** Date Termination Received = <PNC’s Termination Date> 

*** Termination Request Date  = <PNC’s Termination Date>

*** Last Day = <PNC’s Termination Date> 

**** Help Text: Agreement termination date

*** Voluntary Termination = True/Checked

*** Reason for Termination = “Hospital Contract Termination”

*** Exit Interview Requirement = False/Unchecked

*** Bonus Repayment = True/Checked

** Employment Type = Leased, Locum, Managed

*** Date Termination Received = <PNC’s Termination Date> 

*** Termination Request Date  = <PNC’s Termination Date> 

*** Last Day = <PNC’s Termination Date> 

**** Help Text: Agreement termination date

*** Voluntary Termination = True/Checked

*** Reason for Termination = “Hospital Contract Termination”

Then after the current existing Clinician Offboarding automation/functionality will begin for auto Task Creation, Case Closure, Healthcare Practitioner Facility updates (including inactivation on “Effective To” date)  [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505581728|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505581728|smartlink],  [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505582421|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505582421|smartlink], [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505582473|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505582473|smart-link] 

-And the system shall update the **Clinician’s Contract Status = Canceled & Cancel Reason = Terminated** when: [**NEW**]-

** ALL the HPF records related to the **Clinician Contract* record are in Status = Inactive.  this is updated as part of offboarding automation

Note: Unlike the “Initiate Termination Request” process initiate by the Medical Staff Liaison, Data Quality & Clinician Experience Specialist, the Client Termination can be months out, so it was decided that the creation of Clinician Offboarding cases should Only be triggered on the Date of Client Termination.
