## Description

**Steps to reproduce:**

1. Login as Recruiter - Myrtle Will

1. Open a clinician contract record

1. Configure the rates such that the Leadership Incentives is over the facility rate

1. Click ‘Submit Contract Request’ button from Summary tab

1. Open another clinician contract record

1. Configure rates for other bonus type

1. Click ‘Submit Contract Request’ button from Summary tab

**Expected Result:**

1. First contract should be submitted to VPO for approval

1. Second contract should be submitted to VPO and Tactical Team queue for approval

**Actual Result:**

1. Contract is not submitted to VPO for approval when Leadership Incentives is over the facility rate

1. Contract is submitted only to Tactical Team queue for approval when other bonus rate is added

## Root Cause

During development of US1750, the solution specified checking only `Stipend`, `Shift`, `Variable`, and `Target` record types for overrate. However, after the SEP 30 standup call with [~accountid:712020:7c226bec924b4638a65d-349a3d91b34b]  and [~accountid:61f8618e8d9e3c00688e380b] , it was clarified that **all types except** `Bounce` **with** `Over_Rate__c = 1` should be considered for overrate approval.
