## Description

As a MSL or CET, I need offboarding task = “Update Records and Notify Teams" to be created and assigned to Transition Specialist Queue, so that they can be notified to complete necessary activities

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a MSL or clinician experience specialist in the Corporate Support apps

When the Offboarding Guided Experience is completed

Then the following task is created:

- Update Records and Notify Teams  row 11 of [+Clinician Offboarding Cases and Tasks sheet+|https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/14PPkb2n7X13nbwyjKCH4jGUWAdaFpmlXYkoM_Rm2MM/edit?gid=803877699#gid=803877699], updates detailed below and highlighted in green on sheet:

** Task Subject change from “Review Contract for Termination” to “Update Records and Notify Teams”

** Task Assigned To change from “"When Employment Type = Employee, or Partner HR Queue; All others, Transition Specialist Queue" to “Transition Specialist Queue”

** When To Create add criteria for “AND a Contract record exists in Status = Executed”

Modifications to existing solution that was built as part of 1728
