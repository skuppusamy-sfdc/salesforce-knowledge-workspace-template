## Description

Steps to Reproduce:

1-Login as SFO and navigate to a PO contract record.

2-Ensure it has Shift records.

3-Now from summary tab start the Specify Rates flow.

4-Select Type of Rate as Shift and fill all the details on all the screens.

5-On last step click next and navigate back to and fro.

Issue -  Upon navigating back and forth on Rate Details screen on the Specify Rates flow, the Shifts records are getting deselected.

Expected Result - Upon navigating back and forth on Rate Details screen on the Specify Rates flow, the Shifts records shouldn't be deselected.

Actual Result - Upon navigating back and forth on Rate Details screen on the Specify Rates flow, the Shifts records are getting deselected.

This is an enhancement for better user experience, fixed the issue by enhancing the data flow approach
