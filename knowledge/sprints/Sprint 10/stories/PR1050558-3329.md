## Description

#### Title

Anticipated Clinical Hours (Hour Commitment) entered during Clinician Contract Request is **not mapping** to `**_No_of_Hours_Anticipated_to_Workc**_` (HPF field) — field shows **0** after contract creation; history tracking not recorded.

#### Preconditions

1. UI for capturing **Anticipated Clinical Hours** (PR1050558-2791) is deployed and accessible.

1. Data migration field `**_No_of_Hours_Anticipated_to_Workc**_` exists on HPF/Contract object.

1. History tracking should be enabled for `**_No_of_Hours_Anticipated_to_Workc**_` but may not be turned on yet.

1. Clinician is NP/PA, Employee, **non-full time** (only these should capture hour commitment).

#### Steps to Reproduce (exact navigation)

1. Login as **Recruiter** (or PayrollUser).

1. App Launcher → **Corporate Support Apps** → **Clinician Contacts**.

1. Open target **Clinician** record (Professional Title = NP/PA; Employment Type = Employee; Time Type = Part Time).

1. Click **Start New Contact Request** (or **Initiate Contract Request**).

1. Proceed through flow to **Select HPF**.

1. On HPF/Contract Details screen, locate **Anticipated Clinical Hours / Hour Commitment** field.

1. Enter a valid value: **120** (3-digit integer, no decimals). Save.

1. Complete the Contract Request flow and **create/submit** the Contract (finish the flow so Contract record is created).

1. Navigate to the created **Contract**

#### Actual Result

** After contract creation the mapped field `**_No_of_Hours_Anticipated_to_Workc**_` displays **0* (zero) instead of the entered value (`120`).

** Field history shows **no entry* for the Anticipated Clinical Hours change (history tracking not recorded).

#### Expected Result

** The value entered in the Clinician Contract Request flow for **Anticipated Clinical Hours** (e.g., `120`) must be **mapped** and **persisted** to the data-migration target field `**_No_of_Hours_Anticipated_to_Workc*_` on the HPF/CPA/Contract record after contract creation.

- Field history tracking must record the change (old value, new value, user, timestamp).
