## Description

As a Contracting specialist, I need the ability to view the Practice operations contract details when I am on the Clinician contract, so that i can make a sidebyside comparison of the changes that were made as part of Account management process.

## Acceptance Criteria

Given, I am a contracting specialist on the Summary tab of a Draft Clinician contract,

When I click on a HPF (from the side)

Then, I am able to view the Practice Operation’s Summary page view for that Facility title ( filtered by HPF.Facility title)

## Solution

On the summary tab of Clinician Contract, Add the flexcard of PO Contract Summary Card.

Add a filter parameter to the PO Summary card, so that when the Facility is selected on the left side, that facility/Service Account’s and Facility Title details of Contract Summary is visible on the PO Contract summary.

FlexCard **SpecifyRates_SummaryContractScreen**, and its child cards

ip : SpecifyRates_PaginatedRates

The card might need a event listener to get the selected HPF details (from left) and use that as filter to DR

NOTE: The Facility rate can be used to determine the CPA of PO Contract.

To get the correct Practice Operation Contract use

(Practice Operation)Contract.StartDate  <= Clinician Contract Start Date  AND (Practice Operation)Contract.Status = Executed AND HPF.AccountId = (Practice Operation)Contract.AccountId Order By Contract.StartDate DESC LIMIT 1
