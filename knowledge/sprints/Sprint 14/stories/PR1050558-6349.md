## Description

**Steps to reproduce:**

1. Login to Core HC org as Recruiter - Myrtle Will

1. Open a clinician record having Superseded HPF

1. Create a new clinician contract with same Employment Type as the Superseded HPF (Contracting Status = ‘Terminated’)

1. On the new clinician contract in ‘Draft’ status, click ‘Add HPF’ button from Summary tab

1. Verify the HPF(s) displayed

1. Add the new HPF with Facility Title change and mark the contract as Executed

1. Now, using ‘Contract Request’ button create a new clinician contract

1. Verify the HPFs on the new clinician contract

**Expected Result:**

1. Superseded HPF should not be displayed when user clicks ‘Add HPF’ button on the clinician contract

1. When a clinician contract is cloned, ‘Superseded’ HPF should not be linked to the new contract

**Actual Result:**

1. Superseded HPF is displayed when user clicks ‘Add HPF’ button on the clinician contract

1. When a clinician contract is cloned, ‘Superseded’ HPF is also linked to the new contract

## Acceptance Criteria

AC1: Updates Facility selection (original story [+PR1050558893+|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558893]) for a new contract & 'Add HPF' 

Given I am Recruiter within the Corporate Support apps

When I either:

** From a Clinician record, click ‘Contract Request’ & select ‘**Start New Request*’

OR

** From a Draft Clinician Contract’s Summary Tab,  click ‘**Add HPF*’

And I am presented with the ‘**Facility Selection**’ screen/step

Then the system shall display a list of the Clinician’s Healthcare Practitioner Facility (HPF) records for that clinician where Status = ‘Prospect’, ‘Candidate’, ‘Waiting to be Scheduled’, ‘Active’, ‘WTBS Here-WRKG at Sister Site’; **Excludes Status** = ‘Inactive’, **‘****Superseded****’**

NOTE for AC1: All existing functionality stands, this story is just to update the filtering of HPFs displayed. 

AC2: Update contract cloning to exclude HPFs both Status = ‘Inactive’ & ‘Superseded’, update in **blue**

Given I am Recruiter within the Corporate Support apps

When I am on a Clinician record

And select ‘Contract Request’

And select ‘Start From Previous Active Contract’

Then the system shall NOT clone HPF in status = ‘Inactive' or **'****Superseded****’**

## Solution

**Components:** 

DEFetchCPAAndPCARecords - Add filter for “Superseded“ so HPFs are not being retrieve 

DEFetchContractAndPNC - Add filter for “Superseded“ so HPFs are not being retrieve

DRGetFacilityForAddHpf - Add filter for “Superseded“ so HPFs are not being retrieve
