## Description

A regression has been identified in the **Facility Offboarding Guided Flow**. Currently, the system is populating the HPF selection list with records that have a status of **"Superseded."** By definition, a superseded record is obsolete and has been replaced by a newer version. 

### **Expected Result:**  

The flow should filter out any records with a status of **Inactive** or **Superseded**, displaying only relevant, actionable HPFs.

### **Steps to Reproduce**

1. **Log in** QA org > Open clinician record with HPF in status superseded. 

1. Click on  Inititate termination request flow.

1. Observe the list of HPFs available for selection or review.
