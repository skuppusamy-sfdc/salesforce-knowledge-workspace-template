## Description

As a Clinician Contracting Specialist, I want there to be a way to have Billing Only HPF(s) flagged, so that when I generate a Clinician Contract Agreement (document) those HPF can be excluded. 

As a Data Quality team member, I need Billing Only HPFs to be included in the contract, so that when the contract is executed, HPF information is sent to downstream systems and location is available for scheduling, revenue cycle, and payroll.

## Acceptance Criteria

AC1: **Service Account** - Add Billing Only indicator & History Tracking

Given I am Data Quality Team member within the Corporate Support App

When I am on a Service Account record

Than I have the ability to edit checkbox field (default unchecked) = “**Billing Only Account**”  

And other personas with access to the Service Account records can view, but they can NOT edit this field (except System Administrators & Data Quality persona)

And History Tracking is enabled for this new field

AC2: **Healthcare Practitioner Facility (HPF)** - Add Billing Only indicator & History Tracking

Given I am Data Quality Team member within the Corporate Support App

When I am on a Healthcare Practitioner Facility (HPF) record

Than I can see new checkbox field = “**Billing Only (Service Account)**”

And upon initial creation defaulted to match the value of the related Service Account field = “Billing Only Account” 

And I have the ability to edit field = “Billing Only (Service Account)”

And other personas with access to the HPF records can view, but they can NOT edit this field (except System Administrators & Data Quality persona)

And History Tracking is enable for this new field

AC3: **Clinician Contract** - Display Billing Only indicator

Given I am a Recruiter within the Corporate Support Apps
