## Description

**Steps to reproduce:**

1. Login to Core HC org as Recruiter - Myrtle Will

1. Open a clinician record having Superseded HPF

1. Create a new clinician contract with same Employment Type as the Superseded HPF (Contracting Status = ‘Terminated’)

1. On the new clinician contract in ‘Draft’ status, click ‘Add HPF’ button from Summary tab

1. Add the new HPF with Facility Title change and delete the old HPF

1. Mark the contract as Executed

1. Verify the Status and Contracting Status of the old HPF

**Expected Result:**

Old HPF should be marked ‘Superseded’ when the clinician contract with new HPF (Facility Title change) is Executed

**Actual Result:**

Status and Contracting Status of the old HPF is not updated when the old HPF is delinked from the clinician contract with new HPF (Facility Title change) and is then Executed

Record: [https://corehc-qa.sandbox.lightning.force.com/lightning/r/Account/001gP00000HtqaeQAB/view|https://corehc-qa.sandbox.lightning.force.com/lightning/r/Account/001gP00000HtqaeQAB/view]

## Acceptance Criteria

AC1: When creating a contract for a Facility Title change, system should update old HPF as ‘Superseded’ when the contract gets ‘Executed’

Given I am a Recruiter

When I am on a Clinician record

And the Clinician has an Executed clinician contract

And I select guided experience = Contract Request 

And I select to ‘Start from Previous Active Contract' 

And from the ‘Summary' tab I ‘Add HPF’ (add HPF for same service account & Old HPF color turns gray)

Then I **can’t** the 'delete’ (trash can icon) on the Old HPF (gray color hpf)

NOTE: Previous functionality should still work after this update.

User should NOT delete the the Old HPF from the new contract, they leave it so that for historical purposes you can see that in the new contract the ‘Gray’ colored HPF was being removed from the contract.

## Solution

Remove the delete button for the Greyed Out PNCs
