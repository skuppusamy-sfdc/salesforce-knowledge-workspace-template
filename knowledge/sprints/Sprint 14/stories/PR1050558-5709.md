## Description

**Tester:** Murphy Alkhatib

**Role:** Recruiter

**Description:** 

Need the system to check if candidate has an email address before the recruiter can move them to awaiting intake on the kanban - we are having issues with recruiters trying to send intakes and the clinician is not getting them, it's not always the case, but in some instances it's because the recruiter was able to move the candidate to awaiting intake without an email address on file

**Business Impact:**

Clinician won't receive the email needed to sign on to the portal and complete the intake form

## Acceptance Criteria

As a recruiter I am presented with a validation rule on the ATS Applicant record asking to provide an email address prior to making their status “Awaiting Intake”

Error Message: “Please provide a valid email address before moving the candidate to “Awaiting Intake”

## Solution

A new validation rule on ATS Applicant

Name: Awaiting Intake Stage

Logic:

If isChanged(bpats__ATS_Stage__c) && bpats__ATS_Stage__c.bpats__Sequence__c = “3” && ISNULL(bpats__Applicant_Email__c) 

Error Location: Top of Page

Error Message: “Please provide a valid email address before moving the candidate to “Awaiting Intake”
