## Description

### Impact

The bug affects the HR team by preventing the creation of offboarding tasks. This impacts the management of activities necessary to end a clinician’s relationship with SCP.

### Expected vs Actual

When the RN/LPN Manager Account Team Member role does not exist on the Account, the system should either assign the Exit Interview task to a fallback owner (e.g., a queue or the Case owner) or surface an error/alert so the team knows the role is missing. The flow silently skips the Exit Interview task creation entirely. No task is created, no error is raised, and no one is notified that the role is missing.

### Steps to reproduce

- Attempt to create offboarding tasks as an HR team member.

- Observe that the tasks do not generate as expected.

### Environment

Dev/QA/UAT/PROD

### Workarounds

No

### Other information

N/A

## Solution

Restore TeamRole “RN/LPN Manager” and deploy from lower to higher orgs
