## Description

Locums clinicians are receiving welcome emails as intended; however, because they do not complete intakes nor do they provide the necessary information in an agreement- they are reaching out to the CET because they are not receiving okta. 

Locums will not have a CES as they should be going through their agency and then to HWL platform for communication. We need to remove them from the welcome email, or find an alternate solution to allow them access to the portal without providing the appropriate level details.

**Business Impact:**

Locum clinicians are confused about the multiple touchpoints, they cannot receive OKTA since they have no info provided, locums are not managed by the CES, and all communication for locum should go through HWL.
