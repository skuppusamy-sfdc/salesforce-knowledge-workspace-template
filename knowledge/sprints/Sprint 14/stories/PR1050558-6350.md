## Description

**AC**:  When an HPF Status set to “Inactive”

Then the system shall update all of the Clinician’s related HPFs for the same Service Account from Status “Superseded” to “Inactive”.

### Steps to Reproduce:

1. Log into QA org.

1. Open Clinician record with 2 HPF for same service Account & status Superseded & Active respectively.

1. Change the HPF status from Active to Inactive.

1. **Observe the status of Superseded HPF. : - It should change the Status from “Superseded” to “Inactive”**.
