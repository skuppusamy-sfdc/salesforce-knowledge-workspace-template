**Do you have or are you able to obtain procedure / case logs showing that you have performed these procedures within the last 2 years?**

- Procedure / Case logs are records that track the number of patients treated and/or procedures performed by a clinician, often used to document competency and meet training requirements. A copy of these logs can be requested from the hospital/employer's medical staff office, quality assurance department, Health Information Management department, or billing office. Typically, these records are only released to the clinician themselves.
