## Description

**Steps to reproduce:**

1. Open a clinician account with atleast 1 HPF in status other than Prospect, Candidate, Superseded or Inactive, Last Date Worked as blank, Hospital_Privileged_Date__c >= 365 days away from current date. ‘Initial Effective Date’ (Clinician Contract) should also be blank

1. Other HPFs should be in Inactive status

1. E_Verify_Status__c should be populated on the clinician record with open tasks and clinicians

1. Verify the status of the HPF and E_Verify_Status__c on clinician after DeactivateHPFsForClinician batch is executed

**Expected Result:**

1. E_Verify_Status__c should be made blank as other HPFs on clinician are inactive

1. Tasks and Cases associated with clinician should be updated to Status = Closed, Closed Reason = Canceled

1. HPF Effective To should be set to 365 days from the HPF’s 'Hospital Privileged Date'

1. HPFs should not be made inactive if any of the sister HPF’s 'Hospital Privileged Date' is less than 365 days

1. Label should be as per DD for Is Sister Site Location, Inactivity Qualified (270 Days) fields which were newly created

**Actual Result:**

1. E_Verify_Status__c is not made blank even if other HPFs on clinician are inactive

1. Tasks and Cases associated with clinician are not closed

1. HPF Effective To is not set to 365 days from the HPF’s 'Hospital Privileged Date'

1. HPFs are made inactive if any of the sister HPF’s 'Hospital Privileged Date' is less than 365 days

1. Label is not as per DD for Is Sister Site Location, Inactivity Qualified (270 Days)
