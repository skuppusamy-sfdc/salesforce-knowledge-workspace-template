## Description

As a Recruiter, I need prospective clinicians to be automatically provisioned access to the portal, so that they can have access to complete tasks, update their information, and access helpful resources

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a Recruiter in the Corporate Support or Corporate Support Console app

When I update the **ATS Stage** field on an **ATS Applicant** record to **Awaiting Intake Form**

And the clinician has not yet been provisioned HC Portal access

Then the clinician is **automatically provisioned HC Portal access**

- If the Email field is not populated, the portal access is not provisioned

this is an update to [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558811|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558811|smart-link]
