## Description

As a Corporate Support user, for cases Clinician Intake Form and Risk Review cases that I have visibility to I need to see additional information, so that I can see when a case was open, closed and the case number.

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a Recruiter in the Corporate Support or Corporate Support Console app

When I navigate to a **Clinician Intake Form** record 

Then I am able see: 

- Header

** Subject is displayed - no additional fields in highlights panel below

- Main Region

** Tabs component: Details (Default), Responses, Files

*** Details tab: fields are displayed according to data dictionary

***** **Add fields to Additional Details section:*

***** Date/Time Opened

***** Date/Time Closed

***** **Add field to the System Information section:*

***** Case Number

*** Responses tab: assessment related list (will hold responses from the Intake Form guided experience)

*** Files tab: standard files related list
