## Description

As a MSL team member, if a Risk Review is denied, then we need to inactive HPFs and close out all outstanding items, so that we keep our system clean.

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a MSL in the Corporate Support apps

When I a Risk Review is updated with Closed Reason = Not Approved ([https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505581651|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505581651|smart-link] ) , via the approval process

Then the following shall happen:

- Update Healthcare Practitioner Facility records where the Risk Review case(s) are Not Approved, to Status = “Inactive”

- Create a Task for Clinical Match Queue

** Subject: Risk Review - Assess Clinical Match

** Description: Assess if clinical match is needed or should be adjusted

** Related To: the clinician associated with the Risk Review

## Solution

Record Triggered Flow on Case of Record Type : Risk Review where Closed_Reason__c was updated to ‘Not approved' , then query the related HPF record (using Healthcare_Practitioner_Facility__c) and update the  HPF.Status__c to 'Inactive’

Create a Task, with WhoId  Clinician, Whatid  the Risk review Case record

- Subject: Risk Review - Assess Clinical Match

- Description: Assess if clinical match is needed or should be adjusted
