## Description

**Steps to reproduce:**

1. Login as Recruiter - Myrtle Will

1. Open an ATS Applicant record in ‘Interviewing’ stage

1. Update the ATS stage on the Applicant record as ‘Offer’

1. Verify if an HPF record is created with values from ATS Applicant record

**Expected Result:**

HPF record should be created with

1. Practitioner Facility Name as ATS Applicant > Job > Account Name (formula)

1. Job Applicant as ATS Applicant

**Actual Result:**

1. Practitioner Facility Name on created HPF is populated as false instead of ATS Applicant > Job > Account Name

1. Job Applicant is not present on HealthcarePractitionerFacility object

ATS Applicant Record: [https://corehc-qa.sandbox.lightning.force.com/lightning/r/bpats__ATS_Applicant__c/a3ngP000000QHd7QAG/view|https://corehc-qa.sandbox.lightning.force.com/lightning/r/bpats__ATS_Applicant__c/a3ngP000000QHd7QAG/view]

HPF record created: [https://corehc-qa.sandbox.lightning.force.com/lightning/r/HealthcarePractitionerFacility/0bSgP0000000Ac5UAE/view|https://corehc-qa.sandbox.lightning.force.com/lightning/r/HealthcarePractitionerFacility/0bSgP0000000Ac5UAE/view]

## Root Cause

Incorrect mapping Job Applicant was mapped to ATS_Applicant__r.Applicant which is Contact not Job. As well there was an incorrrect way of querying for existing HPF (Job Applcant needs to be used).

Human Error: Developer Mistakes
