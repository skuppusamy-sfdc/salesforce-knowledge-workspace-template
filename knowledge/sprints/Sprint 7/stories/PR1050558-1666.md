## Description

As a Recruiter, I need to specify additional information when I select Other Bonus during Contract Requests, so that I can let the contracting team know what additional compensation is needed for this clinician

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a Recruiter in the Corporate Support apps

When I navigate to a clinician record and completed Contract Request guided process

Then on the Summary Tab, I click button **Configure Rates**

And I am able to configure Bonus with type Other Bonus

And I must specify the following: 

- Other Bonus Details

** free text field

** visible and required when Other Bonus is selected

## Solution

- Update Clinician Rate Omni Script

- When Selecting bonus type, (multi-select picklist) if “Other” option is also selected, then display text field “Other Bonus details”. Map this to Notes field (PreventiveCareAgreement.Notes__c).
