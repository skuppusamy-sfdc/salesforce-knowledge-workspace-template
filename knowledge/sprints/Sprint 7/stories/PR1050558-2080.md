## Description

**Steps to reproduce:**

1. Login as Recruiter - Myrtle Will

1. Open a Clinician account having 2 HPF records linked to it and both should have rates configured on the client contracts (Bonus)

1. Create a clinician contract record for both HPFs

1. From Summary tab of the clinician contract, configure the rates(Bonus) for 1st HPF by adding Additional Bonus(Night Shift)

1. Rates should be created along with respective CPA record

1. Now go to Configure rate of the 2nd HPF > select Bonus > Click on Next

**Expected Result:**

Additional rates should not be shown to the second HPF as Addtional rates should be visible to one Facility

**Actual Result:**

Addtional rates are shown to second Facility

Note: Right now we are only Showing help text and user has option available to create additonal bonus to second HPF Facility.
