## Description

As a Medical Staff Liaison - CPA, I need potential supervisory relationships to be created automatically, so that they are ready for my review as part of the Collaborative Practice Agreement process

## Acceptance Criteria

Given that I am a Medical Staff Liaison - CPA in the Corporate Support apps

When the following criteria are met on a contract record: 

- Contract Record Type = Clinician

** **Professional Title*

*** **Contract > Clinician Person Account*

**** **Professional Title = MD, DO*, NP, PA

** **Status = Executed*

Then, if the executed Contract was for a **Physician** (Clinician’s Professional Titles = MD or DO) a Collaborative Practice Agreement record is created: 

** For each Healthcare Practitioner Facility associated with the executed contract, **associate the Physician to each HPF at that facility* where 

*** **Professional Titles = NP or PA*

** Status = Prospect, Candidate, Waiting to be Scheduled, Active, WTBS Here-WRKG at Sister Site

** If an existing CPA record exists between the MD/DO and NP/PA, update the CPA record with new HPF with Supervision/Collaboration Status = “Pending” and enable history tracking on original NP/PA Healthcare Practitioner Facility and Physician Healthcare Facility using Field History Tracking

- Each Collaborative Practice Agreement record created shall populated with:

** NP/PA Healthcare Practitioner Facility = <create one record for each Professional Titles = NP, PA at every HPF within the Clinician Contract>

** Physician Healthcare Facility = <Healthcare Practitioner Facility associated with executed contract>

** Supervision/Collaboration Status = “Pending”

_Note: If there are NO clinicians with Professional Title NP/PA or MD/DO, then no relationship records are needed_

And, Collaborative Practice Agreement case is auto generated for **each** Service Account with the following information populated:

- Record Type = Collaborative Practice Agreement

- Subject = Collaborative Practice Agreement - <Healthcare Practitioner Facility>

- Type = Collaborative Practice Agreement

- Status = Not Started

- Case Owner = <Account Team “Medical Staff Liaison - CPA>

- Priority = Medium

- Contact Name = <Clinician (Person Account)>

- Account Name = <Service Account from HPF>
