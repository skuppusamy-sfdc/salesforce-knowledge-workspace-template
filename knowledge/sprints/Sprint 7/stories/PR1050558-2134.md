## Description

**Steps to Reproduce:**

1. Login to Core HC org as Recruiter.

2. Navigates to the Accounts tab > Click on Create Clinician record

3. Click on Create HPF button on top right corner.

4. Fill the madatory field and click on Next button on the Update NPI page.

5. Click on Finish button on Update NPI page 

6. Fill the Primary Specialty, Practicing Specialty, Taxonomiey fields on the Clinician Information page > and click on Next button.

7. Search for Service account able to add the service account.

8. Fill the mandatory fields Facility Association page > Click on Save and Refresh After Save button.

9. Click on Next button on the Facility Association page.

10. Try to add more Primary Specialty, Practicing Specialty, Taxonomiey fields on HPF record.

10. Click on Qualifications tab.

11. Verify that Taxonomy & Speciality records.

**Expected Result:** If record exists, it should be marked as primary. If record not exist, it should create a new record.

**Actual Result:** Showing 2 duplicate records among one record marked as primary.
