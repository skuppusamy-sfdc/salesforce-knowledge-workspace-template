## Description

**Steps to reproduce:**

1. Login as Recruiter - Myrtle Will

1. Open a Clinician account having 2 HPF records linked to it and both should have rates configured on the client contracts

1. Create a clinician contract record for both HPFs

1. From Summary tab of the clinician contract, configure the rates for 1st HPF

1. Rates should be created along with respective CPA record

1. Now, try to add the rate for the second HPF record with same Facility Title, Value, Unit of measure

**Expected Result:**

Rates should be created along with respective CPA record for the second HPF record

**Actual Result:**

Rates are not created along with respective CPA record for the second HPF record
