## Description

As a Clinician Contracting Specialist, I need corporate entities to automatically be associated with clinicians and their potential facilities, so that I can create contracts with the right entities and limited manual effort

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a Recruiter, Data Quality, or Enrollment Specialist user in the Corporate Support apps

When I create a HPF record (through automation from ATS Applicant or Create HPF experience)

Then the Healthcare Practitioner Facility record is populated with the **Contracting Corporate Entity** according to the logic below: 

- Using the Facility Title on the HPF record, determine which Corporate Entity Account record to use. From the Account lookup on the HPF record, use Account Account Relationship to filter Service Account - Corporate Entity relationships based on the Party Role Relationships specified below: 

*** Clinician Professional Title = **MD or DO** AND Facility Title (Code Set Sub Type) equals **Employee*

*** Party Role Relationship = EE Physician Contract Entity

*** Clinician Professional Title = **NP or PA** AND Facility Title (Code Set Sub Type) equals **Employee*

*** Party Role Relationship = EE NP/PA Contracting Entity

*** Clinician Professional Title = **RN or LPN** AND Facility Title (Code Set Sub Type) equals **Employee*

*** Party Role Relationship = EE RN/LPN Contracting Entity

*** **All others** (Professional Title 6 with Facility Title (Code Set Sub Type) ≠ **Employee*)

*** Party Role Relationship = Services Contracting Entity

** If Account Account Relationship is not found, Contracting Corporate Entity is blank

NOTE: Based on the update listed above, for story [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505581070|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505581070|smart-link]  we are now pulling the Healthcare Payer Network from Corporate Entity instead from Person Account. 

Given I am a System Administrator

When I navigate to the OWD

Then the following objects shall be set to Public Read Write (internal only)

- Healthcare Payer Network (implemented in 1389)

- Healthcare Provider (implemented in 1389)

- Service Line
