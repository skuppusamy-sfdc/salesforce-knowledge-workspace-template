## Description

As a MSL, I need to specify sister site relationships, so that Service Accounts can be connected to each other when they share the same privileges

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a Medical Staff Liaison in the Corporate Support apps

When I navigate to a Service Account record

Then I am able to see the following

- object for sister group

- object to relate Service Account records to sister group

_Note: today the largest sister group has 11 Service Accounts within it, a Service Account is part of one group, clinicians can be associated to multiple facilities with multiple sister groups_

## Solution

Account Account Relationship - manually enter the relationship between sites, using party  role relationship

Use ARC (Actionable Relationship center) to visually display the sites AAR (relationship established above) in under subtab “Sites” in Service Account record.

Additional fields to be displayed for sites (ARC/Account) will be added - TBD

- Remove filter on Account field

- Add a validation rule for Party Role Relationship: Record Type = Service Account when Party Role Relationship = _SisterSisterAAR_

** Error message: _Only "SisterSisterAAR" PartyRoleRelation can have service account, rest should be Corporate Entity_

- Add formula field = Party Role Name (see dd)
