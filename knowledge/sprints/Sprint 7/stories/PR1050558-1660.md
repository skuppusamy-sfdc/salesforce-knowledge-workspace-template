## Description

As a MSL, I need to initiate the clinical match process, so that SCP teams have visibility into accurate statuses

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am an MSL in the Corporate Support apps

When I navigate to a clinician person account record

Then I am able to select “Initiate Clinical Match” in the header

And I can see the following: 

- Clinical Match Request

** Display clinician name (read only)

** Quality Reason (multi-select picklist)

*** values: Clinical Care, Provider Skillset, Patient Volume, Provider's Efficiencies, Patient Acuity

** Service Reason (multi-select picklist)

*** values: Interpersonal Skills, Anger Management, Shift Attendance, Organizational Skills, Patient Complaints, Provider Behavior

** Business Issues Reason (multi-select picklist)

*** values: Mission Compliance, Malpractice History, Documentation, Litigation History, Contractual Dispute, Hospital Request, Contractual Issue, Scheduling Concerns

** Professionalism Reason (multi-select picklist)

*** values: Personal Hygiene, Recurrent Tardiness, Personal Integrity, Inappropriateness

** Qualifications Reason (multi-select picklist)

*** values: Application Issue, Criminal Background, Health Concern, Impairment, License Revocation, References

** Description (free text, required)

- Then when I select Save, a case record is created with the attributes above
