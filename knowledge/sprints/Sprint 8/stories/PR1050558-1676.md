## Description

As as a Sales VP, I want a Letter of Intent (LOI) Request to be automatically generated and assigned to Legal - Client Contracting, so that they are triggered to create the Letter of Intent & can close the request when complete.

## Acceptance Criteria

AC1: create Letter of Intent (LOI) Request

Given I am a Sales VP in the Corporate Support Apps

When:

- Opportunity (RT: Growth Opportunity)

** Pro Forma Status = Model Confirmed or Approved for Contracting

Then from the Opportunity (RT: Opportunity Growth), the Sales VP has the ability to select the guided experience = “**Initiate Contracting Request**” 

And user will be presented the option to select Contract Type Request = **Letter of Intent** 

And the user will be presented with the following required fields:

- Section Header: NDA Fields

** Proposed Effective Date [date]

** Name of Client Entity [text, 255]

** Address of Client Entity [text area, 255]

- Section Header: Letter of Intent Fields

** Name of Client Representative LOI will be addressed to [text, 255]

** Client Address [text area, 255]

** Name of Client Entity [text, 255]

** Name of each Hospital [text area (long), 131,072] 

** Location of each Hospital (City & State) [text area (long), 131,072]]

** Type of Services to be provided at each Hospital (EM/HM/ICU/Connected Care) [text, 255]

** Description of Services (i.e. HM & CC programs managed by SCP) [text, 255]

** Anticipated Start Date for Services [date]

** Proposed Term of Agreement (i.e. 3 years) [text, 255]

** Financial Terms (i.e. MG, subsidy, at risk money/incentive money, etc.) [text, 255]

** Other Key Terms & Assumptions (i.e. MOR/Governance/GME program, Malpractice limits, etc.) [text, 255]

And available navigation actions:

- Cancel/Exit

** Nothing is saved or created. 

** Guided experience is closed

** User is returned to the Opportunity record

- Previous 

** User is returned to the previous screen with selection retained. 

- Finish (Next button)

** Guided experience is closed

** User is returned to the Opportunity record

** And the system shall create the Letter of Intent request for the Legal - Client Contracting team 

*** Contract Record Type = Letter of Intent

*** Contract Owner = Opportunity’s Owner

*** Status = Draft

*** Account Name = <Opportunity’s Account Name>

*** Source Opportunity = <OpportunityID>

**** Link to Opportunity (user can view Pro Forma Model from the opportunity record) 

AC2: Error message

Given I am a Sales VP in the Corporate Support Apps

When:

- Opportunity (RT: Growth Opportunity)

** Pro Forma Status ≠ Model Confirmed or Approved for Contracting

And from the Opportunity (RT: Opportunity Growth), if the Sales VP selects the guided experience = “**Initiate Contracting Request**” 

And user will be presented the option to select Contract Type Request = **Letter of Intent**

And the user tries to finish/complete the guided experience

Then the system shall display an error message: Pro Forma Model must be in Status = “Model Confirmed” or “Approved for Contracting” to request a Letter of Intent. 

AC3: Update Opportunity to LOI Executed

Given I am a Sales VP in the Corporate Support Apps

When the Letter of Intent Request is closed by the Legal - Client Contracting team [Contract Record Type = Letter of Intent is in Status = Executed

Then the system shall update field = “LOI Complete” to checked/true on the Opportunity (RT: Opportunity Growth).
