## Description

**Steps tor reproduce:**

1. Login as Recruiter - Myrtle Will

1. Open a clinician person account having Employee or Independent Contractor type clinician contract in ‘Executed’ status

1. Click ‘Contract Request’ button

1. Select Employee Type as ‘Employee’ and click ‘Next’ button

1.  Select ‘Start from Previous Active Contract’ option, enter the start date and click ‘Next’ button

1. Open the clinician contract in ‘Executed’ status

1. Click ‘Clone’ button, update status as ‘Draft’ and click ‘Save’ button

**Expected Result:**

New clinician contract should be cloned from the previous active contract with PNC, CPA and PCA records

**Actual Result:**

New clinician contract is created without CPA and PCA records

## Root Cause

Date field was breaking for datetime type field. So used the different method.
