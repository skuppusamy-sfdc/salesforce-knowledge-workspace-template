## Description

As a Medical Staff Liaison, I need to terminate a clinician’s employment at a specific facility in Salesforce, so that I can accurately update the status of employees at facilities that SCP staffs for

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a Medical Staff Liaison in the Corporate Support apps

When I view a **Clinician** record

Then I am able to select Initiate Termination Request and see the following:

- Facility Selection  [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558701|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558701|smartlink]

- Termination Details  [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558306|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558306|smartlink] 

- If I have selected HPF records where the Facility Title indicates they are an Employee (Code Set Sub Type) 

AND Voluntary Termination is not checked

** Service Now Ticket Number (text, 20 characters, required)

*** Help Text: EE Involuntary Term requires notification to Employee Relations team

*** Ticket is per clinician, present this field once regardless of how many HPFs were selected

## Solution

Where? On the second screen of omniscript

Query for HPF.Facility_Title__r.Code_Set_Sub_Type__c = 'Employee'

When the HPF Facility Title is Employee and Voluntary termination field is not checked, 

Conditionally display  the message on the screen 'You must initiate this process in Service Now'

Create the Service Now field on Offboarding Case . Map the value from the form to Case.Service_Now__c

FLS - to any persona who has access to Case.
