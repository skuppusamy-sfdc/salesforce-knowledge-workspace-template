## Description

**Description:**

On the **Opportunity (RT: Growth Opportunity)** record, the **Initiate Contracting Request** button is available even when the **Pro Forma Status** field is blank. As per requirements, the button should only be available when **Pro Forma Status = “Model Confirmed” or “Approved for Contracting.”**

**Steps to Reproduce:**

1. Log in to Salesforce QA Sandbox.

1. Navigate to an **Opportunity (RT: Growth Opportunity)** record.

1. Ensure **Pro Forma Status = blank**.

1. Observe that the **Initiate Contracting Request** button is visible.

**Expected Result:**

The **Initiate Contracting Request** button should **not be visible** when **Pro Forma Status ≠ Model Confirmed / Approved for Contracting**.

**Actual Result:**

The **Initiate Contracting Request** button is **visible** even when Pro Forma Status is blank.

**Impact:**

- Users may attempt to create an LOI Request without meeting prerequisites.
