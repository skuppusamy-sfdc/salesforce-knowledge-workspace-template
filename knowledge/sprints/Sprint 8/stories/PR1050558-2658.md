## Description

**Steps tor reproduce:**

1. Login as Recruiter - Myrtle Will

1. Open a clinician person account having Employee type clinician contract in ‘Executed’ status

1. Click ‘Contract Request’ button

1. Select Employee Type as ‘Employee’ and click ‘Next’ button

1.  ‘Employee’ executed contract’s start and date date should be pre-populated

1. Update the start date with a value 10 days after the executed contract’s end date and end date as another future date and click ‘Next’ button

**Expected Result:**

Start and end date of previous executed contract of same employment type should remain the same

**Actual Result:**

End date of previous executed contract of same employment type is updated as start date - 1 of the newly created clinician contract in ‘Draft’ status
