## Description

As a Data Quality team member, I need clinicians to be inactivated if they haven’t worked in 12 months, so that our clinician data is clean

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a Data Quality team member in the Corporate Support apps

When I navigate to a clinician record 

And I view a Healthcare Practitioner Facility record **where the following is true:** 

- If the HPF Status is not Prospect, Candidate, or Inactive

** **A clinician hasn’t worked in a year*:

** Last Date Worked (HPF) is more than 12 months away for all sister sites that a clinician works at (Account field on HPF where status is not Prospect, Candidate, or Inactive)

** **A clinician has never worked*: 

** If Last Date Worked (HPF) is blank, check the Company Signed Date (on Clinician Contract, aka Effective Date) on the Contract records for all sister sites

** If Last Date Worked (HPF) and Company Signed Date (Clinician Contract) are both blank, Hospital Privileged Date (HPF) is more than 12 months away for all sister sites

Then the HPF record is automatically updated with the following:

- Status = Inactive

- Credentialling Status = Inactive

- Termination Date = date when Last Date Worked is more than 12 months away

And the Clinician record is updated so that I-9 Status is blank 

**ITEMS ABOVE ARE BUILT IN** [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505581580|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505581580|smart-link] 

**for this user story:** 

**And all open cases, child cases, tasks related child cases, & tasks related to the clinician are updated to Status = Closed**

## Solution

single place for IT to adjust time from 12 months (ex. to 6 months)

Create an apex batch job that runs daily. It will query all related sites for the clinician and then look for the HPF’s belonging to that sites. If the Last Worked  Date is more tahn a year and newer that the other ones we proceed to inactivate.  

Batch job on HPF

Query for HPF where Status is not Prospect, Candidate, or Inactive. Get related Clinician records as well

Filter HPF by the following

Today - HPF.Last_Date_Worked__c >=12 months

NOTE

For **A clinician has never worked**:_If Last Date Worked is blank, check the Company Signed Date (aka Effective Date) on the Contract records for all sister sites,_

Sister sites relationship are captured in Account Account relatioship

Look for active contract that are older than a year, then see if the last date worked is blank

Check the same conditions for all other sites (using the AAR relationship) .. and if they are true we update

If any one of them do not satisfy the conditions( meaning active contract older than a year, but have the last date worked populated) we break/skip the update
