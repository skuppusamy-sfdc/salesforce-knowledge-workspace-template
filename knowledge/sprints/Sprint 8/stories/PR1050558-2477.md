## Description

### **Description:**

As a Sales VP, I need to request agreements for my clients so that contracts can be generated with all necessary information for SCP to provide services.

Per the acceptance criteria for the Opportunity layout (record type = Growth Opportunity), the **Right Region** should display the following tabs:

- Related

- Services

- Deal Team

- Contact Roles

- Opportunity Splits

- Opportunity Team (same as Deal Team)

** **Deal Workstreams*

- Notes

- Stage History

- Attachments

- Files (needed for story 1675)

- Approval History (own tab per story 1568)

Currently, the **Deal Workstreams** tab is **missing** in the right region.

### **Steps to Reproduce:**

1. Log in as a Sales VP user in Corporate Support or Corporate Support Console apps.

1. Navigate to an **Opportunity record** with record type = _Growth Opportunity_.

1. Review the **Right Region tabs**.

### **Expected Result:**

The **Right Region** should include all required tabs, including **Deal Workstreams**.

### **Actual Result:**

The **Deal Workstreams tab is missing** from the Right Region in the Opportunity layout.

## Root Cause

The required object permission for Sales VP was not included in the Design Document (DD), resulting in the missing access in the Sales VP permission set.
