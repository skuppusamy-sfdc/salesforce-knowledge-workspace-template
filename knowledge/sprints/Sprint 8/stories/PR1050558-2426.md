## Description

**Steps to reproduce:**

1. Login as Data Quality Team Member

1. Open a HPF record in ‘Active’ status and ‘Last Date Worked’ older than 12 months and having open cases & tasks

1. HPF of sister site accounts with clinician same as initial HPF record should also have ‘Last Date Worked’ older than 12 months

**Expected Result:**

1. HPF record should be updated as below

#* Status = Inactive

#* Credentialling Status = Inactive

#* Termination Date = date when Last Date Worked is more than 12 months away

1. And the Clinician record is updated so that I-9 Status is blank 

And all open cases and tasks related to the clinician are updated to Status = Closed

**Actual Result:**

1. Facility onboarding case are not closed

1. Child cases of SCP and Facility onboarding case are not closed

1. Tasks associated with the Child cases are not closed
