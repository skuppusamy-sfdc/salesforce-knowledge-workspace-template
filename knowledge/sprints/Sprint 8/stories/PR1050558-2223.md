## Description

**Steps to reproduce:**

1. Login as Data Quality Team Member

1. Open a HPF record in ‘Active’ status and ‘Last Date Worked’ older than 12 months

1. HPF of sister site accounts with clinician same as initial HPF record should also have ‘Last Date Worked’ older than 12 months

**Expected Result:**

1. HPF record should be updated as below

#* Status = Inactive

#* Credentialling Status = Inactive

#* Termination Date = date when Last Date Worked is more than 12 months away

1. And the Clinician record is updated so that I-9 Status is blank 

And all open cases and tasks related to the clinician are updated to Status = Closed

**Actual Result:**

1. HPF record and related items are also not updated when there is no sister site 

1. HPF record and related items are also not updated when there is a siter site with HPFs having Last Date Worked is more than 12 months old

## Root Cause

Data Issue - HPF records in Inactive status causing exception

## Root Cause Type

Data: Data Corruption
