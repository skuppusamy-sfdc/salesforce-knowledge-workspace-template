## Description

Steps to Reproduce:-

1-Login as SFO into the Corp HC Org and navigate to the Corporate Support App.

2-From app launcher search for Account object and open a Service Account record.

3-Navigate to related tab and open the Client Contract record where Status = Draft.

4-Navigate to Summary tab and select Specify Rate button.

5​Select Type of Rate ​as Shift and a Single Shift Type is displayed with values  NP/PA, MD/DO, RN/LPN.

6-Verify if NP/PA is selected under Shift Type then Employment Type field is displayed with Employed and Other values.

7-Select the values and click on next and verify Shift records are not being displayed.

Note - Upon selecting any type of shift type the shifts records are not being displayed, even Shift records are present on Practice Operations record.

Issue - The Shifts records are not being displayed under the Specify Rates flow on Practice Operation record.

Expected Result - The Shifts records should be displayed under the Specify Rates flow on Practice Operation record.

Actual Result - The Shifts records are not being displayed under the Specify Rates flow on Practice Operation record.

## Root Cause

Someelse’s changes were present in the Integration Procedure, which caused the error. While testing on dev, those changes were not present. Removed those changes and deployed again.
