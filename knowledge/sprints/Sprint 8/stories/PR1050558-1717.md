## Description

As a Clinician Contracting Specialist, I need Salesforce to enforce validations around creating and updating healthcare practitioner facility records, so that duplicate record creation and mismatches are prevented

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a Recruiter in the Corporate Support apps

When I attempt the following scenarios while creating or updating Healthcare Practitioner Facility records

- Duplicate an Active Clinician/Client Relationship 

** Automatically inactive a currently active HPF (matching Practitioner and Account fields) if a new one is updated to Active 

** _note: this does not result in termination  time based or termination request  in Salesforce or downstream_

_Note: multiple Inactive HPFs can exist with the same Practitioner and Account fields_

## Solution

Record triggered flow on HPF update (when status is made Active)

Query for other HPF for the current HPF’s Clinician Account (PractitionerId) and Service Account (AccountId)

If any of them are active, and having the same Employment Type, check for blank employment type (and exclude them) update the HPF to inactive.
