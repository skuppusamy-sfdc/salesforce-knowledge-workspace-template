## Description

**Description:**

When attempting to set **Contract Status = Executed** for a **Letter of Intent (LOI)** request, the system displays a permission error:

_“You don’t have permission to activate Contracts. Ask your Salesforce admin for help.”_

As per **Acceptance Criteria 3 (AC3)**, when the Legal - Client Contracting team closes the LOI request with **Status = Executed**, the related **Opportunity (RT: Growth Opportunity)** field **LOI Complete** should be updated to **True/Checked**. Currently, this flow is blocked due to the permission error.

**Steps to Reproduce:**

1. Log in to Salesforce QA Sandbox as a user with Legal - Client Contracting role.

1. Navigate to an existing **Contract record** (Record Type = Letter of Intent).

1. Attempt to update **Status = Executed**.

1. Click **Save**.

**Expected Result:**

** System should allow saving with **Status = Executed*.

** Related Opportunity (Source Opportunity) should automatically update **LOI Complete = True*.

**Actual Result:**

- Save fails with error:

_“You don’t have permission to activate Contracts. Ask your Salesforce admin for help.”_

** Opportunity field **LOI Complete* is not updated.
