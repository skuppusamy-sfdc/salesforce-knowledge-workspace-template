## Description

As a SFO (Staffing Fulfillment Optimization) user, I want the ability to generate facility rates by designating the type of rate and specified rate for each Type & Facility Title combinations, so that we have clearly defined rates for each Service Account.

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a SFO in the Corporate Support or Corporate Support Console app

When I navigate to a **Contract** record (record type = **Client**)

And I am on the Summary tab (story [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558715|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558715|smart-link] )

Then I have the ability to click **Specify Rates** button

Given I am a SFO in the Corporate Support or Corporate Support Console app

And I clicked the Specify Rates button

And I clicked **Finish**

Then then the system shall create the appropriate Contract Payment Agreement, and Preventive Care Agreement records as specified in the solution.

And the guided experience shall automatically closed and I am navigated back to Contract (Client) record.

## Solution

Upon selecting button = Finish, then the system shall create the following records:

** Create a **Contract Payment Agreement* record related to the Contract (Client) record.

** Name = <“Account Name” from the Client Contract record “-” Type>

** Status = Draft

** Contract = <Contract (record type = Client) record where initiated>

** Account = <Service Account record - “Account Name” from the Client Contract record>

** Agreement Type = Preventive Care Agreement

** Type = <Type of Rate selected by the user>

** _do not create duplicate Contract Payment Agreements in Draft status for the same Type_ 

** Create **Preventive Care Agreement* record(s) related to the Contract Payment Agreement record; create one per Facility Title & Type captured by the user.

** Facility Title = <Facility Title selected by the user>

** Type = <Type selected by the user>  (field update part of [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558469|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558469|smart-link] )

** Shift Iteration = <Shift Iteration inputted by the user. Only if Type of Rate = Shift> NEW Will move to [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558486|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558486|smart-link] 

** Rate = <Rate inputted by the user>

** Unit of Measure = <Unit of Measure selected by the user>

** Bonus Measure Type = NA

** Target Measure Percent = 0

** Bonus Payout = 0
