## Description

As a SFO (Staffing Fulfillment Optimization) user, I want the ability view and delete existing facility rates created for this Client contract created via the guided Specify Rates experience, so that I can easily manage rates for a specific Contract.

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a SFO in the Corporate Support or Corporate Support Console app

When I navigate to a **Contract** record (record type = **Client**) 

Then I have the ability to select the “Summary” tab

And within the Summary tab, I can view **existing** and **newly created** Rates related to this Contract via the guided Specify Rates experience. 

And the following information shall be displayed, grouped by: (see mock below of view within the Summary tab)

1. **Type of Rate** (Contract Payment Agreement.Type)

1. **Type** (Preventive Care Agreement.Type)

1. **Facility Title & Rate** (Preventive Care Agreement.FacilityTitle__c & Preventive Care Agreement.Rate__c)

!image20250702221245.png|width=1825,height=682,alt="image20250702221245.png"!

Given I am a SFO in the Corporate Support or Corporate Support Console app

When I navigate to a **Contract** record (record type = **Client**) 

And I am within the “Summary” tab

Then I have the option to delete the entire **Type of Rate** (#1 in mock); this will delete all records for that Type of Rate.

And I have the option to delete a specific **Facility Title, Rate, & Unit of Measure** combination (#3 in mock)

NOTE:

- Build the Summary screen with Performance in mind.

## Solution

- When testing ensure you have at least 50 records

** Facility Title & Rate & **Unit of Measure** (Preventive Care Agreement.FacilityTitle__c & Preventive Care Agreement.Rate__c & **_Preventive Care Agreement._***Unit_of_Measure__c**)

*** **Format*:

First line: Facility Title

Second line: <Rate with $> space <Unit of Measure>

**Example**:

Ambassador NP/PA

$12 per Hour

- Add “$” to Rate since it is a currency field. Example: $12 vs 12

- Design: Instead of 3 columns within each Type of Rate section, can we display each “Type” as a collapsible section.

*# Display as collapsed, IF it increases the speed/help with loading. If not, leave expanded by default.
