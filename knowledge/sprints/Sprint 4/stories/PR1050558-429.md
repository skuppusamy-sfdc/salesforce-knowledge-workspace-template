## Description

As a MSL, I need to see key status and date attributes from QGenda in Salesforce, so that there is a complete picture of a clinician for reporting and monitoring in one system

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a MSL in the Corporate Support or Corporate Support App

When I navigate to a Healthcare Practitioner Facility record

Then I am able to see the following fields: 

- Status _(Salesforce)_

** Picklist values: Prospect, Candidate, Waiting to be Scheduled, Active, Inactive, WTBS Here-WRKG at Sister Site

- Time Type _(Asymbl job req->Salesforce)_

** Full Time, Part Time, On Call, PRN, Locums

- Credentialing Status _(Salesforce today, potential to change to QGenda)_

** Picklist values: Active, Billing Only, Credentialed Only, In Deferral, Inactive, Pending, Scheduled Pending, Superv/Collab Phys Only, Temporarily Inactive

- Temporary Inactive Reason _(Salesforce today, potential to change to QGenda)_

** full list of values in Data Dictionary

- Hospital Privileged Date

- Committee Month

- Hospital Application Received Date

- Hospital Application Sent Date (App to Hosp on)

- Contracting Status _(Salesforce)_

** Picklist values: Prospect, Draft, Executed, Amendment in Progress, Termination in Progress, Terminated

- First Clinical Shift

** Help Text: Actual first date a clinician worked

- First Scheduled Date _(Salesforce)_

** Help Text: Anticipated first shift date

- Last Date Worked

Given I am a MSL in the Corporate Support or Corporate Support App

When I navigate to a Clinician person account record

Then I am able to see the following fields: 

- Birthdate

- Military Status

** see data dictionary for picklist values

- Military Service Start Date

- Military Service End Date

** Military status and service fields will be added in story [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558469|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558469|smart-link]
