## Description

As a Sales VP or Business Development Associate, I need to see client division in the Account Hierarchy view, so that I can quickly understand the team associated with Service Accounts in a streamlined view

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a Sales VP or Business Development Associate in the Corporate Support or Corporate Support Console apps

When I navigate to an Account (Service Account) record

Then I can see fields related to staffing operations and client operations in the Operational Structure section as specified in the data dictionary

Given I am a Sales VP or Business Development Associate in the Corporate Support or Corporate Support Console apps

When I navigate to an Operational Structure record

Then I am able to see the following: 

- Header - display Operational Structure Name only

- Main Region

** Tabs component: Details, Related

** Details tab displays fields as specified in data dictionary

** Related tab displays 

*** Accounts related list (columns to display: Account Name, Global Facility ID, Physical Address)

**** No action buttons are available

*** Operational Structure History related list

**** History tracking enabled on Active, Level 1, Level 2, Owner, and Type fields

**** No action buttons are available

## Solution

create custom object

create LRP

update OWD & permission sets (includes updates to Permission set group = **Staffing Operations**, add “**Manage Client Contracts**” permission set)

Field & page layout updates on Account (Service Account)
