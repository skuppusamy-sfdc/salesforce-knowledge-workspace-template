## Description

As a Recruiter, I need a guided experience to create clinician records in Salesforce, so that I can quickly create a new prospect

_Feedback from 6/17 Demo of Story_ [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558195|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558195|smart-link] _to enhance/update existing guided flow._

## Acceptance Criteria

AC1: Update “Geographic Preference” field on step “Clinician Creation”

Given I am a Recruiter logged in to the Corporate Support or Corporate Support Console app

When I navigate to the Contacts tab and click “Create Clinician”

Then the “Clinician Creation” step is displayed

And the “Geographic Preference” field shall be changed:

**From**:  Text field

**To**:       Multi-select picklist of States 

!image20250618232749.png|width=91%,alt="image20250618232749.png"!

## Solution

- “Geographic Preference” field _Contact object_  Update from Text field to a State multiselect picklist

- Design prefer for both “Medical License” & “Geographic Preference” fields to display/behave like the standard SF multi-select picklist.

- Ensure fields are being populated:

** Medical License - the state field is being populated on the Business License Record

** Geographic Preference - the state(s) are being populated on the Person Account (Clinician) “Geographic Preference” field.
