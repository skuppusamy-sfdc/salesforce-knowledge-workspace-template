## Description

As a Sales VP, I need to request agreements for my clients, so that we can generate a contract with necessary information for SCP to provide services

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a Sales VP in the Corporate Support or Corporate Support Console apps

When I navigate to an **Opportunity** record 

Then I have the ability to click the **Agreement Request** button _(button to be created in 472, see solution for how to manually trigger email)_

And I am able to populate information as specified in

[https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558472|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558472|smart-link] 

[https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558486|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558486|smart-link] 

[https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558470|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558470|smart-link] 

And upon selecting button = Finish

- An email is sent to:

** The contract requester

** Users with Role = Client Contracting 

** Users with Role = VP Sales Operations

- Email Subject: New Agreement Request

- Email Body:

** Hi <First Name of Users in Role = Client Contracting & VP Sales Operations>,

A Client Agreement Request has been created for the following Opportunity <link back to Opportunity Record> and Contract <link back to Contract records>. 

Thank you, 

<Client Contract Owner’s First Name> 

The OmniScript is closed automatically and I am navigated to the newly created Contract record (just for providing clarity for testing purpose only no OmniScript if necessary for build)

## Solution

create two roles as specified in security matrix

Create a record triggered flow to look for searching the Role and get the all Users associated to the Role  Client Contracting, VP Sales Operations and send out the email using **Email Alert**.

**Trigger:** When contract record with record type = Client, Status is updated to Submitted
