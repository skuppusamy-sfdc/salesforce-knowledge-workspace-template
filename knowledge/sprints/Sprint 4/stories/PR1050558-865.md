## Description

As per the **Clinician Onboarding sheet**. Facility Onboarding Child cases should not be created for HPF Credential status = Active, Pending, Scheduled Pending, Temporarily Inactive.

!image20250714115037.png|width=1705,height=958,alt="image20250714115037.png"!

**Test steps:**

Step 1: Create/Open Clinician record 

!image20250714120212.png|width=100%,alt="image20250714120212.png"!

Step 2: Associate HPF with status = Prospect && Cretdentialiing Status ==  Active/ Pending Scheduled Pending /Temporarily Inactive.

!image20250714120049.png|width=100%,alt="image20250714120049.png"!

Step 3: Run Intiate Early Onboarding flow & Select Facility specific.

Step 4: Select HPF from Step 2 & Click on Next.

**Step 5: In Clinician record, Go to the cases > & Notice the Parent & Child cases are created.**

!image20250714120136.png|width=100%,alt="image20250714120136.png"!

**Other Observation:** 

**If** Credentialling Status = Blank **then** cases are getting created.
