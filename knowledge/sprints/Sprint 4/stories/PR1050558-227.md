## Description

As a Sales VP or Business Development Associate, I need to see relevant data when I view account hierarchies, so that I can quickly differentiate between hospital systems, facilities, and service lines in a single view

## Acceptance Criteria

Given that I am a Business Development Associate or Sales VP

When I open an Account record and select the Account Hierarchy view

Then I am able to see the following columns: Name, Record Type, Physical Address (Field Name = BillingAddress)  Physical Address State (Field Name = State/Province), Territory, Client Division, Phone, Account Owner Alias

## Solution

Object Manager > Account > Hierarchy Columns > New

Everyone can access the account hierarchy as long as they can see the account.
