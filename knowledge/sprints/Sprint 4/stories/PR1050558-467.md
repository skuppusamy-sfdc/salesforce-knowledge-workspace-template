## Description

As a Clinician, I need to complete an intake form, so that I can provide necessary information to move forward in the recruiting process and it is retained for downstream onboarding processes

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a Recruiter in the Corporate Support or Corporate Support Console app

When I navigate to a clinician record

Then I am able to the **Cases tab** with the following:

- Open Intake Form related list

** Record Type = Clinician Intake Form

** Status does not equal Closed

** Columns: Subject, Priority, Status, Case Owner, Last Modified Date

- Open Onboarding related list

** Record Type = Clinician Onboarding

** Status does not equal Closed

** Columns: Subject, Type, Healthcare Practitioner Facility, Priority Status, Case Owner, Last Modified Date

- Open Risk Review related list

** Record Type = Risk Review

** Status does not equal Closed

** Columns: Subject, Healthcare Practitioner Facility, Priority, Status, Case Owner, Last Modified Date

- Closed Cases related list

** Status = Closed

** Columns: Subject, Record Type, Type, Closed Reason, Case Owner, Last Modified Date
