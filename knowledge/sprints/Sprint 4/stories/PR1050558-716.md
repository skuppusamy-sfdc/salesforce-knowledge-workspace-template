## Acceptance Criteria

AC1: part of [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558385|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558385|smart-link] 

AC2: Update “Board Certified?” field on step “Clinician Creation”

Given I am a Recruiter logged in to the Corporate Support or Corporate Support Console app

When I navigate to the Contacts tab and click “Create Clinician”

Then the “Clinician Creation” step is displayed

And the “Board Certified?” field **name** shall be changed:

**From**: “Board Certified?”

**To**:      “Board Certification Status”

And the “Board Certification Status” field **values** shall be changed:

**From**: 

- Yes

- No

**To**:

- Certified

- Eligible

- Ineligible

AC3:  Update verbiage on step “Clinician Matching Duplicates in System” 

Given I am a Recruiter logged in to the Corporate Support or Corporate Support Console app

When I navigate to the Contacts tab and click “Create Clinician”

And I create a duplicate clinician (ex. input same email)

Then the “Clinician Matching Duplicates in System” step is displayed with existing duplicate Clinician record(s)

And check box (unchecked) with the following verbiage shall be updated to display:

**From**: “Do you want to continue with new clinician record?”

**To**:     “This may be a duplicate clinician record. Do you want to proceed anyway?”

## Solution

- Design prefer for both “Medical License” & “Geographic Preference” fields to display/behave like the standard SF multi-select picklist.

- Board Certification Status - create one record if any of the below values are selected, but do not create duplicate records. if nothing is selected, do NOT do anything; no record created

** Certified

** Eligible

** Ineligible
