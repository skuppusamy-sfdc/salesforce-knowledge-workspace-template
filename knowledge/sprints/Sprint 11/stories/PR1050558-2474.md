## Description

As a MSL, I need to initiate clinical match from clinician termination and vice versa, so that I can quickly make necessary updates for a clinician

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a MSL, Clinician Experience Specialist, or Data Quality team member in the Corporate Support apps

When I navigate to a clinician record

And I go through the Initiate Clinical Match guided experience

I am able to select a checkbox to indicate **Initiate Termination (help text: If checked, after clinical match you can specify facility specific or SCP termination request)**

And if that checkbox is checked, the Initiate Termination Request guided experience is opened for me to complete after the Clinical Match request

Given I am a MSL, Clinician Experience Specialist, or Data Quality team member in the Corporate Support apps

When I navigate to a clinician record

And I go through the Initiate Termination Request guided experience

I am able to select a checkbox to indicate **Initiate Clinical Match**

And if that checkbox is checked, the Initiate Clinician Termination guided experience is opened for me to complete after the Clinical Match request

And Clinical Match cases are not auto created as part of SCP Offboarding, only when indicated in the steps above

NOTE: story [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505581707|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505581707|smart-link] specifies that access to clinical match case records is granted to individuals and not whole personas

## Solution

Existing Screen flow of Initiate Clinical Match

Add a checkbox at the end of the screen?

 **Initiate Termination (help text: If checked, after clinical match you can specify facility specific or SCP termination request)**

If checked, launch the Initiate Termination Request omniscript from flow. (Use the omniscript URL and on click on button navigate to omniscript.(

Existing Omniscript of initiate Termination Request

At the end of the screen, 

Add new checkbox field **Initiate Clinical Match**

If checked, launch the flow ( see if Remote Navigation action can be used - to launch flow or embed in lwc and launch)

Remove autocreation of Clinican match child case logic ( [1727|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505581727])
