## Root Cause

The expected system behavior for **"Continue with Draft"** regarding the **Start Date** did not align with the testers' assumptions. Salesforce confirmed that the feature is working as designed: continuing a draft is meant for minor adjustments (like rate types) and **retains the existing Start Date**. Changing the date requires discarding the draft and initiating a new request.
