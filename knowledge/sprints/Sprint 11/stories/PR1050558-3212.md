## Description

As a Contracting specialist, I need the ability to view the Practice operations contract rates for each type of rate  when I configure rate on the Clinician contract, so that i can choose the rates that should apply on the draft Clinician contract

## Acceptance Criteria

Given, I am a contracting specialist on the ‘Configure Rates’ process of a Draft Clinician contract,

When I select each Type of Rate

Then, I am able to view the Practice Operation’s contract’s rate for that type along with the Clinician rate 

And I can choose to uncheck the additional Practice Operation rates that do not apply for the current draft Clinician contract. (if the contract is cloned then the Cloned Rates & new rates will checked by default, user can unselected them and/or update the rate as needed)

Mock: in the mock the ‘Facility Rate' column holds the rate from the Practice Operations contract & the ‘Rate’ is the from the Clinician’s contract.

## Solution

As discussed over 1:1 , In the Configure rates omniscript  where we display rates  

As part of clone process - update the Facility rate as well, so that it is easier to get the PO contract. (Currently the first time a clinician contract is created, Facility rate field is populated on CPA. Then if the clinician contract is cloned, the Cloned From is populated, but Facility rate is not populated/cloned over. That should be cloned over as well)

Add Facility rate as a column on the rates table (as shown in screenshot)

- in case of rates from Practice operations contract, (where the rates have not been edited), the facility rate and rate column will have same value.

Indicate rates that are from PO contract (facility rate) and clinician contract.

Currently we get rates from both PO and clinician contract. we need to indicate on the UI. Add a column : Source ( Facility Rate , Existing Clinician Rate ) , second column
