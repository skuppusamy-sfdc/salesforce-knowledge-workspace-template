## Description

**Steps to reproduce:**

1. Login as Legal team member

1. Open a client contract in Executed status

1. Click ‘Begin Termination Facility’ button

1. Select the required service accounts, enter termination details and click ‘Save’ button

1. Verify the client child and parent offboarding cases created

**Expected Result:**

When child Case records related to a Client Offboarding Parent Case are Status = Closed & Closed Reason = Completed or Canceled), then the parent case Status is updated as Closed with Closed Reason as Complete

**Actual Result:**

When child Case records related to a Client Offboarding Parent Case are closed, the parent case Status is not updated as Closed
