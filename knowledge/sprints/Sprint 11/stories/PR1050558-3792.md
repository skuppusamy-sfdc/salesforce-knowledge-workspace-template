Here is the detailed information regarding the monthly schedule for clinicians:

Key Points About Monthly Schedules:

Submission of Availability:

Notify your Scheduler of unavailable dates at least 90 days before the scheduled month.

Example: Availability for April is due by January 1.

Availability Preferences:

Provide no more than six (6) dates off per month, including a maximum of one (1) weekend.

Open availability is preferred when possible.

Vacation and Extended Leave:

Vacation or more than four (4) consecutive days off must be approved by the manager and Scheduler before booking travel.

Fair Share Policy:

All core providers are expected to work a fair share of days, nights, weekends, and holidays.

Holidays and Popular Vacation Times:

Certain dates like New Year's Day, Memorial Day, Thanksgiving, etc., are considered holidays, and time off may be restricted.

Shift Swapping:

Providers can swap shifts but must notify SCP Health via the mySCP application.

Schedule Creation and Distribution:

Publishing:

Finalized schedules are published in Qgenda and sent to on-call Provider Coverage Coordinators (PCCs) at least two weeks before the start of the month.

Communication:

For emergencies, contact the SCP Health Answering Service at 1337572-0374.

For non-emergencies, use the mySCP app or contact your Scheduler during business hours.

Provider Responsibilities:

Providers are responsible for their scheduled shifts and must notify the Scheduler of any changes.

If a provider cancels a confirmed shift, they may be financially responsible for bonuses required to fill the shift.

If you need further clarification or have a specific question about the schedule, such as requesting time off or handling shift changes, let me know!
