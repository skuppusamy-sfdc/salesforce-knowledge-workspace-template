## Root Cause

The reported discrepancy in the calculation of blended salary and overage rates was not a defect in the code but arose from a **misunderstanding of the approved calculation methodology.**

** **Misconception:* The initial expectation and formula used for testing (and initial calculation) varied from the method defined in the acceptance criteria.

** **Intended Logic Confirmed:** Discussions confirmed the implementation correctly follows the approved method, which is to **average the rates across all relevant facilities* (as opposed to averaging the facility-specific averages).

** **Future Enhancement:** While the current implementation aligns with the original story, the slight observed variance points toward a need for a **refinement of the accepted methodology** to ensure greater business accuracy (e.g., proper exclusion of night shift bonuses). This future work has been scoped out under new story **[PR1050558-3779]*.

Since the existing code meets the documented requirements, the issue was closed as the confusion regarding the calculation logic was clarified.
