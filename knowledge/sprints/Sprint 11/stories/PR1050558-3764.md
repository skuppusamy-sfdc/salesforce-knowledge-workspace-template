## Root Cause

The issue reported regarding the inability to view **bonus details** on the Contract record's main tabs was ultimately a **usability problem**, not a data or system defect.

** **Data Confirmation:* The data was confirmed to exist and was correctly accessible when navigating directly to the associated PCA from the Bonus CPA record.

** **Usability Flaw:** The difficulty in locating this critical information arose because the **Contract UI lacks a clear or direct display mechanism* (e.g., on the Details, Summary, or Related tabs) for bonus details, forcing users to utilize a less intuitive navigation path. This indicates a gap in the application's usability design for quick information retrieval.
