**Tax_Id and Reason_Description are missing**

## Root Cause

The defect was caused by **incomplete data handling** during the firing of the Salesforce Platform Event for IC Terminations.

Specifically:

1. The downstream integration system (**MuleSoft**) requires critical fields, such as **Tax ID** and **Reason**, to successfully process the IC termination event.

1. The Salesforce Platform Event payload was **not configured to include these required fields**, resulting in the MuleSoft flow failing due to missing data.

This was not a loss of source data, but an error in the **packaging/construction of the data payload** for the integration message. The fix involved correctly updating the event payload to include all necessary fields (`Reason_Description__c` and `Tax_Id__c`), as outlined in the solution from User Story 2462. The issue has been verified and confirmed as resolved.
