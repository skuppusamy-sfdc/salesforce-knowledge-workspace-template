## Description

As a System Administrator, when managing client contracts, I want to relate a Practice Operations contract, a Healthcare Practitioner Facility, and a specific Change Type so that I have a tracking mechanism of the type of change that needs to be made at a clinician level based on changes made at the client contract level.
