Description:  EE NPPA rate fields are calculated/populated on a Contract Record for an IC Physician. This is not applicable to this Clinician type.

1. Log in as Matt Hebert

2. Navigate to Contract Record 00011367

3. Review fields: EE NP/PA Hourly Blended, EE NP/PA Full Time Weekly Blended, EE NP/PA Full Time Overage Rate

Would not expect these fields to be populated for this Clinician type

Record Links: [https://corehc-uat2.sandbox.lightning.force.com/lightning/r/Contract/800bf00000KHns3AAD/view?ws=%2Flightning%2Fr%2FAccount%2F001bf00000QJ19iAAD%2Fview|https://corehc-uat2.sandbox.lightning.force.com/lightning/r/Contract/800bf00000KHns3AAD/view?ws=%2Flightning%2Fr%2FAccount%2F001bf00000QJ19iAAD%2Fview]

## Solution

Restrict the flow built as part of ([https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505582785|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505582785|smart-link] ) that updates below fields restricted only to **Code Set Sub Type**=Employee AND **Shift Type**: NP/PA 

- EE NP/PA Full Time Weekly Blended

*** Hourly Rate averaged across all HPFs ** 40

- EE NP/PA Hourly Blended

** Hourly Rate averaged across all HPFs

- EE NP/PA Full Time Overage Rate

** Hourly Rate averaged across all HPFs + $2

- EE NP/PA Full Time Monthly Blended

*** EE NP/PA Hourly Blended ** NP/PA Tier

## Root Cause

As per the US 2785, we only preventing  Full time EE NP/PA to add hour commitement rate on contract request flow. at the time of US2785, [~accountid:61c036c29ee70a0068954daf] & team decided to allow other emp type clinician to add details.
