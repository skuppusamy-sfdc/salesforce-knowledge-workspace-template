"The travel policy details are not directly available. However, if you have specific questions or need further clarification, feel free to ask!”

1. Agentforce chat window opens with an introduction from the Agent

2. Agent prompts to verify the Clinician Name and Global ID

3. Agent cannot assist without the information in test case step

Type of Questions the Agent can handle in regards to Pay & Compensation:

1. providing agreement or contract copy

1. breakdown of compensation model

1. pay components

1. where to update direct deposit information

1. travel or expense policy

Should produce the policies or FAQs for both travel and expenses.

## Root Cause

The defect in the **Agentforce chat functionality** was caused by a **logic error** in the process designed to handle pay and compensation inquiries.

** **Logic Failure:** The system's logic for fetching policies (such as travel or expense) was structured to fail silently when it did not possess necessary **prerequisite clinician information* (like name and Global ID). Instead of guiding the agent to input this required data or providing a generic policy, the system incorrectly failed to produce any policy output.

** **Resolution:** The fix involved **updating the underlying prompt/logic* to ensure the chat bot properly handles scenarios where specific clinician details are required, thereby allowing the system to successfully retrieve and present the requested policies.
