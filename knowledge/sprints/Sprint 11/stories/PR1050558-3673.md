## Root Cause

The reported visibility issue regarding VPO approval on the Contract Record was determined to be a result of a **misunderstanding of the defined approval assignment logic**, not a technical defect.

** **Misconception:* The bug reporter expected the approval notification or work item to be visible to an individual who was not the designated approver (Maci Broussard).

** **Intended Logic Confirmed:** The Salesforce team confirmed the system is operating as designed: when multiple team members are assigned the **VP Operations role**, the approval is intentionally routed to the **most recently added member*. Furthermore, when multiple service accounts are involved, the approval is sent only to the VPO relevant to the facility in question.

Since the system's behavior was confirmed to align with the current design specifications, the reported issue was **not reproducible as a bug**.
