## Description

As a Legal team member, I want the system to automatically create Facility Offboarding task(s) related to each child case for each Service Account based on the service line, so that relevant tasks are generated for tracking offboarding activities.

## Acceptance Criteria

AC1: Child Facility Offboarding Tasks

Given I am a Legal Team member within the Corporate Support apps

When I complete the “Begin Facility Termination” guided experience [Story [+PR1050558317+|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558317]]

And a Parent Facility Offboarding Case has been created per selected Service Account(s) [Story [+PR10505581683+|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505581683]]

And child cases for each Parent Facility Offboarding Case have been created [Story [+PR10505581683+|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR10505581683]]

Then the system shall **automatically create task(s) related to each Child Case** based on [Document = Client Cases & tasks, tab = Offboarding|https://schumachergroup.sharepoint.com/:x:/r/teams/UnifiedPlatform-ReGenesis/Shared%20Documents/Program%20Documentation/Program%20Execution/Client%20%26%20Clinician%20Experience/Client%20Cases%20%26%20Tasks.xlsx?d=w7f350716ce184e1eb38574b02403912d&csf=1&web=1&e=5LFbsm]:

- Criteria (column G) 

** AND 

- Service Account’s Service Line “Parent Service Line” (column H)

And Tasks shall be populated with the following information:

- Record Type = Default

- Subject = <column D>

- Related To = <column E>

- Assigned To = <column F>

- Status = Open

- Priority = Normal

And if there are **NO Account Team role member**, then assign to the Child Case Owner, so they can manually reassign the task as needed.

And if there are more than one **Account Team role member**, then assign to the **most recently added** member on the Service Account.

AC2: Auto Close Child Case - Closed /Canceled 

Given I am a Legal Team member in the Corporate Support apps

**When there are NO Task records related to a Client Offboarding Child Case**

And the child Case record does not already have Status = Closed

Then the child Case is automatically updated to:

- Status = Closed

- Closed Reason = Canceled
