## Root Cause

The approval process logic is dependent on the presence of the **VPO (Virtual Product Owner)** association. The initial setup did not ensure that the VPO was associated with the necessary records, which is a mandatory element for approval routing. Furthermore, the essential **Tactical and CEM queues are missing required members**, preventing approval assignments from being made correctly.
