## Description

As a Clinician, I need to access the health cloud portal, so that I can complete activities and view information throughout my time at SCP

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a Clinician

When I am provisioned access to the HC portal

Then I am automatically granted the following license: 

- If one or more Healthcare Practitioner Facility records where Status = Active: Customer Community Plus 

- If above criteria not met: Customer Community Login

Given I am a Clinician

When one or more of my HPF records are updated so that Status = Active

AND I have a Customer Community Login for the HC portal

Then my license is automatically updated to Customer Community Plus

## Solution

Check when the first HPF status = Active,  (first clinical shift date is populated) , the license should be updated from Customer community to customer community plus
