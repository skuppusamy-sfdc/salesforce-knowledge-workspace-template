## Description

As a Medical Staff Liaison - HCP, I need to Risk Review cases to be approved by the necessary people, so that I can verify a clinician’s fit with their potential facility based on their credentials, prior experience, and other factors

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a Corporate Support user in the Corporate Support or Corporate Support Console app

When I navigate to the Approval Work Items tab 

Then I am able to view the My Outstanding Approvals list view (visible to all users) **– Workaround: Use the Approval Work Items tab >** **Use ‘Assigned Approval Work Items’ list view**

And I can see the following

- Columns: Approval Work Item Name, Related Record, Related Record Object Name, Created Date

- Filtered on Status = Assigned, Assigned To = myself

Given I am a SVP Litigation, Regional Medical Officer, or Compliance user

When I navigate to an Approval Request record

Then I am able to **Approve** or **Reject** the Risk Review (Work Guide located in right column)

And the following happens:

- Automated updates

** Email notification with final decision - approve or reject with comments sent to 

*** Risk Review Submitter

*** All Approvers

**** Clinician Experience Specialist, see from Account Team on the facility *** only for Reject status

** Closed Date is populated with when the final approval is made

** Status is populated to Closed.

** Closed Reason is set to “Approved” (if Approve) or “Denied” (if Reject)

- I am able to add comments to my approval or rejection decision

Given I am a Medical Staff Liaison

When I navigate to a case with Record Type = Risk Review and Status = Closed

Then I am able to see the Approval History (via Approval Trace component/Approval Work Item) in the Approval tab [tab order: Detail (default), Response, Files, Approval] **Workaround: Use the Approval Work Items tab > ‘Assigned Approval Work Items’ list view**

## Solution

Risk Approval process will be built using Flow Approval process (not Classic approval process), so approval list and history will be shown using enhanced components.

As Approver - following are the options to view list of items needing approval

1. Todo list in utility bar-

1. Approval Work items tab

1. Work item component embedded on page

Approval History is available via Approval Trace component which can be embedded on lightning record page. 

Need to update page template to have the main and a right, Work Guide place in the right column.  Add Approval Trace/ Approval Work Item to the Approval tab.
