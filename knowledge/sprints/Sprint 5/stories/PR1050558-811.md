## Description

As a Recruiter, I need prospective clinicians to be automatically provisioned access to the portal, so that they can have access to complete tasks, update their information, and access helpful resources

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a Recruiter in the Corporate Support or Corporate Support Console app

When I update the **ATS Stage** field on an **ATS Applicant** record to **Screening Call Complete - Successful**

And the clinician has not yet been provisioned HC Portal access

Then the clinician is **automatically provisioned HC Portal access**

- If the Email field is not populated, the portal access is not provisioned

Given I am a **Recruiter**, **Clinician Experience Team Member**, **Data Quality team member** in the Corporate Support or Corporate Support Console app

When I open a clinician record where they have not been provisioned portal access

Then I am able to select the “Enable Customer User” button and manually initiate their portal access

_this is standard functionality, no build needed_
