## Description

**Steps to reproduce:**

1. Login to Core HC org as Recruiter

1. Open a clinician record with Contract having ‘Draft’ status

1. Click ‘Contract’ Request button

1. Click ‘Continue with Draft Request’ button

1. Verify the list of HPF records displayed

1. Click ‘Previous’ button

1. Click 'Discard and Start New Request' button

1. Verify the list of HPF records displayed

**Expected Result:**

Healthcare Practitioner Facility records for that clinician where Status = Prospect, Candidate, Waiting to be Scheduled, Active, WTBS Here-WRKG at Sister Site should be displayed

**Actual Result:**

1. After clicking ‘Continue with Draft Request’ button, HPF records are displayed with an additional blank row which is checked by default

1. After clicking 'Discard and Start New Request' button, duplicate HPF records are seen

Syntax issue
