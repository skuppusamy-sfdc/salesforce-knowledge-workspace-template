## Description

On the Risk Review Confirmation screen, the **"Attach File"** option is incorrectly displayed when the user selects **"No"** in response to the Risk Review prompt. 

According to the expected behavior, When user clicks on **“No”** Flow should end & use redirect to Clinician record.

**Steps to Reproduce:**

1. Log in as a Medical Staff Liaison or Data Quality team member.

1. Navigate to a clinician record.

1. Click the **Risk Review** button.

1. On the Risk Review Confirmation screen, select **"No"**.

1. Observe that the **Attach File** option is still visible.

**Expected Behavior:**

The **Attach File** option should only appear when the user selects **"Yes"** & it should be on the last screen.
