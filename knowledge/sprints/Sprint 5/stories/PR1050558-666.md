## Description

As a Recruiter, I need a guided experience to create HPF records in Salesforce, so that I can quickly update prospect data and have prompts for items to cover on the call.

_Feedback from 6/17 Demo of Story_ [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558195|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558195|smart-link] _to enhance/update existing guided flow. We made updates to the guided Clinician Creation, but the Create HPF also contains the SAME fields, so updating for consistency._

## Acceptance Criteria

AC1: Update button name “Create HPF & Job Association”

Given that I am a recruiter on the Corporate Support apps

When I open a clinician record 

Then the button name = “Create HPF & Job Association” should be renamed:

**From**: “Create HPF & Job Association” 

**To**:      “Create HPF” 

AC2: Update “Board Certified?” field on the guided “Create HPF” experience

Given that I am a recruiter on the Corporate Support apps

When I open a clinician record and click the “Create HPF” button

And the “Board Certified?” field **name** shall be changed:

**From**: “Board Certified?”

**To**:      “Board Certification Status”

And the “Board Certification Status” field **values** shall be changed:

**From**: 

- Yes

- No

**To**:

- Certified

- Eligible

- Ineligible

And the Clinician should have ONLY one Board Certification record with the Status matching the selected “Board Certification Status” value. Do not create duplicate records. If nothing is selected, do NOT do anything; no record created. 

AC3: Update “Medical License State” field on guided “Create HPF” experience

Given that I am a recruiter on the Corporate Support apps

When I open a clinician record and click the “Create HPF” button

Then “Medical License” field shall be displayed as a multi-select picklist (similar to the standard SF field)

And upon completing the process the State field on the Business License Record shall be populated (One Business License Record per selected Medical License state)

## Solution

-  “Medical License”  to display/behave like the standard SF multiselect picklist. Use the LWC built for the MultiSelect (Pick List based LWC)

** same solution as [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558385|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558385|smart-link] 

** Ensure field is being populated:

*** Medical License - the state field is being populated on the Business License Record

- Board Certification Status - do not create duplicate records. if nothing is selected, do NOT do anything; no record created. A Clinician should ONLY have 1 Board Certification record.  Example: if the Clinician already has one record in “Eligible” status and the user goes through this guided process and selects “Ineligible” the the exiting record will be updated from “Eligible” to “Ineligible”.

** Certified

** Eligible

** Ineligible
