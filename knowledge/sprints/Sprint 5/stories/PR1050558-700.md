## Description

As a Business Development Associate, from a Service Account I need to be able to view related Corporate Entities and vice versa, so that I can see the relationship between Service Accounts and Corporate Entities.

## Acceptance Criteria

Given that I am a Legal team member in the Corporate Support or Corporate Support Console app

When I open an Account record where the Record Type = **Service Account** and click New in the Corporate Entities related list OR

When I open an Account record where the Record Type = **Corporate Entity** and click New in the Service Accounts related list

Then I am able to select between the following Party Role Relationships:

** **Services Contracting Entity*

** **Billing Entity*

** **EE Physician Contracting Entity*

** **EE NP/PA Contracting Entity*

** **EE RN/LPN Contracting Entity*

And I can specify **Start Date** (required) and **End Date**

Given that I am a Business Development Associate in the Corporate Support or Corporate Support Console app

When I open an Account record where the Record Type = **Service Account**

Then I am able to see:

- Related Tab

** Account Plans (pcrush object), Opportunities, Contracts, Contacts, Corporate Entities, Account History

*** Corporate Entities related list (Account Account Relationship record between the Service Account and Corporate Entity Accounts)

***** Columns: Account Relationship Id, Account, **Party Role Relationship**, **Start Date**, **End Date*

NOTE: other existing related lists created in [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR105055886|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR105055886|smart-link]

NOTE: makes updates to existing columns in [+PR1050558340+|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558340]

Given that I am a Business Development Associate in the Corporate Support or Corporate Support Console app

When I open an Account record where the Record Type = **Corporate Entity**

Then I am able to see:

- Related Tab

** Contacts, Contracts, Service Accounts, Account History, and Files related lists

*** Service Accounts related list (Account Account Relationship record between the Corporate Entity Account and Service Accounts)

**** Columns: Account Relationship Id, Related Account, **Party Role Relationship**, **Start Date**, **End Date*

NOTE: other existing related lists created in [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR105055886|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR105055886|smart-link]

update columns on Corporate Entity and Service Account related lists
