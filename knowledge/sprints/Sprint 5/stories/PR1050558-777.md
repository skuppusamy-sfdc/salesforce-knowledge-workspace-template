## Description

As a SFO (Staffing Fulfillment Optimization) user, I want the ability to generate facility rates by designating the type of rate and specified rate for each Type & Facility Title combinations, so that we have clearly defined rates for each Service Account.

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a SFO in the Corporate Support or Corporate Support Console app

When I navigate to a Contract record (record type = Client) where Status = New

And I am on the Summary tab (story [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558715|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558715|smart-link] )

Then I **can see the Specify Rates button**

Given I am a SFO in the Corporate Support or Corporate Support Console app

When I navigate to a Contract record (record type = Client) where Status = Submitted, In Progress, Executed, Abandoned

And I am on the Summary tab (story [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558715|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558715|smart-link] )

Then I **cannot see the Specify Rates button**

Given I am a SFO in the Corporate Support or Corporate Support Console app

When I navigate to a Contract record (record type = Client)

And I am on the Summary tab (story [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558715|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558715|smart-link] )

And I click **Specify Rates** button

Then …

- I am able to select more than one Unit of Measure for a combination of Rate Type and Facility Title

** Example: Rate Type = Shift, Shift = Day, Facility Title = EE Midlevel Provider; then I am able to create rates for both “per Month” and “per Hour”  within the same contract
