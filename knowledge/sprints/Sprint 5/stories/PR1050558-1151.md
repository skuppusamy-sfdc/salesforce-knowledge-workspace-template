## Description

Steps to Reproduce:

1. Log into Corp HC org as SFO - Richard Caramel

1. Navigate to Corporate Support Console app and open a client contract record in New status

1. Navigate to Summary tab and click ‘Specify Rates’ button

1. Select a value for Type of Rate and click ‘Next’ button

1. Complete the mandatory fields and click ‘Finish’ button

1. Navigate to Summary tab and click ‘Specify Rates’ button again

**Expected Result:**

1. After step 3, when the flow is launched the sub tab should be named properly

1. After step 5, the Omniscript sub tab should be closed the flow is completed

1. After step 6. the flow screen should load properly and user has to manually refresh to see the Type of Rate field

**Actual Result:**

1. After step 3, when the flow is launched the sub tab is named as Omniscript

1. After step 5, the Omniscript sub tab is still open even though the flow is completed

1. After step 6. the flow screen doesn’t load properly and user has to manually refresh to see the Type of Rate field

## Root Cause

Console tab configuration was not present and for navigation 'replace' was not checked on native action on OS.

## Root Cause Type

Deployment and Configuration: Environment Mismatch
