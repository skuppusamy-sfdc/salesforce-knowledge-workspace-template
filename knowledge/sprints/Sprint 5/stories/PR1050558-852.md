## Description

As a Clinician, I need to complete an intake form, so that I can provide necessary information to move forward in the recruiting process and it is retained for downstream onboarding processes

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am Recruiter in the Corporate Support or Corporate Support Console app

When I update the Declined Reason field on an ATS Applicant record to **Candidate Declined or SCP Declined**

Then the following are updated on the Case record (RT = Clinician Intake Form) 

- Status = Closed 

- Close Reason = Canceled

Given I am Recruiter in the Corporate Support or Corporate Support Console app

When I initiate an intake form send to a clinician

And the status is in Not Started or In Progress after **60** (Weekly Scheduled)

Then the following are updated on the Case record (RT = Clinician Intake Form) 

- Status = Closed 

- Close Reason = Expired

Given I am a Recruiter in the Corporate Support or Corporate Support Console app

When an intake form has been sent to a clinician

Then I have the ability to **recall** the intake form on the Case record

And Then the following are updated on the Case record (RT = Clinician Intake Form)  

- Status = Closed 

- Close Reason = Canceled

- Date/Time Closed = Data when the case is Closed

## Solution

When user update the Declined Reason field on an ATS Applicant record to **Applicant Declined or SCP Declined**

Use a same Record Triggered Flow for Update on Object ATS Applicant built for [https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558615|https://salesforce.atlassian.net/browse/PR1050558615|smart-link],  Field = Declined Reason, Value = Applicant Declined or SCP Declined

** Search for Case Record with RT = **Clinician Intake Form* with criteria

** **Status = Not Started or In Progress*

** When Found Stop

*** Update the Case Record

**** Status = Closed

**** Close Reason = Canceled

** When Not Found

*** End the Flow

----

When I initiate an intake form send to a clinician

And the status is in New or In Progress after **60 to 67 days** (Weekly Scheduled)

Use a scheduled-triggered flow that run every week on Saturday, 11 PM

** Search for Case Record with RT = **Clinician Intake Form* with criteria

** **Status = Not Started, In Progress** and **Date/Time Opened >= Today - 60 Days* 

** When Found Stop

*** Update the Case Record

**** Status = Closed

**** Close Reason = Canceled

**** Date/Time Closed = Today

*** When user say No

**** Do Nothing
