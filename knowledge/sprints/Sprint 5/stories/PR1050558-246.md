## Description

As a Medical Staff Liaison - HCP, I need to Risk Review cases to be approved by the necessary people, so that I can verify a clinician’s fit with their potential facility based on their credentials, prior experience, and other factors

## Acceptance Criteria

Given I am a Medical Staff Liaison or Data Quality team member in the Corporate Support apps

When I navigate to a clinician record

And I can click on the Risk Review button (create a Case record with record type = Risk Review and Status = New)

Then it is automatically submitted for approval

And the following happens:

- Automated updates

** Status updated to In Review

** Approval request is sent to the following approvers

*** SVP Litigation Queue

*** Regional Medical Officer - account team role, reference the Account Name lookup on the Case (this is the Service Account)

*** Compliance Queue - (add Aimee Bertrand to queue)

***** Route to Compliance Queue only if Yes was answered for **either* of the following questions: 

***** Conflict of Interest form contains an answer in the affirmative (route to Compliance) 

***** Any suspensions or exclusions from Insurance Plans/Third Party Payors (route to Compliance) 

**** **Approval request are sent to all necessary approvers at the same time and ALL must approve*

- I am no longer able to update attributes on the Risk Review case record

- I cannot select “Recall” on the Risk Review Assessment record to cancel the approval request

NOTE: Assumption is that Service Account will have a user assigned to the Account Team Role = Regional Medical Officer.
